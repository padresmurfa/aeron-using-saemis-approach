# Narrative Team Report

NARRATIVE TEAM REPORT
========================
Story Scope: Quark Era seed material, including confinement, interiority, proto-memory, and the Fourth Order of Beings
Draft Status: Accepted narrative and canon pass written into saga, cosmology, timeline, and primal-language surfaces
Editor Findings:
- Mythopedia: The era fits current canon only after Electroweak directional interaction and before later matter-forming or light-bearing eras.
- Timeline: Confinement must follow interactional asymmetry, because interiority depends on already-differentiated influences being driven inward under enclosure.
- Creator: The seed's references to foundational alignment with `C` were normalized to `Loran`, and interiority was constrained so it never implies separation from the Creator's rhythm.
- Narrative: The Fourth Order can introduce proto-self structure, stored identity, and proto-memory, but it must not imply already-formed ego, self-awareness, or soul-bearing ethical fracture.
Primal Language:
- Cataloged seven Fourth Order concept files: `Kethor` (Corewardens), `Mirath` (Pressure Hosts), `Tavrek` (Sparkbearers), `Oraeth` (Veilbound), `Athorin` (Identity Seeds), `Selkor` (Cohesion Clusters), and `Phaemor` (Tension Archivists).
- Reused prior root families to anchor the new lexicon: `Kethren` for enclosed cores, `Miravel` for pressured transformation, `Tavhor` for condensed contrast, `Orael` for bound threshold flow, `Athren` for preserved uniqueness, `Selketh` for internal collectivity, and `Phaelor` for retained sequence.
- English descriptors are retained only as gloss and explanation, not as canonical root names.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- A later era will need to decide when stored identity and proto-memory become actual self-awareness rather than merely structural precondition.
- If later canon treats particle-like persistence, early matter assembly, or first light-bearing structures, place those developments downstream of the Quark Era rather than compressing them into confinement alone.
