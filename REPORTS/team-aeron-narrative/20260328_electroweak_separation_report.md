# Narrative Team Report

NARRATIVE TEAM REPORT
========================
Story Scope: Electroweak Separation Era seed material, including interactional differentiation, preference potential, and the Third Order of Beings
Draft Status: Accepted narrative and canon pass written into saga, cosmology, timeline, and primal-language surfaces
Editor Findings:
- Mythopedia: The seed fits current canon if treated as downstream of the Inflationary Era and upstream of later material-light and matter-forming milestones.
- Timeline: Electroweak Separation is the first era in which directional influence and interactional asymmetry become explicit. It must follow domain separation, because preference potential depends on already-localized structures.
- Creator: Preference is acceptable so long as it remains derivative, aligned with `Loran`, and explicitly short of self-owning will.
- Narrative: The era can introduce proto-agency, but it must not imply moral rebellion, autonomous refusal, or already-formed personhood.
Primal Language:
- Cataloged six Third Order concept files: `Sevarn` (Binders), `Miravel` (Transmuters), `Tavhor` (Chargebearers), `Orael` (Veilwalkers), `Selketh` (Cohesion Hosts), and `Athren` (Divergence Keepers).
- Reused prior root families to anchor the new lexicon: `Selem` / `Seren` for binding coherence, `Dara` / `Athal` for lawful transformation, `Tavir` for carried polarity, `Phaes` for threshold transition, `Kethren` / `Seralon` for hosted cohesion, and `Athal` for protected asymmetry.
- English descriptors are retained only as gloss and explanation, not as canonical root names.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- Keep later portrayals of proto-will explicitly pre-moral until a later canon event establishes self-ownership and true refusal.
- If later canon addresses particle-like identity, material light, or early matter stabilization, place those in downstream eras rather than overloading Electroweak Separation.
