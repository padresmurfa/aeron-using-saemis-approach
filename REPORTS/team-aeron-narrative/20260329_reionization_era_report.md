# Narrative Team Report

NARRATIVE TEAM REPORT
=====================
Story Scope: Era of Reionization seed material, including first stellar ignition, created radiance, luminous stewardship, and the Tenth Order of Beings
Draft Status: Accepted narrative and canon pass written into saga, cosmology, timeline, and primal-language surfaces
Editor Findings:
- Mythopedia: `Daravelon` follows `Darathen` correctly and treats ignition as outward-borne created radiance rather than a return to ambient field-light.
- Timeline: Stars, luminous stewardship, creative shaping, and large-scale radiance appear after existential solitude and before worlds, embodied history, soul-bearing ethics, or later inter-creature conflict.
- Creator: The package allows immense created influence without implying any capacity to stand outside `Aru` or `Loran`, and it keeps even enclosed radiance inside reality's fabric.
- Narrative: The saga makes ignition feel like a new burden of office rather than a stock rise-of-rulers myth, and it avoids imported rupture-myth pressure despite the seed material's push in that direction.
Primal Language:
- Canonized `Daravelon` as the Reionization-era headword and nine Tenth Order roots: `Taviloron`, `Vathoral`, `Selkoron`, `Athralon`, `Kethavel`, `Atheror`, `Phaemoral`, `Serathor`, and `Oraethal`.
- Kept the new forms inside live families while letting solitary descendants reopen into more outward-radiant and office-bearing surfaces.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- A later era will need to decide when stars gather into worlds and how created luminous offices begin to support larger historical structures.
- If later canon treats souls, embodied mortal life, ethical dualism, or opposition to first principles as already present here, push those developments well downstream of `Daravelon`.
