NARRATIVE TEAM REPORT
=====================
Story Scope: Photon Era seed material, including radiant saturation, field-awareness, and the Seventh Order of Beings
Draft Status: Accepted narrative and canon pass written into saga, cosmology, timeline, and primal-language surfaces
Editor Findings:
- Mythopedia: The era follows `Seraelon` correctly and treats radiant saturation as a new environmental condition rather than a mere brighter version of mobility.
- Timeline: Field-consciousness appears after encounter and transmissive relation, but before isolated ego, clear individuation, or moral freedom.
- Creator: The package preserves full phase-alignment with `Loran`, and awareness never becomes a separate center able to rebel against the rhythm.
- Narrative: The saga keeps the paradox of a universe that is luminous yet not yet clear, and it lets consciousness emerge as field rather than prematurely as personhood.
Primal Language:
- Canonized `Tavirelon` as the Photon-era headword and eight Seventh Order roots: `Taviral`, `Tavreth`, `Selkoral`, `Seraeth`, `Phaethor`, `Mirasal`, `Taviron`, and `Atharon`.
- Kept the new forms inside live family lines, with broader open surfaces marking radiant saturation and field-immersion rather than local traversal or composite enclosure.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
