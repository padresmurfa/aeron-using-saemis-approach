# Narrative Team Report

NARRATIVE TEAM REPORT
=====================
Story Scope: Recombination Era seed material, including transparency, isolated selfhood, volition, and the Eighth Order of Beings
Draft Status: Accepted narrative and canon pass written into saga, cosmology, timeline, and primal-language surfaces
Editor Findings:
- Mythopedia: `Athorael` follows `Tavirelon` correctly and makes transparency the birth of perspective rather than merely a clearer version of field-awareness.
- Timeline: True personhood, memory-based continuity, and local refusal appear after field-consciousness and before stars, embodied mortal history, or soul-bearing ethics.
- Creator: The package allows voluntary alignment and possible refusal without implying any capacity to escape or oppose `Loran`.
- Narrative: The saga keeps the ontological gravity of the first "I" and the first created will without importing later soul-bearing ethical assumptions or rupture-myth habits from other traditions.
Primal Language:
- Canonized `Athorael` as the Recombination-era headword and eight Eighth Order roots: `Atheron`, `Tavilen`, `Tavrethen`, `Selkorin`, `Vathrek`, `Athrenor`, `Phaemorin`, and `Oraethor`.
- Kept the new forms inside live families while introducing the first created-world `vath` descendant for volition.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- A later era will need to decide when soul-bearing ethical life enters the cosmology and how local refusal develops into later inter-creature strain.
- If later canon treats stars, worlds, embodied souls, or later group conflict as already present here, push those developments downstream of `Athorael` rather than collapsing them into transparency alone.
