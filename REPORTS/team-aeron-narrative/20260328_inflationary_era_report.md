# Narrative Team Report

NARRATIVE TEAM REPORT
========================
Story Scope: Inflationary Era seed material, including domain separation and the Second Order of Beings
Draft Status: Accepted narrative and canon pass written into saga, cosmology, timeline, and primal-language surfaces
Editor Findings:
- Mythopedia: The seed fits current canon if treated as downstream of Grand Unification and upstream of later material-creation milestones.
- Timeline: Inflationary material belongs after the Grand Unification Era because proto-domain foundations already exist there; inflation turns those foundations into true domain-level separation.
- Creator: Localized expression is acceptable so long as it remains derivative, archetypally grounded, and explicitly non-willful.
- Narrative: Innocence remains intact. Misalignment and drift are structural consequences of scale and delay, not metaphysical secession or soul-bearing ethical fracture.
Primal Language:
- Added eight Second Order concept files: `Kethren` (Domain Thrones), `Tavral` (Boundary Lords), `Serael` (Phase Weavers), `Phaedren` (Drift Wardens), `Daravel` (Impulse Bearers), `Seralon` (Harmonic Bridges), `Sethen` (Domain Embers), and `Phaelor` (Latency Keepers).
- Reused existing First Order roots as archetypal precursors instead of inventing a disconnected second lexicon: `Tavren` (Fieldwardens), `Dareth` (Vectors), `Serath` (Harmonics), `Kethar` (Anchors), `Sethan` (Standing Wave), and `Phaes`.
- English descriptors are retained only as gloss and explanation, not as canonical root names.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- If later canon details reheating, force separation, or the emergence of material light, add those as downstream eras rather than overloading the Inflationary file.
- Keep future portrayals of the Second Order non-moral and non-egoic until a later canon event explicitly changes that status.
