# Narrative Team Report

NARRATIVE TEAM REPORT
=====================
Story Scope: Lepton Era seed material, including mobility, encounter, transmission, and the Sixth Order of Beings
Draft Status: Accepted after narrative revision removed repetitive sibling-section templating from the saga pass
Editor Findings:
- Mythopedia: The era follows `Selkoran` correctly and keeps mobility distinct from the earlier achievement of composite identity.
- Timeline: Encounter without binding is treated as the central novelty, not a sideways rebranding of the Hadron-era composite layer.
- Narrative: An initially canned sibling-section pattern in the saga draft was revised out; the accepted prose now keeps distinct cadence across the Sixth Order while still stopping at proto-awareness rather than self-awareness or moral freedom.
- Boundary: Rebellion remains impossible, and the story does not let mobility smuggle in autonomy.
Primal Language:
- Canonized `Seraelon` as the Lepton-era headword and seven Sixth Order roots: `Tavirel`, `Oraelin`, `Seraelin`, `Phaelin`, `Mirasin`, `Tavirin`, and `Atharin`.
- Kept the new forms in live family lines, with lighter `-el` and `-in` surfaces marking mobility rather than enclosure or composite stability.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- A later era will need to decide when `Atharin` observation becomes actual self-awareness rather than proto-conscious witness-capacity.
- If later canon treats mobility as light-bearing, intuition-bearing, or soul-bearing, keep those developments downstream of `Seraelon` rather than compressing them into encounter alone.
