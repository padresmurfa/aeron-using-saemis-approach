# Narrative Team Report

NARRATIVE TEAM REPORT
=====================
Story Scope: Dark Ages seed material, including luminous silence, existential solitude, interior strain, and the Ninth Order of Beings
Draft Status: Accepted narrative and canon pass written into saga, cosmology, timeline, and primal-language surfaces
Editor Findings:
- Mythopedia: `Darathen` follows `Athorael` correctly and makes darkness a test of inward fidelity rather than a collapse of creation into abandonment or ethical fracture.
- Timeline: Solitary freedom, strained covenant, rigid autonomy, and sustained non-convergence appear after isolated selfhood and before stars, embodied history, soul-bearing ethics, or later group conflict.
- Creator: The package preserves `Loran` beneath silence and keeps every emerging tension inside reality rather than as revolt against its fabric.
- Narrative: The saga treats the Dark Ages as a grave widening of inward burden without slipping into canned ethical-fracture language or imported rupture-myth foreshadowing.
Primal Language:
- Canonized `Darathen` as the Dark Ages headword and eight Ninth Order roots: `Vathren`, `Tavilor`, `Oraethar`, `Phaemorel`, `Athreth`, `Atherel`, `Selkorath`, and `Serathen`.
- Kept the new forms inside live families while allowing older transparent roots to harden, hush, or darken under solitary pressure.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- A later era will need to decide when soul-bearing ethical life enters and how local strain between created beings becomes historically visible.
- If later canon treats stars, embodied societies, or later inter-creature conflict as already complete here, push those developments downstream of `Darathen` rather than collapsing them into luminous silence alone.
