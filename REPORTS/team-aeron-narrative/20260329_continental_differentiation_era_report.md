# Narrative Team Report

NARRATIVE TEAM REPORT
=====================
Story Scope: Continental Differentiation Era seed material, including articulated crust, continents, mountains, rifts, basins, stone memory, volcanic renewal, shielding, soil preparation, and land-scale climate rhythm
Draft Status: Accepted narrative and canon pass ready for cosmology, saga, timeline, and primal-language surfaces
Editor Findings:
- Mythopedia: `Athralen` follows `Kethralen` correctly and treats continental emergence as the birth of terrain identity rather than a jump to life, culture, rivers already carved, or awakened land-spirits.
- Timeline: Stable worlds now differentiate into enduring regions after world-binding and before any biosphere, creaturely habitation, or soul-bearing history.
- Creator: The package keeps all land-shaping offices inside `Aru` and `Loran`, with no drift toward sentient continents, national myth backfilled into geology, or moralized tectonic warfare.
- Narrative: The saga frames this age as the moment when place acquires character and avoids dry geology textbook prose, stock elemental pantheons, and premature fantasy-biome language.
- Celestials: Multi-moon stress coupling is clarified as timing modulation only; moons phase-shift release windows inside existing tectonic systems but do not replace `Kethralor`, `Kethaven`, or plate-driven continental formation.
Primal Language:
- Canonized `Athralen` as the Continental Differentiation-era headword and nine Thirteenth Order roots: `Kethralor`, `Mirathor`, `Athrenel`, `Miravelen`, `Phaemoralen`, `Tavreken`, `Tavraleth`, `Selkorel`, and `Seraelen`.
- Kept the new forms audibly descended from world-binding and memory-bearing lineages while shifting them toward region-bearing, terrain-forming, and deep-time environmental surfaces.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- A later era will need to decide when prepared land crosses into actual river systems, rooted plant life, atmosphere-biosphere exchange, and creature-bearing habitats.
- If later canon tries to retroject culture, kingdoms, or pilgrim myths into `Athralen`, reject that collapse and keep this age geological, environmental, and pre-biological.
