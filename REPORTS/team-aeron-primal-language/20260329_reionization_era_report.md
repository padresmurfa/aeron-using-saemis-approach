# Primal Language Team Report

PRIMAL LANGUAGE TEAM REPORT
===========================
Scope: Era of Reionization naming, tenth-order ignition roots, and dictionary/grammar follow-through
Roots:
- Canonized `Daravelon` as the Reionization-era headword for first stellar ignition, outward-borne created radiance, and luminous stewardship.
- Coined nine tenth-order roots: `Taviloron` (Starforged), `Vathoral` (Flamebearer Regents), `Selkoron` (Covenant Luminaries), `Athralon` (Divergent Suns), `Kethavel` (Forgers of Form), `Atheror` (Witness Sovereigns), `Phaemoral` (Memory Stars), `Serathor` (Veiled Radiants), and `Oraethal` (Enclosed Radiants).
- Kept the new forms inside live families while letting dark-age descendants reopen into more outward, radiative, office-bearing, or form-casting surfaces.
Grammar:
- Extended the root-family ledger with new `dar`, `tav`, `vath`, `sel`, `ath`, `keth`, `phae`, `ser`, and `ora` descendants.
- Recorded the observed `Daravelon` tendency toward more projective endings that mark created radiance cast beyond the self, luminous office, selective disclosure, and structure-making fire.
Dictionary:
- Added bidirectional entries for `Daravelon` and all nine tenth-order headwords.
- Propagated the accepted roots back into saga, mythopedia, timeline, and report surfaces so English remains gloss rather than canon headword.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- Later canon should decide when `Taviloron` centers become tied to worlds and how `Vathoral`, `Kethavel`, and `Selkoron` begin to scaffold larger created polities.
- If a later era turns `Athralon` or `Oraethal` into soul-bearing antagonists or ethical dualists, reject that collapse and keep those later developments downstream of `Daravelon`.
