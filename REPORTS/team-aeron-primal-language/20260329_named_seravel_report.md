# Primal Language Team Report

PRIMAL LANGUAGE TEAM REPORT
===========================

Scope: assign canonical primal headwords to Aeron's seven moons so English labels become glosses rather than primary monikers

Accepted Headwords:
- `Serenel`: Silver Moon; the bright governing tide-moon of Aeron
- `Seramor`: Blood Moon; the deep red tide-moon of Aeron
- `Tavrekal`: Ash Moon; the cinder-forge moon of Aeron
- `Taviril`: Blue Moon; the far-sighted stormglass moon of Aeron
- `Kethorel`: Iron Moon; the oath- and war-metal moon of Aeron
- `Oraemor`: Pale Moon; the threshold and ruin-memory moon of Aeron
- `Serathil`: Veiled Moon; the masked passage-moon of Aeron

Naming Logic:
- `Serenel` and `Seramor` were paired in the `ser` line so the two primary tide-moons sound related and civilizationally central rather than like unrelated colors.
- `Tavrekal` and `Taviril` extend the `tav` family into forge-pressure and far-sky distance respectively.
- `Kethorel` anchors the iron moon in the `keth` pressure and burden family.
- `Oraemor` is a blended threshold-memory form to support the revised dream and ruin function of the Pale moon.
- `Serathil` extends the veiled `serath` line into a moon of masked passage and measured disclosure.

Integration Rule:
- Mythopedia and saga surfaces should introduce the primal headword first and retain the English moon label only as a gloss or secondary alias.

Verdict: Accept. Aeron's seven `Seravel` now have canonical primal monikers, and English color-name labels are demoted to secondary glosses.
