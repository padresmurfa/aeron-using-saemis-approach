# Primal Language Team Report

PRIMAL LANGUAGE TEAM REPORT
===========================
Scope: naming the Primal Language itself and the Dimensional Accretion Rule, then wiring both headwords through canon surfaces
Roots:
- Canonized `Darasel` as the high-abstraction headword for the Primal Language: the ordered totality of first meanings latent in `Aru`.
- Canonized `Ketharel` as the structural-principle headword for the Dimensional Accretion Rule: retained-layer growth by which later beings keep prior aspects while gaining new ones.
Grammar:
- Treated `Darasel` as a `dar`-led blend with `Selem`, so the language name stays tied to expression held as inward meaning rather than outward speech.
- Bound `Ketharel` to the `Kethar` lineage while explicitly retaining `Dareth` as its increase-bearing relation, because the rule is governed more by persistence than by law as such.
Dictionary:
- Added bidirectional entries for `Darasel` and `Ketharel`.
- Propagated both names into the README and canon prose surfaces where English-only phrasing had been left standing.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- If later canon develops derivatives of `Darasel`, keep them audibly tied either to `dar` or to a clearly justified blended branch rather than inventing a disconnected mini-family.
- Keep `Ketharel` reserved for retained-layer emergence; do not let it drift into a generic synonym for evolution, complexity, or progress.
