# Primal Language Team Report

PRIMAL LANGUAGE TEAM REPORT
===========================
Scope: Photon Era naming, seventh-order radiant roots, and dictionary/grammar follow-through
Roots:
- Canonized `Tavirelon` as the Photon-era headword for radiant saturation, shared perceptive medium, and field-like awareness.
- Coined eight seventh-order roots: `Taviral` (Luminary Seas), `Tavreth` (Veilfire Hosts), `Selkoral` (Chorus Fields), `Seraeth` (Phase Mirrors), `Phaethor` (Drift Flames), `Mirasal` (Echo Realms), `Taviron` (Entangled Constellations), and `Atharon` (Witness Fires).
- Kept the new forms inside live family lines rather than inventing an isolated vocabulary for radiance and consciousness.
Grammar:
- Extended the root-family ledger with the new `tav`, `sel`, `ser`, `phae`, `mir`, and `ath` descendants.
- Recorded the observed Photon-era tendency that radiant descendants often broaden into more open surfaces to mark ambient saturation, field-spread relation, and observer-capacity diffused through luminous immersion.
Dictionary:
- Added bidirectional entries for `Tavirelon` and all eight seventh-order headwords.
- Propagated the accepted roots back into saga, mythopedia, timeline, and report surfaces so English remains gloss rather than canon headword.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- Later canon should decide when `Atharon` observation becomes isolated selfhood rather than field-conscious witness-identity alone.
- If a later era turns `Taviral` or `Mirasal` into clear astral planes, transparent heavens, or ancestral domains with distinct selves, keep that development downstream of `Tavirelon` rather than collapsing it into first saturated awareness.
