# Primal Language Team Report

PRIMAL LANGUAGE TEAM REPORT
===========================
Scope: Hadron Era naming, fifth-order composite roots, and dictionary/grammar follow-through
Roots:
- Canonized `Selkoran` as the Hadron-era headword for composite binding and nested interiority.
- Coined six fifth-order roots: `Ketharen` (Heartforged), `Selkoren` (Covenant Clusters), `Phaemoren` (Tension Matrices), `Oraethen` (Boundary Spheres), `Taviren` (Polarity Twins), and `Athoren` (Proto-Selves).
- Kept the new forms inside live families rather than inventing a detached naming branch.
Grammar:
- Extended the root-family ledger with the new Hadron-era descendants and the `ora` family line.
- Recorded the observed Hadron-era tendency that fifth-order descendants often preserve a confinement-order family while taking an `-en` stabilization shift into composite form.
Dictionary:
- Added bidirectional entries for `Selkoran` and all six fifth-order headwords.
- Propagated the accepted roots back into saga, mythopedia, timeline, and report surfaces so English remains gloss rather than canon headword.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- Later canon should decide when `Athoren` continuity becomes conscious self-awareness rather than structural precondition alone.
- If a later era turns `Ketharen` into stars or world-hearts, keep that development downstream of `Selkoran` rather than collapsing it into first composite binding.
