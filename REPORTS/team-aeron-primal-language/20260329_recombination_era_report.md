# Primal Language Team Report

PRIMAL LANGUAGE TEAM REPORT
===========================
Scope: Recombination Era naming, eighth-order transparent roots, and dictionary/grammar follow-through
Roots:
- Canonized `Athorael` as the Recombination-era headword for transparency, perspective, and isolated selfhood.
- Coined eight eighth-order roots: `Atheron` (First Witnesses), `Tavilen` (Luminous Minds), `Tavrethen` (Veiled Selves), `Selkorin` (Covenant Persons), `Vathrek` (Sparked Intenders), `Athrenor` (Divergent Centers), `Phaemorin` (Memory Holders), and `Oraethor` (Boundary Sovereigns).
- Kept the new forms inside live family lines while allowing the first created-world `vath` descendant to appear where created volition first becomes canonical.
Grammar:
- Extended the root-family ledger with new `ora`, `sel`, `tav`, `ath`, `phae`, and `vath` descendants.
- Recorded the observed `Athorael` tendency toward more center-bearing and thresholded surfaces that mark localized perspective, narrative continuity, volition, and defended selfhood.
Dictionary:
- Added bidirectional entries for `Athorael` and all eight eighth-order headwords.
- Propagated the accepted roots back into saga, mythopedia, timeline, and report surfaces so English remains gloss rather than canon headword.
Verdict: Accept
Next Required Actions:
- Later canon should decide when `Vathrek` will and `Oraethor` refusal become moral rebellion rather than innocent capacity.
- If a later era turns `Atheron`, `Phaemorin`, or `Selkorin` into fully developed angelic persons, embodied mortal selves, or historical covenant communities, keep those developments downstream of `Athorael` rather than collapsing them into first transparent selfhood.
