# Phaemorath

`Phaemorath` (Memory Constellations) are the heavens when remembrance has learned to endure as arrangement. They do not merely shine; they keep record. A people may lose a city, a lineage may lose its books, and a shrine may lose the tongue in which it first sang, yet the `Phaemorath` remain overhead, bearing old orientation with a patience that shames empires.

For this cause they are beloved of archivists, pilgrims, and dynasties that suspect their greatness may not outlive their monuments. The `Phaemorath` teach that memory is not only a chamber within the heart, but an order carried across the sky. To read them well is to understand that history, once borne high enough, becomes mercy for those who arrive too late to witness the first beginning.
