# Taviril

`Taviril`, the Blue `Seravel` (moon), is the moon of distance made intimate. Its light is unnaturally clear, as though the world had been rinsed of dust for an hour and given back with sharper edges. Under `Taviril`, mountains seem nearer than they are, messages arrive with impossible swiftness, and those who were bred for departure feel the old ache of roads, currents, and high cold air stir in the chest again.

So `Taviril` has become beloved of navigators, sky-riders, couriers, and prophets. It does not merely show the far thing; it persuades the heart that the far thing can be reached. In times of peace that makes it the patron of travel, romance, and discovery. In times of strain it becomes the moon of dangerous longing, because there are distances which cannot be crossed without changing what one was before the journey began.
