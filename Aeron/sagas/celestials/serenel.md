# Serenel

`Serenel`, the Silver `Seravel` (moon), is the gracious face of the Water axis. It lays bright governance upon harbors, open tides, and those forms of order that do not need concealment in order to endure. Sailors trust `Serenel` because it tells the sea to show its hand. Magistrates bless it because it favors visible law. Lovers and pilgrims alike set forth beneath it when they would rather meet the road honestly than master it by cunning.

Yet `Serenel` is not weak because it is lucid. Its power lies in composure. It draws the world toward rhythm rather than frenzy, return rather than rupture. Wherever it reigns, calendars steady, ferries depart on time, and even grief seems more able to speak its name without shame. It is the moon by which a people remembers that law may yet be beautiful when it is not cruel.
