# Solaryth

`Solaryth` is no borrowed label. It is a primal word, and in that word the heavens declare their open favor. When `Solaryth` ascends, the world is not merely lit; it is affirmed. Field, wall, banner, harbor, and waking face are summoned into clearer being beneath it. Crops answer. Roads become legible. Oaths spoken in its strength are heard as though the day itself had taken witness.

Yet `Solaryth` is not gentle because it is bright. It is exacting. What grows beneath it must also endure disclosure. Rot cannot long pretend to be fruit, nor cowardice to be peace, when the great life-bearing `Taviran` (luminary) stands above the world in full and unsoftened sanction. Thus kings love it for coronations, farmers for harvest, healers for renewal, and liars fear it for the same reason.
