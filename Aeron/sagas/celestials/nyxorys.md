# Nyxorys

`Nyxorys` keeps the other half of the covenant of the sky. It does not shine to comfort. It shines to test. Its darklight does not erase the world, but strips it of flattering excess and forces each thing nearer to its limit. Under `Nyxorys`, doors are felt before they are seen, vows are weighed after the cheering is gone, and every boundary asks whether it is true or only painted.

For this reason the timid call it cursed, but the wise do not. `Nyxorys` is lawful. It is the counter-luminary without which revelation would become vanity and growth would swell into blindness. Hidden schools keep vigil under it. Abyss-walkers mark its hours. Those who traffic in taboo, masks, ordeal, and necessary renunciation know that there are truths which will not come in the generous blaze of `Solaryth`, but only in the stern and searching answer of `Nyxorys`.
