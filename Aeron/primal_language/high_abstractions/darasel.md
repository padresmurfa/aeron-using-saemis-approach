# Darasel

- Rendered form: `Darasel`
- Gloss: the Primal Language; the ordered totality of first meanings contained within being
- Tier: high abstraction

## Meaning

`Darasel` names the total expressive intelligibility by which truths contained within `Aru` may be rightly named without coming from outside `Aru`. It is not spoken sound before sound exists, but the inward order of first meanings later apprehended in creation as the Primal Language.

## Scope

`Darasel` carries:

- First names held within being
- Expression joined to self-knowing
- Total lexical potential prior to utterance
- Ordered intelligibility that does not stand outside the Creator

## Pre-Creation Use

In the pre-Big-Bang state, `Darasel` exists as perfectly contained order of meaning within `Aru`. It is real before creation, even though no created tongue, script, or listener yet exists.

## Boundaries

`Darasel` must not be confused with a created language, a later grammar imposed from outside, or an authority above `Aru`. It is contained within `Aru` and later apprehended by creation, not external to the Creator.

## Relations

- [Aru](aru.md) contains `Darasel` wholly; the Creator is the aggregate of all its words.
- [Dara](dara.md) gives `Darasel` its expressive lineage.
- [Selem](selem.md) gives `Darasel` its inward self-knowing lineage.

## Note On Form

`Darasel` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
