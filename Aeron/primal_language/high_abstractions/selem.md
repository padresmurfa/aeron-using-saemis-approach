# Selem

- Rendered form: `Selem`
- Gloss: immediate self-knowing; indivisible selfhood
- Tier: high abstraction

## Meaning

`Selem` names the Creator's perfect self-awareness when no distinction yet exists. It is self-knowledge without mirror, dialogue, polarity, or internal distance.

## Scope

`Selem` carries:

- Reflexive interiority
- Perfect identity
- Presence of the whole self to the whole self

## Pre-Creation Use

In the pre-Big-Bang state, `Selem` describes how the Creator knows Himself without becoming divided into knower and known as separate entities.

## Boundaries

`Selem` must not imply internal multiplicity, debate, or reflection across differentiated aspects.

## Relations

- [Aru](aru.md) is the absolute being in which `Selem` inheres.
- [Vatha](vatha.md) depends on `Selem`, because unified will presumes unified selfhood.

## Note On Form

`Selem` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
