# Vatha

- Rendered form: `Vatha`
- Gloss: unified will; unstrained intention held wholly within being
- Tier: high abstraction

## Meaning

`Vatha` names will before alternatives, contest, or exercised choice. It is the Creator's volitional power in perfect unity, unpressured by lack and unopposed by any second principle.

## Scope

`Vatha` carries:

- Will without dilemma
- Intention without tension
- Potential without external possibility space

## Pre-Creation Use

In the pre-Big-Bang state, `Vatha` describes the Creator's will as real, complete, and wholly unfractured, yet not yet expressed into differentiation.

## Boundaries

`Vatha` must not be described as compulsion, need, or reaction. It is sovereign will, not remedial impulse.

## Relations

- [Aru](aru.md) grounds `Vatha`.
- [Dara](dara.md) is the expressive capacity held within `Vatha`.
- [Orun](../threshold_events/orun.md) is the threshold at which will first moves into self-differentiating act.

## Note On Form

`Vatha` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
