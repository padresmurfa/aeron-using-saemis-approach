# Aru

- Rendered form: `Aru`
- Gloss: absolute being; undivided reality
- Tier: high abstraction

## Meaning

`Aru` names being before distinction. It does not mean "a thing that exists" among other things. At its highest and strictest use, it refers to the Creator as sole reality in the pre-Big-Bang state.

## Scope

`Aru` carries:

- Singularity without remainder
- Simplicity without composition
- Fullness without lack
- Reality without external contrast

## Pre-Creation Use

In the pre-Big-Bang state, `Aru` is the broadest valid primal root. It names the Creator's absolute being without implying parts, environment, or opposition.

## Boundaries

`Aru` must not be reduced to mere existence inside a universe. It names the ontological whole prior to all created categories.

## Relations

- [Selem](selem.md) expresses `Aru` as immediate self-knowing.
- [Vatha](vatha.md) expresses `Aru` as unified will.
- [Dara](dara.md) expresses `Aru` as latent expression.
- [Darasel](darasel.md) names the total ordered intelligibility contained within `Aru`'s being.

## Note On Form

`Aru` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root. It should not be understood as a spoken sound uttered before sound existed.
