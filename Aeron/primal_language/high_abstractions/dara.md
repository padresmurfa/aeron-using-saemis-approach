# Dara

- Rendered form: `Dara`
- Gloss: latent expression; generativity as capacity
- Tier: high abstraction

## Meaning

`Dara` names the Creator's capacity for expression before any projection, manifestation, or differentiation has occurred. It is generativity held in reserve without being absent.

## Scope

`Dara` carries:

- Expression as capacity only
- Manifestation not yet enacted
- Creative power without outward distinction

## Pre-Creation Use

In the pre-Big-Bang state, `Dara` describes the fact that the Creator can express, even though expression has not yet gone forth.

## Boundaries

`Dara` must not be confused with completed creation. It is potency prior to act.

## Relations

- [Vatha](vatha.md) gives `Dara` its volitional root.
- [Orun](../threshold_events/orun.md) is the first act in which `Dara` crosses from capacity toward manifest differentiation.

## Note On Form

`Dara` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
