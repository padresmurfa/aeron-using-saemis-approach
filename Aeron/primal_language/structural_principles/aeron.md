# Aeron

- Rendered form: `Aeron`
- Gloss: Aeron; the canonical proper name of the central world
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Aeron` names the stable terrestrial world at the center of the current mythic corpus: a layered, enduring place whose continuity makes geology, climate, celestial reading, and later life-history possible.

## Scope

`Aeron` carries:

- World-name
- Layered terrestrial continuity
- Geocentric cosmological reference
- Lawful gravity, sky, sea, heat, and rotation
- Stable place-bearing centrality

## Planetary Use

In current canon, `Aeron` is the proper primal headword for the world itself. It is the central reference frame for local cosmology, astrology, calendar doctrine, and the future histories that unfold upon it.

## Boundaries

`Aeron` must not be flattened into a generic word for every world, every planet, or every inhabited realm. It must also not be treated as a world-soul, an awakened planetary person, or a later English nickname imposed onto primal-language canon.

## Relations

- [Kethralen](../era_names/kethralen.md) is the era in which worlds such as `Aeron` are bound into durable layered places.
- [Athralen](../era_names/athralen.md) is the era in which `Aeron`'s surface differentiates into enduring land-bearing regions.
- [Solaryth](solaryth.md) and [Nyxorys](nyxorys.md) are the two ruling `Taviran` of `Aeron`'s sky.
- [Seravel](seravel.md) are the moons whose recurrent pressures answer the world-centered luminary frame of `Aeron`.

## Note On Form

`Aeron` is a preserved primal planetary headword. Its canonical form names the world directly rather than translating outward from a later English label.
