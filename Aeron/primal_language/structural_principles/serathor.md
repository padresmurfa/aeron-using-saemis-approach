# Serathor

- Rendered form: `Serathor`
- Gloss: Veiled Radiants; selective lights that disclose only what relation can rightly bear
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Serathor` names the tenth-order ignition intelligence that arises when `Serathen` cease to labor only unseen and begin to reveal harmony by measured disclosure. It is the principle by which light can be given selectively without ceasing to serve relation.

## Scope

`Serathor` carries:

- Selective revelation
- Guarded light
- Hierarchical disclosure
- Measured transmission of knowledge

## Daravelon (Reionization Era) Use

In `Daravelon` (the Reionization Era), the `Serathor` regulate access to luminous knowledge and make hidden harmony partially manifest without throwing every depth open at once.

## Boundaries

`Serathor` must not be mistaken for deception, arbitrary withholding, or soul-bearing priesthood already complete. It is guarded disclosure in service of relation, not falsehood.

## Relations

- [Serathen](serathen.md) are the solitary lineage from which `Serathor` rise.
- [Selkoron](selkoron.md) often depend on `Serathor` when luminous networks must be disclosed by degree rather than all at once.
- [Oraethal](oraethal.md) may resemble `Serathor` outwardly, but `Serathor` veil in order to preserve answerable relation rather than enclose light for itself.

## Note On Form

`Serathor` keeps the `serath` line while taking a more authoritative close, because unseen harmonizing maintenance here becomes selective and luminous disclosure.
