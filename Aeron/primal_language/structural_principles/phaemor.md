# Phaemor

- Rendered form: `Phaemor`
- Gloss: Tension Archivists; regulators of accumulated internal sequence and retained phase history
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaemor` names the fourth-order confinement intelligence that records and regulates sequence once time-pressure is borne inside an enclosure. It is the principle by which an interior gathers history rather than merely undergoing isolated moments.

## Scope

`Phaemor` carries:

- Proto-memory structure
- Accumulated phase history
- Internal timeline management
- Retained sequence under pressure

## Kethoran (Quark Era) Use

In `Kethoran` (the Quark Era), the `Phaemor` preserve accumulated internal sequence and make proto-memory possible inside confined beings.

## Boundaries

`Phaemor` must not be confused with conscious recollection, karmic judgment, or historical self-awareness. It is retained sequence, not reflective memory.

## Relations

- [Phaelor](phaelor.md) is the domain-order foundation from which `Phaemor` interiorizes.
- [Oraeth](oraeth.md) governs the threshold crossings through which `Phaemor` tracks accumulated sequence.
- [Athorin](athorin.md) retains the patterned kernel whose persistence `Phaemor` gives a history.

## Note On Form

`Phaemor` is a created-world rendering of a confinement-order intelligence, not an English archival term.
