# Taviloren

- Rendered form: `Taviloren`
- Gloss: Stars; fixed heavenly fires bearing named correspondence
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Taviloren` names the visible stars of the heavens as fixed, readable fires. It is the principle by which celestial lights stand in enduring place and bear correspondence to primal words without becoming the source of those words.

## Scope

`Taviloren` carries:

- Fixed sky-fire
- Enduring visibility
- Named correspondence
- Long-range orientation

## Planetary Use

In planetary astrology, the `Taviloren` form the visible lexicon of the sky: each primal-language word may be witnessed through its corresponding star.

## Boundaries

`Taviloren` must not be confused with the pre-stellar words they witness, with every deep ignition-order intelligence behind them, or with transient sky events such as shooting stars.

## Relations

- [Taviloron](taviloron.md) are the ignition orders through whom the first stable created lights arise.
- [Tavilorath](tavilorath.md) anchor enduring stellar systems at realm scale.
- [Tavirath](tavirath.md) are the readable constellations formed by related `Taviloren`.

## Note On Form

`Taviloren` keeps the `Tavilor` line while softening into a more observational surface, because the word names the star as visible fixed witness rather than the ignition office that first kindled it.
