# Nyxorys

- Rendered form: `Nyxorys`
- Gloss: Nyxorys; the darklight-bearing counter-luminary of Aeron
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Nyxorys` names the counterposed `Taviran` whose darklight tests, inverts, and reveals limits within Aeron's local sky without standing outside lawful creation.

## Scope

`Nyxorys` carries:

- Darklight
- Counterposed governance
- Testing revelation
- Bounded negation and taboo pressure

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Nyxorys` is the anti-sun and the second ruling `Taviran`, held in permanent opposition to `Solaryth`.

## Boundaries

`Nyxorys` must not be treated as a cosmic mistake, an independent evil principle, or mere absence of light. It is a lawful counter-luminary within Aeron's design.

## Relations

- [Taviran](taviran.md) is the broader celestial office to which `Nyxorys` belongs.
- [Solaryth](solaryth.md) is `Nyxorys`'s life-affirming counterpart in permanent opposition.
- [Seravel](seravel.md) reflect the mixed pressures of `Nyxorys` and `Solaryth` in local cyclical life.

## Note On Form

`Nyxorys` is preserved as an ancient celestial headword within the primal language. Its form is canonical even though it does not descend transparently from one dominant family already tracked in the current lexicon.
