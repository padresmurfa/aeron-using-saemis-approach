# Athoren

- Rendered form: `Athoren`
- Gloss: Proto-Selves; coherent composite interiors capable of structural self-reference
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Athoren` names the fifth-order composite intelligence that arises when `Athorin` achieve stable composite integration and a being acquires a unified pattern that can refer to itself in structure. It is the principle by which composite identity becomes continuous enough to prefigure later personhood without yet awakening into reflective self-awareness.

## Scope

`Athoren` carries:

- Internal self-reference
- Persistent unified pattern
- Strong identity continuity
- Composite self-configuration

## Selkoran (Hadron Era) Use

In `Selkoran` (the Hadron Era), the `Athoren` maintain coherent multi-part interior structure and make the first true proto-ego possible.

## Boundaries

`Athoren` must not be mistaken for soulhood fulfilled, self-awareness, moral freedom, or awakened ego. It is the architecture of selfhood, not selfhood complete.

## Relations

- [Athorin](athorin.md) is the confinement-order seed from which `Athoren` integrate.
- [Selkoren](selkoren.md) gives `Athoren` the coordinated plurality they unify into one enduring composite pattern.
- [Phaemoren](phaemoren.md) preserves the structural history through which `Athoren` continuity can deepen across cycles.

## Note On Form

`Athoren` is a created-world rendering of a fifth-order composite intelligence, not an English psychological abstraction.
