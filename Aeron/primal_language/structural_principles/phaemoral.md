# Phaemoral

- Rendered form: `Phaemoral`
- Gloss: Memory Stars; long-burning identities whose story lights whole ages
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaemoral` names the tenth-order ignition intelligence that arises when `Phaemorel` burn with remembered continuity and narrative itself becomes a durable source of orientation. It is the principle by which identity can anchor epochs through luminous remembrance.

## Scope

`Phaemoral` carries:

- Long-duration influence
- Myth-bearing memory
- Narrative anchoring across epochs
- Identity preserved in luminous continuity

## Daravelon (Reionization Era) Use

In `Daravelon` (the Reionization Era), the `Phaemoral` stabilize identity across epochs and transmit memory as a radiant continuity rather than only an inward shelter.

## Boundaries

`Phaemoral` must not be mistaken for fate already fixed, immortal soul-history, or institutions fully formed. It is myth-bearing continuity in created memory, not final destiny.

## Relations

- [Phaemorel](phaemorel.md) are the solitary lineage from which `Phaemoral` ignite.
- [Atheror](atheror.md) often clarify what `Phaemoral` preserve across ages.
- [Kethavel](kethavel.md) can embody the remembered patterns that `Phaemoral` hold luminous over time.

## Note On Form

`Phaemoral` stays close to the `phaemor` line while broadening into a more radiant surface, because memory here becomes star-like continuity rather than only inward recollection.
