# Phaethor

- Rendered form: `Phaethor`
- Gloss: Drift Flames; corrective radiances that burn away phase misalignment
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaethor` names the seventh-order radiant intelligence that arises when `Phaelin` are intensified by total luminosity and diagnosis becomes incandescent correction. It is the principle by which drift can be burned out of the field before it hardens into enduring disorder.

## Scope

`Phaethor` carries:

- Purifying function
- Corrective radiance
- Intense but non-moral intervention
- Drift-removal through luminous pressure

## Tavirelon (Photon Era) Use

In `Tavirelon` (the Photon Era), the `Phaethor` burn away accumulated misalignment and keep saturated awareness from collapsing into confused distortion.

## Boundaries

`Phaethor` must not be confused with judgment deities, punitive intelligences, or moral fire. It is corrective radiance, not verdict-bearing wrath.

## Relations

- [Phaelin](phaelin.md) are the mobility-order lineage from which `Phaethor` intensify.
- [Tavreth](tavreth.md) supply the fierce blaze through which `Phaethor` correction may become incandescent.
- [Seraeth](seraeth.md) reveal the patterns of drift that `Phaethor` then purge.

## Note On Form

`Phaethor` keeps the `phae` family because radiant correction in this age remains governed by phase, sequence, and misalignment rather than by arbitrary force.
