# Miravelor

- Rendered form: `Miravelor`
- Gloss: Oceanic Foundations; world-scale keepers of fluid continuity and basin depth
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Miravelor` names the twelfth-order world-binding office that arises when `Kethavelor` stabilize water-rich basins until `Miravel`-like transformation can persist as enduring fluid continuity. It is the principle by which a world keeps seas, depths, and chemistry-bearing flow from vanishing into mere passing event.

## Scope

`Miravelor` carries:

- Fluid continuity
- Basin depth
- Solvent persistence
- World-scale transformative flow

## Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era) Use

In `Kethralen` (the Planetary Formation Era), the `Miravelor` hold oceans and other lasting fluid domains in place so that worlds may retain chemical depth and remembered flow across ages.

## Boundaries

`Miravelor` must not be confused with animate seas, marine life already present, or a sentient ocean. It is persistent fluid foundation, not awakened water-personhood.

## Relations

- [Miravel](miravel.md) provides the older transmuting lineage whose flow becomes world-scale continuity in `Miravelor`.
- [Kethavelor](kethavelor.md) furnish the elemental patterned substance and basin-form within which `Miravelor` endure.
- [Oraethalen](oraethalen.md) often complement `Miravelor`, since seas and skies must answer one another for a world to keep continuity.

## Note On Form

`Miravelor` keeps the `miravel` line while thickening toward a more enduring close, because catalytic flow here becomes retained depth and basin memory rather than passing transformation alone.
