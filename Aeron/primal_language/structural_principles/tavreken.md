# Tavreken

- Rendered form: `Tavreken`
- Gloss: Fire Veins; volcanic conduits that release interior renewal through the crust
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavreken` names the thirteenth-order land-shaping office that arises when `Tavrekor` surface through faults, rifts, and volcanic corridors. It is the principle by which deep ember becomes episodic eruption, lava-bearing renewal, and heat-transfer through the land's broken shell.

## Scope

`Tavreken` carries:

- Volcanic release
- Crustal heat transfer
- Renewal through destructive fire
- Sudden terrain transformation

## Athralen (Continental Differentiation Era) Use

In `Athralen` (the Continental Differentiation Era), the `Tavreken` break open old surfaces, renew exhausted ground by fire, and keep continents from hardening into sterile stillness.

## Boundaries

`Tavreken` must not be confused with infernal invasion, moralized wrath, or creatures already living in flame. It is surface fire-release inside lawful geology, not adversarial incursion.

## Relations

- [Tavrekor](tavrekor.md) provide the deep ember from which `Tavreken` surface.
- [Kethaven](kethaven.md) preserve the pressured interior that `Tavreken` intermittently vent.
- [Kethralor](kethralor.md) and [Athrenel](athrenel.md) provide the plate seams and fracture corridors through which `Tavreken` can rise.

## Note On Form

`Tavreken` keeps the `tavrek` line while narrowing into a channel-bearing close, because ember here is no longer only kept in depth but made to run upward through living stone.
