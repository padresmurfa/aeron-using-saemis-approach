# Oraethen

- Rendered form: `Oraethen`
- Gloss: Boundary Spheres; composite shells that regulate interior-exterior exchange
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Oraethen` names the fifth-order composite intelligence that arises when `Oraeth` integrate with `Kethor` and a gathered being acquires a stable, regulated shell. It is the principle by which a composite whole keeps a defined self-boundary without sealing itself into sterility.

## Scope

`Oraethen` carries:

- Defined self-boundary
- Controlled permeability
- Interior-exterior regulation
- Durable composite enclosure

## Selkoran (Hadron Era) Use

In `Selkoran` (the Hadron Era), the `Oraethen` stabilize interior and exterior exchange and make regulated composite wholeness possible.

## Boundaries

`Oraethen` must not be mistaken for later biological membranes, planetary atmospheres, or ego-boundary archetypes fully formed. It is a composite threshold-shell, not mature embodied personhood.

## Relations

- [Oraeth](oraeth.md) is the confinement-order threshold lineage from which `Oraethen` deepen.
- [Kethor](kethor.md) provides the durable enclosure that `Oraethen` render composite and permeable.
- [Ketharen](ketharen.md) often depend on `Oraethen` to keep their gathered radiance from uncontrolled dissipation.

## Note On Form

`Oraethen` preserves the `ora` family because threshold governance dominates the composite, even though `Kethor` gives it structural durability.
