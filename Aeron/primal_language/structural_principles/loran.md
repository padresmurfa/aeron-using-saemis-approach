# Loran

- Rendered form: `Loran`
- Gloss: divine oscillation; rhythmic alternation within singular being
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Loran` names the oscillatory condition that begins when the Creator becomes `C` through `Orun`. It is not division into parts, but rhythmic alternation within one harmonic being.

## Scope

`Loran` carries:

- Alternation without fragmentation
- Rhythm without chaos
- Internal relation within singular identity

## First-Pulse Use

At the level of the First Pulse, `Loran` is the root condition for phase, resonance, harmony, and all later structured emergence.

## Boundaries

`Loran` must not be reduced to material vibration in space. It is pre-material divine rhythm.

## Relations

- [Orun](../threshold_events/orun.md) opens `Loran`.
- [Tavir](tavir.md) is the polar structure within `Loran`.
- [Phaes](phaes.md) is the ordered transition within `Loran`.
- [Seren](seren.md) is the aligned state of `Loran`.

## Note On Form

`Loran` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
