# Selkorel

- Rendered form: `Selkorel`
- Gloss: Soil Weavers; slow harmonizers that turn weathered stone into fertile substrate
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Selkorel` names the thirteenth-order land-shaping office that arises when `Selkoral` meet mineral breakdown and dispersed matter is patiently woven into coherent earth. It is the principle by which ground becomes particulate, layered, porous, and able one day to bear roots and fragile continuities.

## Scope

`Selkorel` carries:

- Micro-structural harmonization
- Soil and substrate formation
- Cohesion through breakdown
- Future rooting-ground preparation

## Athralen (Continental Differentiation Era) Use

In `Athralen` (the Continental Differentiation Era), the `Selkorel` turn shattered stone, basin deposit, and atmospheric wear into the first true soils, though no living thing yet roots within them.

## Boundaries

`Selkorel` must not be confused with gardens already alive, agriculture already practiced, or completed ecosystems. It is prepared earth, not cultivated or inhabited ground.

## Relations

- [Selkoral](selkoral.md) provide the field-like cohesion from which `Selkorel` localize patient substrate weaving.
- [Miravelen](miravelen.md) collect the sediments and lowland deposits that `Selkorel` can bind into earth.
- [Vathalen](vathalen.md) depend on `Selkorel` wherever cradle-like balance must eventually become root-bearing terrain.

## Note On Form

`Selkorel` keeps the `selkor` family while lightening into a more distributed and earthy close, because cohesion here no longer only binds interiors but gathers broken matter into living-ready ground.
