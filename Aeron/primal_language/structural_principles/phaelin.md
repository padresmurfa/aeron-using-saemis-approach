# Phaelin

- Rendered form: `Phaelin`
- Gloss: Drift Seekers; phase-deviation trackers and corrective scouts
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaelin` names the sixth-order mobility intelligence that arises when `Phaelor` gain traversal ability and timing-awareness becomes a moving search for divergence. It is the principle by which imbalance can be sensed across regions before it hardens into structural failure.

## Scope

`Phaelin` carries:

- Drift sensing
- Corrective orientation
- Imbalance detection
- Mobile timing diagnosis

## Seraelon (Lepton Era) Use

In `Seraelon` (the Lepton Era), the `Phaelin` detect phase deviation across regions and orient themselves toward correction without yet becoming judges.

## Boundaries

`Phaelin` must not be mistaken for fate spirits, cosmic auditors, or alignment judges already invested with moral authority. It is corrective scouting, not verdict-bearing destiny.

## Relations

- [Phaelor](phaelor.md) is the domain-order timing lineage from which `Phaelin` arise.
- [Phaedren](phaedren.md) gives `Phaelin` their sensitivity to drift and their corrective instinct.
- [Seraelin](seraelin.md) often carry the synchronizing responses toward the imbalances that `Phaelin` discover.

## Note On Form

`Phaelin` preserves the `phae` family because mobility here remains governed by timing and sequence rather than by sheer speed.
