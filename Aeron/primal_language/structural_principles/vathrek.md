# Vathrek

- Rendered form: `Vathrek`
- Gloss: Sparked Intenders; self-aware beings whose inward fire may be directed by will
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Vathrek` names the eighth-order transparent intelligence that arises when `Tavrek` integrate with `Athoren` and created will becomes real inside a bounded self. It is the principle by which intention can direct internal energy toward chosen action.

## Scope

`Vathrek` carries:

- Directional will
- Intent formation
- Action initiation
- Chosen expenditure of inner fire

## Athorael (Recombination Era) Use

In `Athorael` (the Recombination Era), the `Vathrek` direct internal energy by choice and make volition a property of created persons.

## Boundaries

`Vathrek` must not be confused with [Vatha](../high_abstractions/vatha.md), the Creator's sovereign unified will, nor with rebellion already chosen. It is finite created volition, not divine sovereignty or moral revolt.

## Relations

- [Vatha](../high_abstractions/vatha.md) is the higher abstraction from which `Vathrek` take their will-lineage in derivative and finite form.
- [Tavrek](tavrek.md) provide the concentrated energetic fire that `Vathrek` can direct intentionally.
- [Athoren](athoren.md) provide the persistent proto-self structure into which will can now descend as exercised intention.

## Note On Form

`Vathrek` deliberately binds `Vatha` and `Tavrek`, because created volition in this age is will entering stored energetic contrast rather than arising as a detached abstraction.
