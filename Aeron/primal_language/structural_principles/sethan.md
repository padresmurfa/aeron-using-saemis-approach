# Sethan

- Rendered form: `Sethan`
- Gloss: standing wave; stable harmonic structure within oscillation
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Sethan` names the stable, persistent pattern that arises within the oscillatory field when oscillation steadies into a fixed rhythm. It is the metaphysical condition that allows for the emergence of localized stability and the formation of archetypes.

## Scope

`Sethan` carries:

- Stability within oscillation
- Localized persistence
- Foundation for archetypal emergence

## Lorathen (Grand Unification Era) Use

In `Lorathen` (the Grand Unification Era), `Sethan` names the structures within the divine oscillation that give rise to the first archetypes (`Tavren`, `Dareth`, `Serath`, and `Kethar`). It is not a being, but the stable pattern that makes such emergence possible.

## Boundaries

`Sethan` must not be confused with material waves or physical vibration. It is a metaphysical structure, not a physical phenomenon.

## Relations

- [Serath](serath.md) stabilizes the relationships that form `Sethan`.
- [Kethar](kethar.md) establishes the persistence of `Sethan`.

## Note On Form

`Sethan` is a created-world rendering of a metaphysical structure, not an English phrase or a literal wave.
