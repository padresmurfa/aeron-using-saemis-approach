# Mirasin

- Rendered form: `Mirasin`
- Gloss: Echoes; residual harmonic traces that carry identity imprints without full structure
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Mirasin` names the sixth-order mobility intelligence that appears when composite encounter leaves behind transformed signatures able to travel after the originating form has passed. It is the principle by which identity can cast a mobile trace without sending the whole self.

## Scope

`Mirasin` carries:

- Fragmentary identity imprint
- Memory-bearing residue
- Unstable persistence
- After-relation transmission

## Seraelon (Lepton Era) Use

In `Seraelon` (the Lepton Era), the `Mirasin` carry identity traces between structures and make residual relational memory possible without preserving the full being.

## Boundaries

`Mirasin` must not be confused with ancestral souls, complete surviving selves, or soul fragments already canonized as persons-in-pieces. It is mobile residue, not personhood broken loose.

## Relations

- [Phaemoren](phaemoren.md) provides the structural history from which `Mirasin` traces can be shed.
- [Athoren](athoren.md) gives `Mirasin` the identity imprint they carry in fragmentary form.
- [Atharin](atharin.md) can register the traces that `Mirasin` leave behind across encounter.

## Note On Form

`Mirasin` preserves the `mir` family because an echo is a transformed remainder of encounter, not a stable memory-whole.
