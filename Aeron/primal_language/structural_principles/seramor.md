# Seramor

- Rendered form: `Seramor`
- Gloss: Blood Moon; the deep red tide-moon of Aeron
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Seramor` names the grave primary `Seravel` of Aeron: the moon of undertow, wound-memory, and the costly half of lawful water.

## Scope

`Seramor` carries:

- Deep tides and undertow
- Wound-memory and red reckoning
- Costly order
- Grave return beneath visible rhythm

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Seramor` is one of the two primary `Seravel` and the depth-bearing counterpart to `Serenel` in the world's Water axis.

## Boundaries

`Seramor` must not be flattened into blood as substance alone, eclipse superstition, or a merely ominous title. It is the canonical primal headword of Aeron's red tide-moon.

## Relations

- [Seravel](seravel.md) is the broader celestial office to which `Seramor` belongs.
- [Serenel](serenel.md) is `Seramor`'s bright-water counterpart in the primary tidal pair.
- [Nyxorys](nyxorys.md) weighs more heavily in `Seramor`'s readings, though `Seramor` still answers the full `Solaryth`-`Nyxorys` axis.

## Note On Form

`Seramor` keeps the `ser` harmony-phase lineage but darkens into a more burdened close, because lawful water here descends into cost, depth, and remembered injury.
