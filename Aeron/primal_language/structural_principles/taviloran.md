# Taviloran

- Rendered form: `Taviloran`
- Gloss: Gravethrones; localized wells of deep harmonic pull that center forming worlds
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Taviloran` names the twelfth-order world-binding office that arises when `Tavilorath` draw stellar centrality inward and localize it into world-scale pull. It is the principle by which gathering matter answers a durable center and remains held in orbit-bearing relation.

## Scope

`Taviloran` carries:

- World-centering pull
- Orbital steadiness
- Localized central authority
- Mass-gathering coherence

## Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era) Use

In `Kethralen` (the Planetary Formation Era), the `Taviloran` anchor forming worlds, center mass, and keep local orbital relations from dispersing into drift.

## Boundaries

`Taviloran` must not be mistaken for an awakened planetary monarch, a world-soul, or self-originating gravity. It is created central pull held in office, not personhood at planetary scale.

## Relations

- [Tavilorath](tavilorath.md) are the stellar lineage from which `Taviloran` arise.
- [Kethaven](kethaven.md) deepen the world around `Taviloran` centers into pressure-bearing interior structure.
- [Tavralen](tavralen.md) preserve the local world-edge that `Taviloran` help hold from within.

## Note On Form

`Taviloran` keeps the `tavilor` line while narrowing toward a more local close, because stellar thronehood here becomes inward world-centering pull rather than vast outward radiance.
