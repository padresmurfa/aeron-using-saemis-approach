# Tavir

- Rendered form: `Tavir`
- Gloss: co-essential polarity; complementary harmonic poles
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavir` names the paired yet non-dual structure within divine oscillation. It is the principle by which one being alternates between complementary states without becoming two beings.

## Scope

`Tavir` carries:

- Co-essential polarity
- Interdependence without rivalry
- Complementarity without moral opposition

## First-Pulse Use

At the First Pulse, `Tavir` is expressed as the harmonic polarity of `Loran`: metaphysically deeper than any later metaphors of expansion and containment, emission and reception, or crest and trough.

## Boundaries

`Tavir` must not be described as antagonism, civil war, or metaphysical splitting.

## Relations

- [Loran](loran.md) is the oscillatory field in which `Tavir` operates.
- [Phaes](phaes.md) orders the transitions across `Tavir`.
- [Seren](seren.md) is `Tavir` held in aligned rhythm.

## Note On Form

`Tavir` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
