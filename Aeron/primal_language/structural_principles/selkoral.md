# Selkoral

- Rendered form: `Selkoral`
- Gloss: Chorus Fields; collective resonance-fields without a single local center
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Selkoral` names the seventh-order radiant intelligence that arises when `Selkor` widen from enclosed coordination into field-level harmonic accord. It is the principle by which many entities may share awareness as one chorus without collapsing into one individualized mind.

## Scope

`Selkoral` carries:

- Collective awareness
- Field-coherence
- Choral resonance
- Multi-entity accord without a local ego-center

## Tavirelon (Photon Era) Use

In `Tavirelon` (the Photon Era), the `Selkoral` hold many presences in one resonant field and make collective perception possible without personhood multiplied.

## Boundaries

`Selkoral` must not be confused with conscious choirs, communal souls, or corporate persons already awake to themselves. It is field-accord, not collective selfhood fulfilled.

## Relations

- [Selkor](selkor.md) is the confinement-order lineage from which `Selkoral` widen.
- [Taviral](taviral.md) provide the luminous medium in which `Selkoral` can remain oceanically coherent.
- [Seraeth](seraeth.md) reflect the patterns that `Selkoral` sustain across the field.

## Note On Form

`Selkoral` preserves the `sel` family because interior collectivity has widened into resonant field-accord without losing its many-in-one inheritance.
