# Orael

- Rendered form: `Orael`
- Gloss: Veilwalkers; threshold intelligences of subtle passage and rare transition
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Orael` names the third-order interactional intelligence that permits slight influence across boundaries and low-probability transitions between states. It is the lawful thinning of closure without the abolition of distinction.

## Scope

`Orael` carries:

- Rare transition
- Threshold passage
- Permeability gradients
- Hidden but lawful causality

## Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era) Use

In `Mirevar` (the Electroweak Separation Era), the `Orael` allow subtle influence to pass where absolute closure would otherwise prevail, making unlikely but lawful transitions possible.

## Boundaries

`Orael` must not be mistaken for death spirits, smugglers of law, or beings outside order. It is threshold intelligence, not hidden autonomy outside reality's fabric.

## Relations

- [Tavral](tavral.md) provides the boundaries across which `Orael` moves.
- [Phaelor](phaelor.md) orders the timing of the rare transitions `Orael` permits.
- [Tavhor](tavhor.md) maintains the contrast that gives threshold passage meaning.

## Note On Form

`Orael` is a created-world rendering of an interactional-order intelligence, not an English mystical epithet.
