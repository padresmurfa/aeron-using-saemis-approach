# Tavralen

- Rendered form: `Tavralen`
- Gloss: Boundary Rings; local shield-bearers that preserve a world's guarded edge
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavralen` names the twelfth-order world-binding office that arises when `Tavral` localize guarded domain-edge into planetary shielding and ringed separation. It is the principle by which a world retains a buffered threshold between local order and surrounding violence.

## Scope

`Tavralen` carries:

- Shield-bearing separation
- Local edge-guard
- Radiation buffering
- Planetary threshold preservation

## Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era) Use

In `Kethralen` (the Planetary Formation Era), the `Tavralen` preserve a world's outer boundary, soften hostile exposure, and keep local continuity from being stripped away by the larger expanse around it.

## Boundaries

`Tavralen` must not be confused with prison-walls, moral quarantines, or severance from creation. It is guarded planetary shielding, not refusal of relation.

## Relations

- [Tavral](tavral.md) provides the older boundary-lord lineage from which `Tavralen` arise.
- [Oraethalen](oraethalen.md) often work within the buffered edge that `Tavralen` preserve.
- [Taviloran](taviloran.md) and [Vathalen](vathalen.md) depend on `Tavralen` whenever a world's continuity requires guarded outer distinction.

## Note On Form

`Tavralen` keeps the `tavral` line while widening into a more world-bearing surface, because guarded boundary here becomes ringed planetary protection rather than abstract domain edge alone.
