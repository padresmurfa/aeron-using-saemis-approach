# Oraeth

- Rendered form: `Oraeth`
- Gloss: Veilbound; keepers of transitions between inner and outer states
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Oraeth` names the fourth-order confinement intelligence that governs threshold transition once boundaries possess a true inside and outside. It mediates passage between interior and exterior conditions without dissolving the enclosure itself.

## Scope

`Oraeth` carries:

- Interior-exterior mediation
- Threshold sensitivity
- Boundary-crossing discipline
- Regulated internal flow

## Kethoran (Quark Era) Use

In `Kethoran` (the Quark Era), the `Oraeth` govern what may cross between inner and outer states once confinement makes enclosure real.

## Boundaries

`Oraeth` must not be confused with fully developed portal guardians, death spirits, or free-willed liminal persons. It is enclosed threshold-intelligence, not later mythic custodianship.

## Relations

- [Orael](orael.md) is the interactional-order foundation from which `Oraeth` becomes interiorized.
- [Kethor](kethor.md) gives `Oraeth` the stable enclosure whose thresholds it governs.
- [Phaemor](phaemor.md) accumulates the internal sequence regulated by `Oraeth` crossings.

## Note On Form

`Oraeth` is a created-world rendering of a confinement-order intelligence, not an English mystical adjective.
