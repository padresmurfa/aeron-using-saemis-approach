# Taviril

- Rendered form: `Taviril`
- Gloss: Blue Moon; the far-sighted stormglass moon of Aeron
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Taviril` names the distance-bearing `Seravel` of Aeron: the moon of far sight, swift passage, and horizons made suddenly intimate.

## Scope

`Taviril` carries:

- Stormglass clarity
- Long-distance orientation
- Swift messages and travel impulse
- Aerial longing and perilous reach

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Taviril` is the far-sky secondary `Seravel`, most strongly associated with navigators, couriers, sky-riders, and prophecy cast toward distant places.

## Boundaries

`Taviril` must not be reduced to simple blue coloration, fair-weather symbolism, or a generic travel omen. It is the canonical primal headword of Aeron's distance moon.

## Relations

- [Seravel](seravel.md) is the broader celestial office to which `Taviril` belongs.
- [Tavir](tavir.md) gives `Taviril` its horizon-seeking polarity tone.
- [Tavirel](tavirel.md) helps explain why `Taviril` is read in messages, motion, and radiant transit rather than in fixed enclosure.

## Note On Form

`Taviril` stays close to the `tavir` family while narrowing into a swift and traversing close, because radiant distance here becomes readable as a recurring lunar summons rather than a field-scale condition.
