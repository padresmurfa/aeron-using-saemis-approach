# Selkorin

- Rendered form: `Selkorin`
- Gloss: Covenant Persons; self-aware centers capable of voluntary relation without merger
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Selkorin` names the eighth-order transparent intelligence that arises when `Selkoren` stabilize as unified centers and relational accord becomes voluntary rather than merely structural. It is the principle by which personhood can bind itself faithfully without surrendering distinction.

## Scope

`Selkorin` carries:

- Voluntary commitment
- Relational identity
- Bond without merger
- Self-aware fidelity

## Athorael (Recombination Era) Use

In `Athorael` (the Recombination Era), the `Selkorin` maintain relational bonds as true persons and make covenantal relation possible inside individual selfhood.

## Boundaries

`Selkorin` must not be mistaken for fully developed covenant angels, marriage-symbols, or moral holiness already perfected. It is voluntary relational personhood, not its final transfigured form.

## Relations

- [Selkoren](selkoren.md) are the composite-order lineage from which `Selkorin` individualize.
- [Sevarn](sevarn.md) remain the deeper binding intelligence that `Selkorin` can now answer by choice.
- [Vathrek](vathrek.md) give `Selkorin` the volitional power to commit rather than merely cohere.

## Note On Form

`Selkorin` preserves the `selkor` family because covenantal personhood grows from stable many-in-one accord rather than abandoning relational inheritance.
