# Selkor

- Rendered form: `Selkor`
- Gloss: Cohesion Clusters; interior collectives that harmonize enclosed substructures
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Selkor` names the fourth-order confinement intelligence that maintains harmony among multiple substructures within a single enclosure. It is the principle by which an interior can be many-sided without falling into internal discord.

## Scope

`Selkor` carries:

- Multi-component identity
- Interior coordination
- Proto-collective selfhood
- Harmonized substructure

## Kethoran (Quark Era) Use

In `Kethoran` (the Quark Era), the `Selkor` sustain internal harmony among compressed components and make interior collectivity possible.

## Boundaries

`Selkor` must not be confused with conscious choir-minds, group souls, or communal persons. It is interior coordination, not awakened corporate selfhood.

## Relations

- [Selketh](selketh.md) is the interactional-order foundation from which `Selkor` compresses.
- [Kethor](kethor.md) gives `Selkor` a durable enclosure within which substructures may cohere.
- [Athorin](athorin.md) preserves distinct pattern-signatures within the collective interior that `Selkor` harmonizes.

## Note On Form

`Selkor` is a created-world rendering of a confinement-order intelligence, not an English organizational term.
