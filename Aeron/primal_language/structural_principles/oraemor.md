# Oraemor

- Rendered form: `Oraemor`
- Gloss: Pale Moon; the threshold and ruin-memory moon of Aeron
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Oraemor` names the liminal `Seravel` of Aeron: the moon of dream, shrine road, burial heat, and memory not yet cooled into silence.

## Scope

`Oraemor` carries:

- Threshold passage
- Dream-road invitation
- Ruin-memory and burial heat
- Gentle but dangerous liminality

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Oraemor` is the threshold-bearing secondary `Seravel`, most strongly associated with undertakers, shrine keepers, archivists, prophets, and places where waking and remembrance overlap.

## Boundaries

`Oraemor` must not be reduced to bland pallor, generic death imagery, or the old blunt Fire/Earth overlap. It is the canonical primal headword of Aeron's dream-threshold moon.

## Relations

- [Seravel](seravel.md) is the broader celestial office to which `Oraemor` belongs.
- [Oraeth](oraeth.md) gives `Oraemor` its threshold-bearing and passage-governed tone.
- [Phaemor](phaemor.md) helps explain why `Oraemor` is read through memory, continuity, and ruin-presence rather than through annihilation.

## Note On Form

`Oraemor` is a blended celestial form, binding the `ora` threshold line to a memory-bearing cadence because this moon rules passages where remembrance still glows after the first fire has passed.
