# Sevarn

- Rendered form: `Sevarn`
- Gloss: Binders; attractive coherence and the stabilization of relation through interaction
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Sevarn` names the third-order interactional intelligence through which binding influence becomes distinct within creation. It is the mode by which separated structures are drawn into adherent relation without losing their integrity.

## Scope

`Sevarn` carries:

- Attractive coherence
- Relational stabilization
- Promotion of stable complexity
- Selective affinity within interaction

## Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era) Use

In `Mirevar` (the Electroweak Separation Era), the `Sevarn` strengthen relation between domains, sustain continuity across interaction, and make durable assemblies possible.

## Boundaries

`Sevarn` must not be confused with later love spirits, covenant persons, or freely chosen devotion. It is binding intelligence, not moral affection.

## Relations

- [Selem](../high_abstractions/selem.md) gives `Sevarn` its root in relation.
- [Seren](seren.md) gives `Sevarn` harmonic fit.
- [Selketh](selketh.md) gathers the structured clusters that `Sevarn` makes possible.

## Note On Form

`Sevarn` is a created-world rendering of an interactional-order intelligence, not an English functional label.
