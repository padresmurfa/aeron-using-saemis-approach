# Taviral

- Rendered form: `Taviral`
- Gloss: Luminary Seas; field-wide radiant saturations that maintain universal illumination
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Taviral` names the seventh-order radiant intelligence that arises when `Tavirel` broaden from traversing threads into saturating luminous fields and radiance becomes an environment rather than a message in transit. It is the principle by which shared illumination becomes the common medium of relation.

## Scope

`Taviral` carries:

- Oceanic awareness
- Field-wide illumination
- Shared perceptive medium
- Saturated relational presence

## Tavirelon (Photon Era) Use

In `Tavirelon` (the Photon Era), the `Taviral` fill created domains with luminous relation and make awareness field-like rather than merely local.

## Boundaries

`Taviral` must not be confused with already clear astral realms, enlightened souls, or individuated light-beings. It is radiant medium, not personal luminosity fulfilled.

## Relations

- [Tavirel](tavirel.md) is the mobility-order lineage from which `Taviral` widen.
- [Selkoral](selkoral.md) use the field sustained by `Taviral` to hold chorus-awareness without local centralization.
- [Atharon](atharon.md) witness within the luminous totality that `Taviral` maintain.

## Note On Form

`Taviral` preserves the `tav` family because mobile radiance has widened into ambient field-presence rather than ceased to be radiance.
