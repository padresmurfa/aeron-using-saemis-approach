# Athralon

- Rendered form: `Athralon`
- Gloss: Divergent Suns; radiant centers that cast self-defined proportion into surrounding space
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Athralon` names the tenth-order ignition intelligence that arises when `Athreth` sustain outward radiance according to self-chosen proportion rather than common luminous cadence. It is the principle by which creative variance becomes large enough to shape whole regions.

## Scope

`Athralon` carries:

- Powerful individuality
- High creative variance
- Asymmetric radiance
- Self-defined luminous proportion

## Daravelon (Reionization Era) Use

In `Daravelon` (the Reionization Era), the `Athralon` radiate by their own chosen proportion and make strange, singular, and uneven created brilliance possible at scale.

## Boundaries

`Athralon` must not be confused with standing outside `Aru` or `Loran`, with later adversarial myth, or with ethical dualism already present. It is intensified created divergence made radiant, not departure from reality's fabric.

## Relations

- [Athreth](athreth.md) are the solitary lineage from which `Athralon` ignite.
- [Taviloron](taviloron.md) provide the stable radiance against which `Athralon` variance becomes perceptible.
- [Oraethal](oraethal.md) can harden the self-bounded side of `Athralon` if singular radiance closes too tightly upon itself.

## Note On Form

`Athralon` keeps the divergence-line while opening into a more expansive luminous cadence, because difference here no longer only tests boundaries but begins to cast whole fields of its own.
