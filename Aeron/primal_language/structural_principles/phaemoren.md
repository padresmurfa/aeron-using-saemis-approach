# Phaemoren

- Rendered form: `Phaemoren`
- Gloss: Tension Matrices; linked interiors that retain pressure, history, and transformation potential
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaemoren` names the fifth-order composite intelligence that arises when `Phaemor` bind with `Mirath` and stored pressure becomes a durable matrix of remembered transformation. It is the principle by which a composite being gathers structural history without surrendering adaptive capacity.

## Scope

`Phaemoren` carries:

- Memory-integrated transformation
- Accumulated structural history
- Adaptive pressure management
- Retained transformation potential

## Selkoran (Hadron Era) Use

In `Selkoran` (the Hadron Era), the `Phaemoren` preserve accumulated pressure as structural memory and make enduring transformational depth possible inside composites.

## Boundaries

`Phaemoren` must not be confused with karmic judgment, conscious lineage memory, or reflective historical awareness. It is retained structural history, not awakened remembrance.

## Relations

- [Phaemor](phaemor.md) gives `Phaemoren` their accumulated phase-history.
- [Mirath](mirath.md) supplies the adaptive pressure and reforming potential that `Phaemoren` preserve.
- [Athoren](athoren.md) receives the stronger continuity that `Phaemoren` can sustain across repeated internal change.

## Note On Form

`Phaemoren` preserves the `phae` family because retained sequence governs the composite, even though its adaptive pressure descends from `Mirath`.
