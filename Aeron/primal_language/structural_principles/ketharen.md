# Ketharen

- Rendered form: `Ketharen`
- Gloss: Heartforged; stabilized energetic centers that persist as composite cores
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Ketharen` names the fifth-order composite intelligence that arises when `Kethor` bind with `Tavrek` and gathered radiance becomes a durable interior center. It is the principle by which composite beings hold energetic force in a stable hearth rather than letting it disperse at once.

## Scope

`Ketharen` carries:

- Composite core stability
- Stored radiance
- Persistent harmonic signature
- Pressure-bearing ignition reserve

## Selkoran (Hadron Era) Use

In `Selkoran` (the Hadron Era), the `Ketharen` create stabilized energetic centers and make durable composite cores possible.

## Boundaries

`Ketharen` must not be confused with later stars, world-hearts, or divine thrones. It is a composite energetic hearth, not yet a mature cosmic sovereign center.

## Relations

- [Kethor](kethor.md) gives `Ketharen` the durable core around which the composite gathers.
- [Tavrek](tavrek.md) supplies the concentrated radiance and energetic contrast that `Ketharen` stabilize.
- [Oraethen](oraethen.md) governs the controlled exchange through which `Ketharen` centers answer their surroundings.

## Note On Form

`Ketharen` preserves the `keth` family because stability governs the composite, even though its radiance descends from `Tavrek`.
