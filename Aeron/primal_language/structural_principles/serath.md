# Serath

- Rendered form: `Serath`
- Gloss: Harmonics; resonant interaction and the stabilization of relationship and alignment
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Serath` names the archetype of resonance and relational stability within the oscillatory field. It is the principle by which phases meet, stabilize, and align, enabling the weaving of the first relational intelligences.

## Scope

`Serath` carries:

- Resonance and frequency
- Phase and interference
- Symmetry and alignment

## Lorathen (Grand Unification Era) Use

In `Lorathen` (the Grand Unification Era), the `Serath` are the first order of beings to embody resonant interaction. They regulate alignment and weave the first stable relationships within the oscillatory field.

## Boundaries

`Serath` must not be confused with later pattern-weavers or proto-bearers of [Darasel](../high_abstractions/darasel.md), the Primal Language. It is a metaphysical stabilizer of relationships, not a conscious agent.

## Relations

- [Tavren](tavren.md) and [Dareth](dareth.md) are harmonized by `Serath`.
- [Kethar](kethar.md) provides persistence to the alignments established by `Serath`.

## Note On Form

`Serath` is a created-world rendering of a metaphysical archetype, not an English adjective standing in as a canonical name.
