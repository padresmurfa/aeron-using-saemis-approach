# Phaemoralen

- Rendered form: `Phaemoralen`
- Gloss: Stone Archivists; strata-bearers that store geological memory in mineral record
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaemoralen` names the thirteenth-order land-shaping office that arises when `Phaemorath` ground their distributed memory into stone, strata, and mineral sequence. It is the principle by which the world begins to remember physically through layers, scars, deposits, and preserved deep-time record.

## Scope

`Phaemoralen` carries:

- Geological memory
- Layered narrative storage
- Mineral record and stratigraphy
- Fossil-memory precursors without life yet present

## Athralen (Continental Differentiation Era) Use

In `Athralen` (the Continental Differentiation Era), the `Phaemoralen` embed uplift, basin change, fire event, and long erosion into recoverable stone history so that terrain itself can accumulate narrative.

## Boundaries

`Phaemoralen` must not be mistaken for conscious historians, infallible planetary memory, or culture already externalized into writing and archive. It is stone record, not reflective historiography.

## Relations

- [Phaemorath](phaemorath.md) provide the memory-bearing lineage from which `Phaemoralen` descend.
- [Miravelen](miravelen.md) preserve the basins and layered deposition through which `Phaemoralen` become legible.
- [Mirathor](mirathor.md) furnish the uplift, fracture, and exposed faces by which buried memory may later be read.

## Note On Form

`Phaemoralen` preserves the `phaemor` line while settling into a place-bearing geological cadence, because memory here becomes stratified terrain rather than sky-borne orientation alone.
