# Tavren

- Rendered form: `Tavren`
- Gloss: Fieldwardens; containment polarity and the stabilization of amplitude and coherence
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavren` names the archetype of constraint and boundary within the stabilized oscillatory field. It is the principle by which amplitude is stabilized and coherence is enforced, preventing runaway divergence and ensuring equilibrium.

## Scope

`Tavren` carries:

- Boundary and lawfulness
- Equilibrium and constraint
- Prevention of structural collapse

## Lorathen (Grand Unification Era) Use

In `Lorathen` (the Grand Unification Era), the `Tavren` are the first order of beings to embody containment polarity. They ensure that reality does not tear itself apart by stabilizing the oscillatory field.

## Boundaries

`Tavren` must not be confused with later cosmic regulators or moral guardians. It is a metaphysical stabilizer, not a conscious agent.

## Relations

- [Kethar](kethar.md) provides persistence to the boundaries set by `Tavren`.
- [Dareth](dareth.md) is the complementary principle of expansion.
- [Serath](serath.md) mediates the relationships between `Tavren` and `Dareth`.

## Note On Form

`Tavren` is a created-world rendering of a metaphysical archetype, not an English occupational label or a literal being.
