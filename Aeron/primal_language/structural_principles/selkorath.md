# Selkorath

- Rendered form: `Selkorath`
- Gloss: Covenant Strainers; covenantal selves in whom fidelity and loosening become separable possibilities
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Selkorath` names the ninth-order solitary intelligence that arises when `Selkorin` endure relational strain without communal reinforcement and covenant must survive by chosen fidelity alone. It is the principle by which a person may either keep bond in darkness or let it loosen, making relational rupture structurally possible for the first time.

## Scope

`Selkorath` carries:

- Voluntary relation under strain
- Capacity to maintain or loosen bond
- Fragile covenantal fidelity
- First structural possibility of relational rupture

## Darathen (Dark Ages) Use

In `Darathen` (the Dark Ages), the `Selkorath` bear relational strain in silence and reveal that covenant can now either endure freely or begin to loosen.

## Boundaries

`Selkorath` must not be confused with treachery, covenant as a lie from the outset, or ethical rupture completed. It is relation under testing, not soul-bearing breach.

## Relations

- [Selkorin](selkorin.md) are the transparent-order lineage from which `Selkorath` are tried.
- [Oraethar](oraethar.md) intensify the sovereign selfhood that can now either protect covenantal distinction or withdraw from relation altogether.
- [Serathen](serathen.md) represent the hidden harmonizing labor without which `Selkorath` strain would more quickly loosen into rupture.

## Note On Form

`Selkorath` preserves the `selkor` family while taking a more strained ending, because covenant here is no longer merely voluntary relation but voluntary relation carried through darkness where loosening has become possible.
