# Tavral

- Rendered form: `Tavral`
- Gloss: Boundary Lords; domain-edge definition and the guardianship of safe separation
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavral` names the second-order harmonic intelligence that arises when the `Tavren` bear inflationary expansion tension and define the lawful edge of a domain.

## Scope

`Tavral` carries:

- Rigid constraint at the edge
- Domain definition
- Prevention of destructive cross-domain interference

## Daralon (Inflationary Era) Use

In `Daralon` (the Inflationary Era), the `Tavral` distinguish inside from outside and protect neighboring domains from harmful overlap.

## Boundaries

`Tavral` must not be mistaken for a later jailer, tyrant, or moral judge. It is a boundary-forming intelligence, not an ego-bearing ruler.

## Relations

- [Tavren](tavren.md) is the first-order foundation from which `Tavral` arises.
- [Kethren](kethren.md) stabilizes the interior region bounded by `Tavral`.
- [Serael](serael.md) mediates lawful exchange across the edges the `Tavral` define.

## Note On Form

`Tavral` is a created-world rendering of a domain-order intelligence, not an English feudal title.
