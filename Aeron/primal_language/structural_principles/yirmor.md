# Yirmor

- Rendered form: `Yirmor`
- Gloss: The Slow-Unmade, Decay-Vatharen; the wandering force-bearer of cosmic entropy
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Yirmor` names the [Vatharen](vatharen.md) that bears Decay as the cosmic principle of slow-unmaking. It is the principle by which what is, becomes less, made visible as a wandering radiant body in Aeron's sky.

## Scope

`Yirmor` carries:

- Cosmic entropy and slow-unmaking
- The substance of decline-into-depth
- The patience of dissolution
- Decay as primal force, not as a personal god of death or destruction

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Yirmor` is gazed upon by those who attend to dissolution as cosmic mode: physicians of long illness, ruin-readers, mages of decomposition, and those who understand that nothing kept stays kept without effort. Its mana-flavor expresses as the felt pull toward depth and exhaustion. Its paired counterpart is [Yirven](yirven.md), the Increase-Vatharen.

## Boundaries

`Yirmor` must not be confused with [Oraemor](oraemor.md) (the moon of liminal threshold, which is the dream-edge between waking and remembrance) or with personal gods of death in tier-4 pantheons. `Yirmor` is cosmic entropy-as-substance — the slow erosion of pattern — not the final threshold of soul.

## Relations

- [Yirven](yirven.md) is `Yirmor`'s polar counterpart: cosmic surplus against which slow-unmaking is felt as decline.
- [Vatharen](vatharen.md) is the class to which `Yirmor` belongs.
- [Oraemor](oraemor.md) is the moon of threshold; `Yirmor` is the celestial of the long descent, distinct from any specific crossing.

## Note On Form

`Yirmor` blends the `Yir-` family (becoming) with `-mor` (depth, descent), echoing the depth-counterpart pattern of [Seramor](seramor.md) against [Serenel](serenel.md). The slow loss of being is the depth-pole of becoming.
