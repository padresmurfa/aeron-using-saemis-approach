# Athren

- Rendered form: `Athren`
- Gloss: Divergence Keepers; guardians of lawful difference and protected uniqueness
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Athren` names the third-order interactional intelligence that allows asymmetry to persist under interaction without lapsing into ruin or uniformity. It keeps lawful difference alive where binding and transformation might otherwise erase it.

## Scope

`Athren` carries:

- Protected distinction
- Controlled divergence
- Flourishing multiplicity
- Resistance to homogenization

## Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era) Use

In `Mirevar` (the Electroweak Separation Era), the `Athren` preserve uniqueness under interaction and guard the structural conditions from which later individuality can descend.

## Boundaries

`Athren` must not be treated as ego, self-ownership, or soulhood. It is safeguarded difference, not personal identity.

## Relations

- [Athal](athal.md) gives `Athren` structural asymmetry.
- [Miravel](miravel.md) generates the changes whose divergence `Athren` keeps lawful.
- [Sevarn](sevarn.md) is balanced by `Athren` so cohesion does not become uniformity.

## Note On Form

`Athren` is a created-world rendering of an interactional-order intelligence, not an English philosophical abstraction.
