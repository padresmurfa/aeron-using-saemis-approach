# Kethorel

- Rendered form: `Kethorel`
- Gloss: Iron Moon; the oath- and war-metal moon of Aeron
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Kethorel` names the pressure-bearing `Seravel` of Aeron: the moon of iron, oath, burden, and endurance under strain.

## Scope

`Kethorel` carries:

- War-metal gravity
- Oath pressure and judgment
- Endurance under burden
- Buried force awaiting release

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Kethorel` is the earthward secondary `Seravel`, most strongly associated with fortress culture, miners, judges, armorers, and relics long held beneath stone.

## Boundaries

`Kethorel` must not be mistaken for every iron object, brute militarism alone, or a merely dark and heavy moon. It is the canonical primal headword of Aeron's oath-metal moon.

## Relations

- [Seravel](seravel.md) is the broader celestial office to which `Kethorel` belongs.
- [Kethor](kethor.md) gives `Kethorel` its compressed, burden-bearing tone.
- [Ketharen](ketharen.md) helps explain why `Kethorel` is often read in oath-forged resolve, martial centers, and pressure endured rather than escaped.

## Note On Form

`Kethorel` preserves the `kethor` line while taking a more office-bearing lunar close, because compression here is not merely inward stability but visible cyclical judgment over what has been bound.
