# Aenel

- Rendered form: `Aenel`
- Gloss: The Knotted, Binding-Vatharen; the wandering force-bearer of cosmic linkage
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Aenel` names the [Vatharen](vatharen.md) that bears Binding as the cosmic principle of linkage. It is the principle by which separable things are held in relation to one another, made visible as a wandering radiant body in Aeron's sky.

## Scope

`Aenel` carries:

- Cosmic linkage between things otherwise distinct
- The substance of relation across distance
- The web by which kinship, lineage, and consequence travel
- Binding as primal force, not as a personal god of family or oath

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Aenel` is gazed upon by those who study connection: scribes of lineage, mages of sympathetic linkage, diplomats and matchmakers reading cosmic threads of affinity. Its mana-flavor expresses as the felt presence of unseen connection. Its paired counterpart is [Aenor](aenor.md), the Severance-Vatharen.

## Boundaries

`Aenel` must not be confused with [Kethorel](kethorel.md) (oath-bound endurance, which is interpersonal binding under pressure), with [Taelden](taelden.md) (the cosmic principle of rule-coherence — distinct from web-of-relation), or with the lexical-correspondence function of [Taviloren](taviloren.md) (which links word to star, not deed to deed). `Aenel` is cosmic relation-as-substance.

## Relations

- [Aenor](aenor.md) is `Aenel`'s polar counterpart: the cleaving against which linkage is felt as linkage.
- [Vatharen](vatharen.md) is the class to which `Aenel` belongs.
- [Taelden](taelden.md) is the cosmic principle of rule; `Aenel` is the cosmic principle of web. They are distinct: one is law, the other is connection.

## Note On Form

`Aenel` blends the `Aen-` family (linking) with `-el` (knotted), because the word names connection held as a knot — neither rigid nor severed.
