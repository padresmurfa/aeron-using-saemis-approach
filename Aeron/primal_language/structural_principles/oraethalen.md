# Oraethalen

- Rendered form: `Oraethalen`
- Gloss: Skybearers; world-scale envelope keepers of atmosphere and circulation
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Oraethalen` names the twelfth-order world-binding office that arises when `Vathorel` take up the shell-logic of `Oraethen` on planetary scale. It is the principle by which a world bears atmosphere, circulation, and layered exchange between surface and open expanse.

## Scope

`Oraethalen` carries:

- Atmospheric continuity
- Layered circulation
- Shell-bearing protection
- Surface-to-expanse mediation

## Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era) Use

In `Kethralen` (the Planetary Formation Era), the `Oraethalen` sustain skies, regulate circulation, and keep a world's outer envelope from thinning into unprotected exposure.

## Boundaries

`Oraethalen` must not be mistaken for conscious sky-gods, a completed heaven, or already inhabited weather. It is world-envelope stewardship, not a pantheon of personal air and storm.

## Relations

- [Oraethen](oraethen.md) provides the earlier shell-and-exchange lineage that `Oraethalen` scale into atmosphere.
- [Vathorel](vathorel.md) furnish the long-range world-preparing governance from which `Oraethalen` arise.
- [Vathalen](vathalen.md) rely on `Oraethalen` when cradle-conditions require moderated exposure rather than bare openness.

## Note On Form

`Oraethalen` preserves the `oraeth` line while broadening toward a more world-bearing surface, because boundary here becomes borne sky and circulation rather than only local threshold control.
