# Serenel

- Rendered form: `Serenel`
- Gloss: Silver Moon; the bright governing tide-moon of Aeron
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Serenel` names the lucid primary `Seravel` of Aeron: the moon of open tides, visible law, and civic rhythm.

## Scope

`Serenel` carries:

- Open and governing tides
- Visible law and civic trust
- Clear calendrical rhythm
- Graceful return without concealment

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Serenel` is one of the two primary `Seravel` and the brightward half of the world's Water axis.

## Boundaries

`Serenel` must not be reduced to generic silver coloration, a mere mineral comparison, or a poetic nickname detached from celestial office. It is the canonical primal headword of Aeron's bright tide-moon.

## Relations

- [Seravel](seravel.md) is the broader celestial office to which `Serenel` belongs.
- [Seramor](seramor.md) is `Serenel`'s deep-water counterpart in the primary tidal pair.
- [Solaryth](solaryth.md) and [Nyxorys](nyxorys.md) are the two `Taviran` whose mixed light `Serenel` reflects into ordinary rhythm.

## Note On Form

`Serenel` preserves the `seren` line while taking a lucid returning close, because harmony here is not hidden but made public, phase-bearing, and dependable.
