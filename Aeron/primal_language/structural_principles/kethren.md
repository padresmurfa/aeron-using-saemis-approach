# Kethren

- Rendered form: `Kethren`
- Gloss: Domain Thrones; domain-core stabilization and persistence under expansion stress
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Kethren` names the second-order harmonic intelligence that arises when the `Kethar` endure inflationary expansion stress and stabilize large harmonic regions from within.

## Scope

`Kethren` carries:

- Domain-core identity
- Massive structural gravity
- High persistence under widening scale

## Daralon (Inflationary Era) Use

In `Daralon` (the Inflationary Era), the `Kethren` stabilize large harmonic regions and give each domain a durable interior center.

## Boundaries

`Kethren` must not be confused with a later sovereign ruler or free-willed throne. It is a functional intelligence aligned with Loran, not an autonomous monarch.

## Relations

- [Kethar](kethar.md) is the first-order foundation from which `Kethren` arises.
- [Tavral](tavral.md) defines the edge around the domain stabilized by `Kethren`.
- [Seralon](seralon.md) preserves long-range relation between distant domain cores.

## Note On Form

`Kethren` is a created-world rendering of a domain-order intelligence, not an English compound title.
