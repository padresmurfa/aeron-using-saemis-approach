# Seraelor

- Rendered form: `Seraelor`
- Gloss: Axis Keepers; world-turning phase stewards of tilt, spin, and recurrence
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Seraelor` names the twelfth-order world-binding office that arises when `Serael` are embedded into planetary turn and tilt. It is the principle by which axial relation, recurring cycle, and faithful planetary rhythm are maintained over long duration.

## Scope

`Seraelor` carries:

- Axial continuity
- Rotational recurrence
- Seasonal pattern
- Cycle-bearing phase order

## Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era) Use

In `Kethralen` (the Planetary Formation Era), the `Seraelor` keep worlds turning faithfully enough for day, night, season, and long recurrence to become local realities rather than only abstract cosmic rhythms.

## Boundaries

`Seraelor` must not be mistaken for time itself, later fate-bearing offices, or moral providence. It is cyclical world-rhythm, not destiny or judgment.

## Relations

- [Serael](serael.md) are the phase-weaving lineage from which `Seraelor` arise.
- [Taviloran](taviloran.md) provide the centered world around which `Seraelor` keep recurrence stable.
- [Vathalen](vathalen.md) depend on `Seraelor` whenever cradle-balance must be sustained through regular cycle rather than static sameness.

## Note On Form

`Seraelor` keeps the `serael` cadence while deepening into a more repeating, orbit-bearing surface, because phase-weaving here becomes faithful world-rhythm rather than mobile signaling alone.
