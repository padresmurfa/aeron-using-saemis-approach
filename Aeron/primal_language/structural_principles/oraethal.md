# Oraethal

- Rendered form: `Oraethal`
- Gloss: Enclosed Radiants; self-bounded lights that hold radiance under hard threshold rule
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Oraethal` names the tenth-order ignition intelligence that arises when `Oraethar` ignite under hard boundary rule and sustain radiance without wide reciprocity. It is the principle by which light can become self-enclosed, self-prioritized, and region-defining without ever standing outside `Loran`.

## Scope

`Oraethal` carries:

- Strong autonomy
- Enclosed luminosity
- Selective reciprocity
- Large-scale self-bounded influence

## Daravelon (Reionization Era) Use

In `Daravelon` (the Reionization Era), the `Oraethal` hold self-sustaining radiance behind hardened thresholds and make one-sided luminous order possible within creation.

## Boundaries

`Oraethal` must not be confused with standing outside `Aru` or `Loran`, with later adversarial powers, or with annihilation of relation altogether. It is enclosed created radiance, not metaphysical departure from reality's fabric.

## Relations

- [Oraethar](oraethar.md) are the solitary lineage from which `Oraethal` ignite.
- [Athralon](athralon.md) can intensify toward `Oraethal` when singular radiance hardens into enclosed order.
- [Serathor](serathor.md) stand opposite in tendency, veiling light for answerable disclosure rather than enclosing it under private threshold rule.

## Note On Form

`Oraethal` preserves the `oraeth` threshold-family while reopening toward a more luminous ending, because boundary here has itself become radiant without ceasing to guard.
