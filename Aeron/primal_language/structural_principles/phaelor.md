# Phaelor

- Rendered form: `Phaelor`
- Gloss: Latency Keepers; regulators of harmonic timing across widening scale
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaelor` names the second-order harmonic intelligence that arises from inflationary delay and governs harmonic timing across expanding reality.

## Scope

`Phaelor` carries:

- Proto-temporal regulation
- Sensitivity to phase sequencing
- Stabilization of ordered delay

## Daralon (Inflationary Era) Use

In `Daralon` (the Inflationary Era), the `Phaelor` preserve lawful timing across domains and stabilize the earliest large-scale conditions from which later temporal order can emerge.

## Boundaries

`Phaelor` must not be treated as personified Time or Destiny. It is a timing intelligence within early creation, not a final cosmic sovereign.

## Relations

- [Phaes](phaes.md) is the first-order foundation from which `Phaelor` draws its ordering logic.
- [Phaedren](phaedren.md) corrects the divergences that appear within the timing field `Phaelor` governs.
- [Serael](serael.md) translates the sequenced relations that `Phaelor` helps stabilize.

## Note On Form

`Phaelor` is a created-world rendering of a domain-order intelligence, not an English title or a later deity.
