# Aenor

- Rendered form: `Aenor`
- Gloss: The Cut, Severance-Vatharen; the wandering force-bearer of cosmic cleaving
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Aenor` names the [Vatharen](vatharen.md) that bears Severance as the cosmic principle of cleaving. It is the principle by which what was joined is made distinct by separation, made visible as a wandering radiant body in Aeron's sky.

## Scope

`Aenor` carries:

- Cosmic cleaving by which one becomes two
- The substance of distinction-by-separation
- The lawful cut that allows kinds to be themselves
- Severance as primal force, not as a personal god of war or judgment

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Aenor` is gazed upon by those who study distinction: classifiers, edge-mages, surgeons of the cosmic kind, and those who hold that being requires unjoining as much as joining. Its mana-flavor expresses as the felt edge along which one thing ends and another begins. Its paired counterpart is [Aenel](aenel.md), the Binding-Vatharen.

## Boundaries

`Aenor` must not be confused with [Taelmoth](taelmoth.md) (cosmic friction within rule) or with personal gods of war or sacrifice in tier-4 pantheons. `Aenor` is cosmic distinction-by-cut — the lawful cleaving that lets being be plural — not violence and not the failure of law.

## Relations

- [Aenel](aenel.md) is `Aenor`'s polar counterpart: the knotted relation against which the cut is felt as cut.
- [Vatharen](vatharen.md) is the class to which `Aenor` belongs.
- [Taelmoth](taelmoth.md) is friction; `Aenor` is distinction. Friction abrades a single pattern; distinction makes two patterns possible.

## Note On Form

`Aenor` blends the `Aen-` family (linking) with `-or` (carried-apart, borne-away), because the word names the linking root viewed from the other side: where `Aenel` is the knot, `Aenor` is the cut that makes the knot meaningful.
