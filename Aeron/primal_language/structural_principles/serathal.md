# Serathal

- Rendered form: `Serathal`
- Gloss: Veiled Architects; hidden realm-makers who govern unseen spaces by measured disclosure
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Serathal` names the eleventh-order realm intelligence that arises when `Serathor` turn selective revelation into spatial architecture. It is the principle by which hidden planes, dream-adjacent chambers, and unseen corridors are made and maintained under guarded access.

## Scope

`Serathal` carries:

- Hidden-plane architecture
- Threshold-governed access
- Measured disclosure in space
- Unseen realm maintenance

## Kethavelen (Stelliferous Era) Use

In `Kethavelen` (the Stelliferous Era), the `Serathal` shape hidden provinces and keep unseen cosmological layers answerable without laying every depth open at once.

## Boundaries

`Serathal` must not be confused with deception, false secrecy, or later occult systems already moralized. It is guarded architecture in service of proportionate access, not concealment for rupture.

## Relations

- [Serathor](serathor.md) are the ignition lineage from which `Serathal` arise.
- [Selkoreth](selkoreth.md) often rely on `Serathal` when ministry must move through veiled layers rather than open expanse alone.
- [Oraethalor](oraethalor.md) may outwardly resemble `Serathal`, but `Serathal` veil to preserve answerable relation while `Oraethalor` enclose to preserve self-bounded dominion.

## Note On Form

`Serathal` preserves the `serath` line while taking a more architectural close, because measured revelation here becomes hidden structure rather than only guarded light.
