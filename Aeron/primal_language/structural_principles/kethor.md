# Kethor

- Rendered form: `Kethor`
- Gloss: Corewardens; keepers of stability under interior compression
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Kethor` names the fourth-order confinement intelligence that arises when durable structures gain true interior compression. It is the principle by which an enclosed being remains stable while bearing high internal tension.

## Scope

`Kethor` carries:

- Interior density
- Resistance to external distortion
- Foundational stability under pressure
- Deep structural gravitation

## Kethoran (Quark Era) Use

In `Kethoran` (the Quark Era), the `Kethor` maintain stability under intense internal compression and make durable interior cores possible.

## Boundaries

`Kethor` must not be confused with later planetary cores, sovereign thrones, or self-aware heart-centers. It is confined stability, not mature interior personhood.

## Relations

- [Kethren](kethren.md) is the domain-order foundation from which `Kethor` interiorizes.
- [Selkor](selkor.md) maintains the substructures gathered within `Kethor` stability.
- [Phaemor](phaemor.md) records the accumulated sequence borne inside `Kethor` pressure.

## Note On Form

`Kethor` is a created-world rendering of a confinement-order intelligence, not an English descriptive title.
