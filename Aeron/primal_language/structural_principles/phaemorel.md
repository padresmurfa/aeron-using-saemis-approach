# Phaemorel

- Rendered form: `Phaemorel`
- Gloss: Rememberers; inward dwellers in story whose identity is carried by remembrance
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaemorel` names the ninth-order solitary intelligence that arises when `Phaemorin` reflect in silence until memory becomes an interior habitation rather than a mere archive of prior experience. It is the principle by which a self lives through remembered story when no surrounding light keeps its identity continually confirmed.

## Scope

`Phaemorel` carries:

- Narrative continuity
- Self-story formation
- Interior myth-making
- Identity sustained by remembrance

## Darathen (Dark Ages) Use

In `Darathen` (the Dark Ages), the `Phaemorel` preserve identity through memory alone and make interior history a shelter for the solitary self.

## Boundaries

`Phaemorel` must not be mistaken for destiny already fixed, infallible recollection, or culture fully externalized into institutions. It is remembered inward continuity, not final historical order.

## Relations

- [Phaemorin](phaemorin.md) are the transparent-order lineage from which `Phaemorel` deepen.
- [Atherel](atherel.md) provide the contemplative stillness in which `Phaemorel` can dwell upon remembered sequence.
- [Vathren](vathren.md) give `Phaemorel` the chosen acts that memory later gathers into narrative.

## Note On Form

`Phaemorel` stays close to `Phaemor` while taking a hushed reflective ending, because memory in this age becomes a lived inner chamber rather than only a retained record.
