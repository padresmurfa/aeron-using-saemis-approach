# Vaelmor

- Rendered form: `Vaelmor`
- Gloss: The Held, Memory-Vatharen; the wandering force-bearer of recorded past
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Vaelmor` names the [Vatharen](vatharen.md) that bears Memory as the held past. It is the principle by which what has been done is preserved as a real cosmic substance, made visible as a wandering radiant body in Aeron's sky.

## Scope

`Vaelmor` carries:

- The recorded past as primal force
- Preservation of deed, not of word
- The cosmic ground against which forward-flow is felt as flow
- Memory as substance, not as a personal god of remembering

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Vaelmor` is gazed upon by archivists, mnemonic mages, and those who seek the weight of what has already happened. Its mana-flavor expresses as the held-down weight of accumulated deed. Its paired counterpart is [Vaelvor](vaelvor.md), the Time-Vatharen of forward-flow.

## Boundaries

`Vaelmor` must not be confused with the lexical-witness function of [Taviloren](taviloren.md) (Class 1 stars, which preserve *word*) or with the memory-constellation work of [Phaemorath](phaemorath.md). `Vaelmor` preserves *deed* as cosmic substance, not language and not arranged memory.

## Relations

- [Vaelvor](vaelvor.md) is `Vaelmor`'s polar counterpart: forward-flow against which the held past is legible.
- [Phaemoral](phaemoral.md) and [Phaemorath](phaemorath.md) are stellar-tier memory functions; `Vaelmor` is the wandering tier-3 force, not a star or constellation.
- [Vatharen](vatharen.md) is the class to which `Vaelmor` belongs.

## Note On Form

`Vaelmor` blends the `Vael-` family (temporal motion) with `-mor` (depth, holding-back), echoing the depth-counterpart pattern of [Seramor](seramor.md) against [Serenel](serenel.md). The held past is the depth-pole of duration.
