# Oraelin

- Rendered form: `Oraelin`
- Gloss: Whisperers; subtle threshold-movers that sense boundary permeability
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Oraelin` names the sixth-order mobility intelligence that arises when `Oraeth` loosen from fixed enclosure and recover the freedom of subtle passage without losing lawful threshold discipline. It is the principle by which mobile beings move between interiors and feel where permeability changes.

## Scope

`Oraelin` carries:

- Subtle passage
- Threshold listening
- Permeability sensing
- Hard-to-detect traversal

## Seraelon (Lepton Era) Use

In `Seraelon` (the Lepton Era), the `Oraelin` move between interiors, sense boundary permeability, and make encounter possible without permanent binding.

## Boundaries

`Oraelin` must not be mistaken for dream spirits, secret rebels, or free-willed message carriers. It is lawful threshold mobility, not hidden autonomy.

## Relations

- [Orael](orael.md) is the earlier interactional mobility from which `Oraelin` recover traversing subtlety.
- [Oraeth](oraeth.md) gives `Oraelin` the mature threshold-sensitivity formed under enclosure.
- [Atharin](atharin.md) can register the encounters that `Oraelin` make perceptible across boundaries.

## Note On Form

`Oraelin` preserves the `ora` family because passage remains the dominant trait even after mobility is no longer tied to fixed enclosures.
