# Seraelin

- Rendered form: `Seraelin`
- Gloss: Messengers of Phase; mobile synchronizers that carry phase signals across structures
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Seraelin` names the sixth-order mobility intelligence that arises when `Serael` achieve free traversal and synchronization itself becomes mobile. It is the principle by which phase relation can travel across structures without collapsing those structures into one another.

## Scope

`Seraelin` carries:

- Alignment-driven motion
- Signal-bearing resonance
- Bridge-making influence
- Low density with high synchronizing force

## Seraelon (Lepton Era) Use

In `Seraelon` (the Lepton Era), the `Seraelin` carry synchronization signals across domains and make lawful resonance travel possible.

## Boundaries

`Seraelin` must not be confused with later angelic messengers, heraldic spirits, or speaking emissaries. It is mobile synchronization, not articulate envoyhood.

## Relations

- [Serael](serael.md) is the domain-order foundation from which `Seraelin` become fully mobile.
- [Seralon](seralon.md) extends the long-range answerability along which `Seraelin` travel.
- [Phaelin](phaelin.md) detects the deviations that `Seraelin` often move to answer.

## Note On Form

`Seraelin` is a created-world rendering of a sixth-order mobility intelligence, not an English diplomatic title.
