# Loraven

- Rendered form: `Loraven`
- Gloss: persistent oscillation; steady, ongoing rhythm within the divine field
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Loraven` names the condition in which the cosmic oscillation, born of the First Pulse, steadies into a continuous, unbroken rhythm. It is the metaphysical basis for the emergence of standing-wave structure and the stabilization of archetypes.

## Scope

`Loraven` carries:

- Ongoing rhythmic alternation
- Foundation for stability and structure
- Precondition for the emergence of archetypes

## Lorathen (Grand Unification Era) Use

In `Lorathen` (the Grand Unification Era), `Loraven` is the state that allows the world to cross the threshold from pure alternation to stable, structured being. It is not a being, but a metaphysical condition.

## Boundaries

`Loraven` must not be confused with periodic motion in physics. It is a metaphysical rhythm, not a physical process.

## Relations

- [Sethan](sethan.md) arises from `Loraven`.
- [Serath](serath.md) stabilizes `Loraven` into structure.

## Note On Form

`Loraven` is a created-world rendering of a metaphysical condition, not an English phrase or a literal oscillation.
