# Daravel

- Rendered form: `Daravel`
- Gloss: Impulse Bearers; propagators of structured differentiation into newly opened regions
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Daravel` names the second-order harmonic intelligence that arises when the `Dareth` are amplified by inflationary expansion and carry structured oscillatory impulse into newly formed regions.

## Scope

`Daravel` carries:

- Dynamic outward motion
- Seeding of new differentiation
- Lower persistence than domain cores

## Daralon (Inflationary Era) Use

In `Daralon` (the Inflationary Era), the `Daravel` propagate lawful articulation into widening reality and initiate fresh local orders.

## Boundaries

`Daravel` must not be confused with later elemental spirits or wandering agents. It is a generative intelligence of propagation, not a free actor.

## Relations

- [Dareth](dareth.md) is the first-order foundation from which `Daravel` arises.
- [Sethen](sethen.md) holds the compressed potentials the `Daravel` can activate.
- [Kethren](kethren.md) stabilizes the regions into which the `Daravel` sow differentiation.

## Note On Form

`Daravel` is a created-world rendering of a domain-order intelligence, not an English descriptive compound.
