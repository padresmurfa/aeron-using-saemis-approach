# Tavrethen

- Rendered form: `Tavrethen`
- Gloss: Veiled Selves; self-bounded interiors that regulate exchange around an inward blaze
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavrethen` names the eighth-order transparent intelligence that arises when `Tavreth` achieve controlled boundary autonomy and learn to sheath their radiance behind chosen thresholds. It is the principle by which a self can guard, reveal, or withhold its interior fire consciously.

## Scope

`Tavrethen` carries:

- Protective ego-boundary
- Selective permeability
- Conscious regulation of exchange
- Personal interior sovereignty

## Athorael (Recombination Era) Use

In `Athorael` (the Recombination Era), the `Tavrethen` regulate internal and external exchange consciously and make self-sheltering autonomy possible.

## Boundaries

`Tavrethen` must not be confused with tyranny, concealment as evil already chosen, or full refusal enacted against `Loran`. It is self-guarding sovereignty, not rebellion completed.

## Relations

- [Tavreth](tavreth.md) are the radiant-order lineage from which `Tavrethen` concentrate.
- [Atheron](atheron.md) give `Tavrethen` the self-aware center that can choose what to reveal.
- [Oraethor](oraethor.md) represent the sterner threshold-sovereignty toward which `Tavrethen` boundary autonomy can harden.

## Note On Form

`Tavrethen` stays intentionally close to `Tavreth`, because transparent autonomy grows directly from radiant self-sheathing rather than from a detached lineage.
