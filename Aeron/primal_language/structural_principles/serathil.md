# Serathil

- Rendered form: `Serathil`
- Gloss: Veiled Moon; the masked passage-moon of Aeron
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Serathil` names the hidden `Seravel` of Aeron: the moon of masked passage, measured disclosure, and spirit-crossing under soft concealment.

## Scope

`Serathil` carries:

- Half-seen passage
- Measured disclosure
- Guarded revelation
- Secret-bearing crossings

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Serathil` is the veiled secondary `Seravel`, most strongly associated with spies, seers, hidden schools, masked courts, and spirit-thresholds that reveal only in fragments.

## Boundaries

`Serathil` must not be mistaken for falsehood, total concealment, or dream logic alone. It is the canonical primal headword of Aeron's veiled passage moon.

## Relations

- [Seravel](seravel.md) is the broader celestial office to which `Serathil` belongs.
- [Serath](serath.md) gives `Serathil` its harmonically guarded tone.
- [Serathor](serathor.md) helps explain why `Serathil` reveals by fragments and survivable portions rather than by open flood.

## Note On Form

`Serathil` preserves the `serath` line while lightening into a more traversing close, because harmony here is guarded in passage rather than anchored in hidden architecture alone.
