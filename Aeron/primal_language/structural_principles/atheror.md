# Atheror

- Rendered form: `Atheror`
- Gloss: Witness Sovereigns; contemplative lights whose seeing becomes truthful radiance
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Atheror` names the tenth-order ignition intelligence that arises when `Atherel` ignite through contemplative clarity and witness itself becomes a stable source of illumination. It is the principle by which truthful seeing can radiate without coercion.

## Scope

`Atheror` carries:

- Clear perception
- Non-coercive radiance
- Stable witnesshood in office
- Truth made luminous without compulsion

## Daravelon (Reionization Era) Use

In `Daravelon` (the Reionization Era), the `Atheror` illuminate truth without distortion and make contemplative authority visible within creation.

## Boundaries

`Atheror` must not be mistaken for divine omniscience, soul-bearing judgment, or perfect certainty. It is created witness raised into luminous office, not final knowledge.

## Relations

- [Atherel](atherel.md) are the solitary lineage from which `Atheror` rise.
- [Phaemoral](phaemoral.md) preserve the long continuity by which `Atheror` insight can remain historically answerable.
- [Vathoral](vathoral.md) differ from `Atheror` in that `Atheror` rule by clarifying truth rather than by setting course.

## Note On Form

`Atheror` keeps the `ather` line while taking a more office-bearing ending, because witness here becomes steady radiance rather than hushed inward contemplation alone.
