# Miravelen

- Rendered form: `Miravelen`
- Gloss: Basin Keepers; inland-sea and great-basin stewards at the edges of continents
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Miravelen` names the thirteenth-order land-shaping office that arises when `Miravelor` settle into continental margins, rift bowls, and lowland catches. It is the principle by which basins, inland seas, deltas-in-potential, and fertile depressions retain water, sediment, and long environmental memory.

## Scope

`Miravelen` carries:

- Basin depth and retention
- Water-land mediation
- Sediment gathering
- Fertility-bearing lowland preparation

## Athralen (Continental Differentiation Era) Use

In `Athralen` (the Continental Differentiation Era), the `Miravelen` shape inland seas, continental shelves, great depressions, and the low remembered places from which later fertile regions may arise.

## Boundaries

`Miravelen` must not be confused with rivers already carved, living wetlands already inhabited, or agricultural abundance already present. It is basin stewardship before biology, not completed fertile civilization.

## Relations

- [Miravelor](miravelor.md) provide the fluid continuity from which `Miravelen` localize basin memory.
- [Athrenel](athrenel.md) often prepare the rifts and margins in which `Miravelen` endure.
- [Vathalen](vathalen.md) depend on `Miravelen` wherever later cradle-balance will require retained water and sediment rather than bare stone alone.

## Note On Form

`Miravelen` keeps the `miravel` line while softening into a more place-held cadence, because transformative flow here becomes retained basin and remembered depth.
