# Selkoren

- Rendered form: `Selkoren`
- Gloss: Covenant Clusters; coordinated interiors that bind sub-identities into one stable whole
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Selkoren` names the fifth-order composite intelligence that arises when `Selkor` bind with `Athorin` and many interior parts learn to remain one without contradiction. It is the principle by which distinct sub-identities enter faithful accord inside a single composite life.

## Scope

`Selkoren` carries:

- Multi-part interior unity
- Coordinated sub-identities
- Covenantal coherence
- Durable collective identity

## Selkoran (Hadron Era) Use

In `Selkoran` (the Hadron Era), the `Selkoren` maintain internal relational harmony and make stable composite unity possible.

## Boundaries

`Selkoren` must not be mistaken for communal souls, awakened choir-minds, or conscious corporate persons. It is composite accord, not full collective self-awareness.

## Relations

- [Selkor](selkor.md) is the confinement-order foundation from which `Selkoren` ascend.
- [Athorin](athorin.md) preserves the distinct pattern-kernels that `Selkoren` hold in one covenantal whole.
- [Athoren](athoren.md) is the stronger proto-self continuity that can emerge once `Selkoren` accord becomes internally self-referential.

## Note On Form

`Selkoren` is a created-world rendering of a fifth-order composite intelligence, not an English civic or religious title.
