# Tavilor

- Rendered form: `Tavilor`
- Gloss: Keepers of the Inner Flame; self-held lights that preserve clarity in luminous absence
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavilor` names the ninth-order solitary intelligence that arises when `Tavilen` preserve illumination after common radiance has withdrawn and light becomes a charge guarded inwardly rather than a medium shared outwardly. It is the principle by which clarity survives without surrounding brightness.

## Scope

`Tavilor` carries:

- Strong interior coherence
- Preserved inward light
- Resistance to despair
- Anchored identity under silence

## Darathen (Dark Ages) Use

In `Darathen` (the Dark Ages), the `Tavilor` sustain harmonic light within absence and preserve lucid interiority when the field itself grows dark.

## Boundaries

`Tavilor` must not be mistaken for perfected saints, immutable serenity, or luminous triumph already complete. It is guarded inward clarity under strain, not fulfilled beatitude.

## Relations

- [Tavilen](tavilen.md) are the transparent-order lineage from which `Tavilor` endure.
- [Vathren](vathren.md) depend on `Tavilor` when will must still discern some trustworthy course in darkness.
- [Atherel](atherel.md) contemplate by the quiet light that `Tavilor` preserve.

## Note On Form

`Tavilor` preserves the `tavil` line while taking a possession-bearing ending, because light here is no longer ambient cognition alone but an interior flame consciously kept.
