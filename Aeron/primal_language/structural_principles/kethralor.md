# Kethralor

- Rendered form: `Kethralor`
- Gloss: Plate Sovereigns; deep-throned governors of articulated crust and continental drift
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Kethralor` names the thirteenth-order land-shaping office that arises when `Kethaven`, `Vathalen`, and `Tavralen` commit world surfaces to enduring plate identity rather than one unbroken shell. It is the principle by which tectonic plates may move, collide, subduct, and uplift without dissolving planetary continuity.

## Scope

`Kethralor` carries:

- Plate-bearing authority
- Subduction and uplift cycles
- Continental drift
- Long-duration crustal tension management

## Athralen (Continental Differentiation Era) Use

In `Athralen` (the Continental Differentiation Era), the `Kethralor` govern tectonic motion, preserve large crustal identities, and let continents be made by movement rather than by static placement alone.

## Boundaries

`Kethralor` must not be mistaken for awakened continental gods, moral kings of stone, or autonomous earth-wills. It is articulated plate governance inside creation, not sovereign personhood.

## Relations

- [Kethaven](kethaven.md) supply the deep pressure and mantle vitality through which `Kethralor` can move plates without collapse.
- [Vathalen](vathalen.md) require `Kethralor` whenever habitable balance must survive crustal violence across ages.
- [Tavralen](tavralen.md) preserve the guarded distinction by which plate identities can remain coherent rather than melting into one featureless surface.

## Note On Form

`Kethralor` keeps the `kethra` cadence while taking an office-bearing close, because bound place here becomes governing crust rather than only layered world stability.
