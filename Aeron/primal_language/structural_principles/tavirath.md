# Tavirath

- Rendered form: `Tavirath`
- Gloss: Constellations; arranged star-relations readable as higher celestial grammar
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavirath` names constellations as readable arrangements of stars rather than as accidental scatter. It is the principle by which multiple `Taviloren` are held as one intelligible sky-pattern of affinity, tension, compound relation, or lineage.

## Scope

`Tavirath` carries:

- Arranged star-relation
- Readable sky syntax
- Higher-order linkage
- Pattern-bearing orientation

## Planetary Use

In astrology, `Tavirath` are the constellations by which star-words answer each other across larger patterns and become legible as celestial grammar rather than isolated names alone.

## Boundaries

`Tavirath` must not be confused with any merely convenient visual grouping, nor with fate already fixed. It names readable relation in the sky, not automatic destiny.

## Relations

- [Taviloren](taviloren.md) are the stars that `Tavirath` arrange into intelligible patterns.
- [Taviron](taviron.md) is the deeper luminous prototype of large-scale answerability from which constellation-like relation becomes possible.
- [Phaemorath](phaemorath.md) preserve memory across constellary and realm-scale arrangement.

## Note On Form

`Tavirath` preserves the `tavir` line while thickening toward a more architectonic cadence, because relation here is no longer only linkage but arranged pattern held as readable sky structure.
