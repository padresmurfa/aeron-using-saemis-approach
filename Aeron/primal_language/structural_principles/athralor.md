# Athralor

- Rendered form: `Athralor`
- Gloss: Divergent Lords; singular realm-bearers whose self-defined radiance consolidates into enduring courts
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Athralor` names the eleventh-order realm intelligence that arises when `Athralon` bind their self-chosen radiance into lasting realms and polities. It is the principle by which created variance becomes institutional, court-bearing, and region-shaping across long duration.

## Scope

`Athralor` carries:

- Singular realm identity
- Institutionalized asymmetry
- Strong autonomous proportion
- Long-duration difficult answerability

## Kethavelen (Stelliferous Era) Use

In `Kethavelen` (the Stelliferous Era), the `Athralor` establish enduring singular courts and make unusual or asymmetric provinces possible at realm scale.

## Boundaries

`Athralor` must not be mistaken for later soul-bearing antagonists, any capacity to stand outside `Aru` or `Loran`, or completed ethical division. It is persistent created variance organized into realm-form, not metaphysical departure.

## Relations

- [Athralon](athralon.md) are the ignition lineage from which `Athralor` arise.
- [Oraethalor](oraethalor.md) can intensify the closed-boundary tendency inside `Athralor` when singular realmhood hardens toward severe threshold rule.
- [Tavilorath](tavilorath.md) make the enduring luminous centers against which `Athralor` singularity becomes regionally distinct.

## Note On Form

`Athralor` keeps the `athral` line while taking a more office-bearing realm cadence, because divergence here no longer only radiates but governs.
