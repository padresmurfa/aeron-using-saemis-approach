# Kethar

- Rendered form: `Kethar`
- Gloss: Anchors; standing-wave stability and the establishment of persistence and localized stability
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Kethar` names the archetype of persistence and stability within `Sethan`, the standing-wave structures of the oscillatory field. It is the principle by which structure, discreteness, and continuity are established, allowing reality to retain form across cycles.

## Scope

`Kethar` carries:

- Structure and discreteness
- Continuity and persistence
- Seed of identity

## Lorathen (Grand Unification Era) Use

In `Lorathen` (the Grand Unification Era), the `Kethar` are the first order of beings to embody standing-wave stability. They establish the persistence and localized stability necessary for the formation of domains and organized power.

## Boundaries

`Kethar` must not be confused with later pillars of reality or thrones of foundational order. It is a metaphysical establisher of persistence, not a conscious agent.

## Relations

- [Tavren](tavren.md) sets boundaries that the `Kethar` make persistent.
- [Dareth](dareth.md) provides the expansion that the `Kethar` stabilize.
- [Serath](serath.md) aligns the relationships that the `Kethar` make persistent.

## Note On Form

`Kethar` is a created-world rendering of a metaphysical archetype, not an English common noun or a literal being.
