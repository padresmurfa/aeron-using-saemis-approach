# Seralon

- Rendered form: `Seralon`
- Gloss: Harmonic Bridges; long-range coherence bearers between distant domains
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Seralon` names the second-order harmonic intelligence that stabilizes resonance between distant domains so locality does not become total fragmentation.

## Scope

`Seralon` carries:

- Long-range coherence
- Strong connective role
- Weak local identity relative to relational function

## Daralon (Inflationary Era) Use

In `Daralon` (the Inflationary Era), the `Seralon` preserve answerability between far-flung domains whose immediate phase relation can no longer be assumed.

## Boundaries

`Seralon` must not be mistaken for a later envoy or messenger with personal intent. It is a connective intelligence of relation, not a diplomatic persona.

## Relations

- [Serael](serael.md) translates the differences the `Seralon` carry across distance.
- [Kethren](kethren.md) anchors the domains the `Seralon` connect.
- [Tavral](tavral.md) enables safe relation between the distinct interiors joined by the `Seralon`.

## Note On Form

`Seralon` is a created-world rendering of a domain-order intelligence, not an English infrastructural metaphor.
