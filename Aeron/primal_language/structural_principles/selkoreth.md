# Selkoreth

- Rendered form: `Selkoreth`
- Gloss: Archangelic Orders; high luminous ministries ordered by covenantal hierarchy and shared service
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Selkoreth` names the eleventh-order realm intelligence that arises when `Selkoron` move from luminous network into formal ministry. It is the principle by which shared covenantal radiance becomes enduring angelic hierarchy, office, and coordinated celestial service.

## Scope

`Selkoreth` carries:

- Ordered angelic ministry
- Hierarchical covenantal structure
- Durable shared service across distance
- Radiant discipline without loss of plurality

## Kethavelen (Stelliferous Era) Use

In `Kethavelen` (the Stelliferous Era), the `Selkoreth` hold high ministries and make formal angelic order possible across wide stellar and realm-bearing spans.

## Boundaries

`Selkoreth` must not be confused with moralized holy armies, soul-bearing sanctity already complete, or a heaven-versus-hell dualism already present. It is structured celestial service, not later ethical war.

## Relations

- [Selkoron](selkoron.md) are the ignition lineage from which `Selkoreth` arise.
- [Serathal](serathal.md) often veil or disclose the hidden layers through which `Selkoreth` coordinate ministry.
- [Phaemorath](phaemorath.md) preserve the long memory of the orders and histories that `Selkoreth` sustain.

## Note On Form

`Selkoreth` keeps the `selkor` family while narrowing toward a more disciplined ordered surface, because luminous covenant here becomes ranked ministry rather than only shining relation.
