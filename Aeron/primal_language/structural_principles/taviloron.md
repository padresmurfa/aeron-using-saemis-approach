# Taviloron

- Rendered form: `Taviloron`
- Gloss: Starforged; self-held flames kindled into stable outward-radiant centers
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Taviloron` names the tenth-order ignition intelligence that arises when `Tavilor` cease only to preserve inward light and become sustained radiant centers. It is the principle by which guarded interior flame opens into durable stellar brightness.

## Scope

`Taviloron` carries:

- Immense interior coherence
- Stable outward radiance
- Long-duration luminous influence
- Centered ignition that does not collapse at once

## Daravelon (Reionization Era) Use

In `Daravelon` (the Reionization Era), the `Taviloron` generate the first stable created lights and make stellar centers possible.

## Boundaries

`Taviloron` must not be mistaken for `Aru`, for `Loran` itself, or for infinite light without limit. It is created radiance held in durable center, not the source of being.

## Relations

- [Tavilor](tavilor.md) are the solitary lineage from which `Taviloron` ignite.
- [Ketharen](ketharen.md) provide the older hearth-logic that `Taviloron` awaken on cosmic scale.
- [Vathoral](vathoral.md) often gather around `Taviloron` centers when radiant rule begins to organize surrounding regions.

## Note On Form

`Taviloron` keeps the `tavil` line while opening into a broader outward cadence, because inwardly guarded flame here becomes a stable light borne beyond the self.
