# Taelmoth

- Rendered form: `Taelmoth`
- Gloss: The Friction, Strife-Vatharen; the wandering force-bearer of cosmic discord
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Taelmoth` names the [Vatharen](vatharen.md) that bears Strife as the cosmic principle of friction. It is the principle by which change is driven by tension between rule and rupture, made visible as a wandering radiant body in Aeron's sky.

## Scope

`Taelmoth` carries:

- Cosmic friction
- The tension by which lawful pattern is contested and reformed
- The substance of broken-out-of-pattern
- Strife as primal force, not as a personal god of war

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Taelmoth` is gazed upon by those who attend to discord as a creative force: insurrectionists, reformers, mages of disruption, and those who would understand why coherent things break. Its mana-flavor expresses as the felt agitation of pattern under stress. Its paired counterpart is [Taelden](taelden.md), the Order-Vatharen.

## Boundaries

`Taelmoth` must not be confused with [Tavrekal](tavrekal.md) (the moon of tempering pressure under siege and forge, which is interpersonal endurance) or with personal gods of war in tier-4 pantheons. `Taelmoth` is cosmic friction-as-substance, not battle-prowess and not chaos as the absence of law. It is the necessary stress within law.

## Relations

- [Taelden](taelden.md) is `Taelmoth`'s polar counterpart: rule-coherence against which friction is felt as friction.
- [Vatharen](vatharen.md) is the class to which `Taelmoth` belongs.
- [Tavrekal](tavrekal.md) carries tempering pressure at the moon-tier; `Taelmoth` carries the cosmic principle from which all friction is possible.

## Note On Form

`Taelmoth` blends the `Tael-` family (rule-binding) with `-moth` (broken-out-of), because the word names cosmic substance escaping its own pattern. The pair `Taelden`/`Taelmoth` mirrors the held-versus-broken polarity within a single root.
