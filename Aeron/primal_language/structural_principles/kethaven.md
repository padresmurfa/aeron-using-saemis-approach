# Kethaven

- Rendered form: `Kethaven`
- Gloss: Mantle Wardens; deep keepers of fire and stone under planetary pressure
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Kethaven` names the twelfth-order world-binding office that arises when `Kethavelor` bind elemental fire and stone into lasting interior depth. It is the principle by which a forming world keeps pressure, mantle, and heat in slow balance.

## Scope

`Kethaven` carries:

- Deep interior order
- Pressure-bearing continuity
- Tectonic restraint
- Fire-stone balance

## Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era) Use

In `Kethralen` (the Planetary Formation Era), the `Kethaven` maintain interior heat, regulate deep pressure, and keep planetary depth from either collapse or premature rupture.

## Boundaries

`Kethaven` must not be confused with a conscious world-heart, a living underworld, or any later chthonic pantheon. It is deep structural stewardship, not awakened subterranean personhood.

## Relations

- [Kethavelor](kethavelor.md) are the elemental lineage from which `Kethaven` arise.
- [Tavrekor](tavrekor.md) keep the regulated ember seated within `Kethaven` depth.
- [Taviloran](taviloran.md) provide the local central hold around which `Kethaven` order deep interior mass.

## Note On Form

`Kethaven` preserves the `kethav` cadence while easing into a slower, more layer-bearing ending, because shaping fire here becomes warded interior depth rather than open elemental province.
