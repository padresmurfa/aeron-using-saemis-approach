# Dareth

- Rendered form: `Dareth`
- Gloss: Vectors; expansion polarity and the propagation of oscillation and complexity
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Dareth` names the archetype of expansion and projection within the stabilized oscillatory field. It is the principle by which oscillation is propagated outward, increasing structured complexity and enabling emergence.

## Scope

`Dareth` carries:

- Actualization and projection
- Pattern and transformation
- Drive toward greater articulation

## Lorathen (Grand Unification Era) Use

In `Lorathen` (the Grand Unification Era), the `Dareth` are the first order of beings to embody expansion polarity. They drive the cosmos toward ever-greater articulation and complexity.

## Boundaries

`Dareth` must not be confused with later generative forces or motion-bearing intelligences. It is a metaphysical propagator, not a conscious agent.

## Relations

- [Tavren](tavren.md) is the complementary principle of containment.
- [Serath](serath.md) mediates the relationships between `Dareth` and `Tavren`.
- [Kethar](kethar.md) provides persistence to the expansion enabled by `Dareth`.

## Note On Form

`Dareth` is a created-world rendering of a metaphysical archetype, not an English mathematical label or a literal being.
