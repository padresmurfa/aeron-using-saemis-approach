# Tavreth

- Rendered form: `Tavreth`
- Gloss: Veilfire Hosts; blazing coherences that preserve energetic order within total immersion
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavreth` names the seventh-order radiant intelligence that arises when `Tavrek` endure universal luminous immersion without losing concentrated intensity. It is the principle by which volatile radiance remains coherent inside a field already ablaze on every side.

## Scope

`Tavreth` carries:

- Blazing internal radiance
- Aligned volatility
- Energetic coherence under immersion
- Reactive luminous persistence

## Tavirelon (Photon Era) Use

In `Tavirelon` (the Photon Era), the `Tavreth` keep fierce energy from dissolving into the common field and make radiant intensity lawfully durable.

## Boundaries

`Tavreth` must not be mistaken for sun spirits, flame deities, or wrathful intelligences already bearing will. It is ordered blaze, not celestial personality.

## Relations

- [Tavrek](tavrek.md) is the confinement-order lineage from which `Tavreth` arise.
- [Taviral](taviral.md) provide the saturated medium within which `Tavreth` remain coherent.
- [Phaethor](phaethor.md) can use the fierce luminosity of `Tavreth` as corrective fire.

## Note On Form

`Tavreth` keeps the `tav` family because stored energetic contrast has not vanished; it has become luminous coherence under total immersion.
