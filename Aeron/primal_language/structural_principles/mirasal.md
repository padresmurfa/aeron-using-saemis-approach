# Mirasal

- Rendered form: `Mirasal`
- Gloss: Echo Realms; memory-fields preserving layered harmonic traces across domains
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Mirasal` names the seventh-order radiant intelligence that arises when `Mirasin` no longer remain only drifting residues but deepen into persistent memory-fields inside saturated radiance. It is the principle by which remembrance becomes environmental without yet becoming a society of surviving individuals.

## Scope

`Mirasal` carries:

- Memory-field persistence
- Layered identity traces
- Non-individual remembrance
- Harmonic depth across domains

## Tavirelon (Photon Era) Use

In `Tavirelon` (the Photon Era), the `Mirasal` preserve harmonic memory across domains and make the world's past linger as field rather than as isolated residue.

## Boundaries

`Mirasal` must not be mistaken for ancestral planes already populated by discrete selves, conscious ghosts, or collective afterlives complete. It is remembrance diffused through radiance, not personal survival fulfilled.

## Relations

- [Mirasin](mirasin.md) are the mobility-order lineage from which `Mirasal` deepen.
- [Selkoral](selkoral.md) give `Mirasal` a collective field within which traces can layer.
- [Atharon](atharon.md) can witness the memory-depths that `Mirasal` preserve.

## Note On Form

`Mirasal` preserves the `mir` family because an echo-field remains a transformed remainder of relation, even when it has widened into a realm-like persistence.
