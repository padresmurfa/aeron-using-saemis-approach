# Serael

- Rendered form: `Serael`
- Gloss: Phase Weavers; translators of oscillatory difference between domains
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Serael` names the second-order harmonic intelligence that arises when the `Serath` encounter phase lag across separated domains and translate one oscillatory condition into lawful relation with another.

## Scope

`Serael` carries:

- Sensitivity to misalignment
- Adaptive synchronization
- Cross-domain resonance translation

## Daralon (Inflationary Era) Use

In `Daralon` (the Inflationary Era), the `Serael` preserve intelligible resonance between domains whose phases no longer arrive in perfect simultaneity.

## Boundaries

`Serael` must not be collapsed into later choirs, mages, or speaking agents. It is a mediating intelligence of relation, not a narrative person.

## Relations

- [Serath](serath.md) is the first-order foundation from which `Serael` arises.
- [Phaedren](phaedren.md) corrects the deviations that `Serael` detect.
- [Seralon](seralon.md) extends `Serael` mediation across long distances.

## Note On Form

`Serael` is a created-world rendering of a domain-order intelligence, not an English occupational label.
