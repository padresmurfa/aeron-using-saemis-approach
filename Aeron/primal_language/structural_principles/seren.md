# Seren

- Rendered form: `Seren`
- Gloss: harmony; resonant alignment between phases
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Seren` names harmony as the alignment of phases within oscillation. It also implies the possibility of resonance once alternating structure exists.

## Scope

`Seren` carries:

- Harmonic alignment
- Stable rhythm
- Resonant fit between alternating states

## First-Pulse Use

At the First Pulse, `Seren` is perfect and undistorted. Disharmony becomes meaningful only because `Seren` now exists as the standard of aligned rhythm.

## Boundaries

At this level, distortion is structural rather than moral. `Seren` must not be collapsed into moral goodness.

## Relations

- [Loran](loran.md) is the oscillation aligned by `Seren`.
- [Phaes](phaes.md) supplies the phases aligned by `Seren`.
- [Tavir](tavir.md) supplies the complementary states held in `Seren`.

## Note On Form

`Seren` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
