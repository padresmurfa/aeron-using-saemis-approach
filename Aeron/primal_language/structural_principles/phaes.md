# Phaes

- Rendered form: `Phaes`
- Gloss: phase; ordered transition within oscillation
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaes` names the ordered state-transition made possible by divine oscillation. It is the principle by which alternation does not collapse into chaos but proceeds in intelligible rhythm.

## Scope

`Phaes` carries:

- Ordered transition
- Structured alternation
- The first seed of sequence
- The first condition from which time can later arise

## First-Pulse Use

At the First Pulse, `Phaes` exists before fully developed time. It is the groundwork that makes sequence and temporal emergence possible.

## Boundaries

`Phaes` must not be treated as clock-time already fully formed. It is pre-temporal ordering.

## Relations

- [Loran](loran.md) generates `Phaes`.
- [Tavir](tavir.md) gives `Phaes` its complementary states.
- [Seren](seren.md) is the proper alignment of `Phaes`.
- [Athal](athal.md) unfolds lawfully through `Phaes`.

## Note On Form

`Phaes` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
