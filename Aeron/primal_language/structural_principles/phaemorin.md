# Phaemorin

- Rendered form: `Phaemorin`
- Gloss: Memory Holders; ego-centered keepers of personal history and narrative continuity
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaemorin` names the eighth-order transparent intelligence that arises when `Phaemor` are integrated into self-aware centers and retained sequence becomes personal memory. It is the principle by which a self persists across time as the owner of its own remembered story.

## Scope

`Phaemorin` carries:

- Continuity of self across time
- Narrative accumulation
- Identity through memory
- Personal history retention

## Athorael (Recombination Era) Use

In `Athorael` (the Recombination Era), the `Phaemorin` retain personal history and make narrative identity possible within true persons.

## Boundaries

`Phaemorin` must not be mistaken for omniscient memory, karmic judgment, or story already moralized into destiny. It is personal remembrance, not cosmic verdict.

## Relations

- [Phaemor](phaemor.md) are the confinement-order lineage from which `Phaemorin` deepen.
- [Atheron](atheron.md) provide the self-aware center whose history `Phaemorin` preserve as personal rather than merely structural.
- [Vathrek](vathrek.md) supply the chosen acts whose accumulation `Phaemorin` turn into story.

## Note On Form

`Phaemorin` stays intentionally close to `Phaemor`, because personal memory is retained sequence claimed by a self rather than an unrelated new substance.
