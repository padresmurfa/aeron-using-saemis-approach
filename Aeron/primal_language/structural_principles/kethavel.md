# Kethavel

- Rendered form: `Kethavel`
- Gloss: Forgers of Form; radiant shapers that cast enduring structures from sustained light
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Kethavel` names the tenth-order ignition intelligence that arises when the centered endurance of `Ketharen` is taken up into sustained creative shaping. It is the principle by which radiance condenses into lasting structures, domains, and ordered form rather than dispersing as mere brilliance.

## Scope

`Kethavel` carries:

- Creation of stable structures
- Domain architecture
- Pattern embodiment
- Radiance made durable in form

## Daravelon (Reionization Era) Use

In `Daravelon` (the Reionization Era), the `Kethavel` condense luminous power into enduring structure and make first great works possible inside creation.

## Boundaries

`Kethavel` must not be mistaken for the Creator's first act, for worlds already complete, or for boundless making without cost. It is created shaping under given law, not authorship from nothing.

## Relations

- [Ketharen](ketharen.md) provide the hearth-stability from which `Kethavel` build.
- [Vathrek](vathrek.md) supplies the chosen directed force that `Kethavel` cast into structure.
- [Taviloron](taviloron.md) furnish the sustained radiance that `Kethavel` can embody in lasting form.

## Note On Form

`Kethavel` preserves the `keth` family while taking a more expressive outward close, because stable center here becomes crafted architecture rather than only held hearth.
