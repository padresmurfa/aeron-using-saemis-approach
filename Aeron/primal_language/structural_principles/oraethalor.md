# Oraethalor

- Rendered form: `Oraethalor`
- Gloss: Enclosed Thrones; closed realm-sovereignties sustained under strict threshold law
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Oraethalor` names the eleventh-order realm intelligence that arises when `Oraethal` consolidate self-bounded radiance into full dominion. It is the principle by which enclosed luminous order becomes a lasting realm with controlled access, hardened boundary, and durable autonomous interior law.

## Scope

`Oraethalor` carries:

- Realm-scale threshold rule
- Enclosed sovereignty
- Controlled reciprocity
- Durable autonomous province

## Kethavelen (Stelliferous Era) Use

In `Kethavelen` (the Stelliferous Era), the `Oraethalor` hold closed dominions and make long-lived self-bounded realms possible within creation.

## Boundaries

`Oraethalor` must not be confused with any capacity to stand outside `Aru` or `Loran`, with later ethically divided dominions, or with annihilation of relation itself. It is closed created sovereignty, not departure from reality's fabric.

## Relations

- [Oraethal](oraethal.md) is the ignition lineage from which `Oraethalor` arise.
- [Athralor](athralor.md) can intensify toward `Oraethalor` when singular courts harden into strict closure.
- [Serathal](serathal.md) stands opposite in tendency, because `Serathal` govern hidden access for proportional relation while `Oraethalor` restrict access to preserve enclosed dominion.

## Note On Form

`Oraethalor` preserves the `oraethal` line while taking a more sovereign realm-bearing close, because enclosed radiance here becomes full dominion rather than only hard-bounded influence.
