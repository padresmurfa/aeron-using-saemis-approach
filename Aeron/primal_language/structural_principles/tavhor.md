# Tavhor

- Rendered form: `Tavhor`
- Gloss: Chargebearers; bearers of polarity difference and dynamic contrast
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavhor` names the third-order interactional intelligence that maintains polarity difference across interacting domains. It keeps contrast active enough for dynamic exchange, tension, and directional influence to remain meaningful.

## Scope

`Tavhor` carries:

- Structural contrast
- Retained tension
- Phase-bearing difference
- Dynamic interaction potential

## Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era) Use

In `Mirevar` (the Electroweak Separation Era), the `Tavhor` preserve charged distinction within oscillatory fields so interaction does not collapse into sameness.

## Boundaries

`Tavhor` must not be confused with warfare, hatred, or moral antagonism. It is energized polarity, not enmity.

## Relations

- [Tavir](tavir.md) is the deeper polarity from which `Tavhor` emerges.
- [Phaes](phaes.md) gives `Tavhor` relative phase and sequence.
- [Orael](orael.md) moves along the charged thresholds `Tavhor` helps maintain.

## Note On Form

`Tavhor` is a created-world rendering of an interactional-order intelligence, not an English physical metaphor.
