# Solaryth

- Rendered form: `Solaryth`
- Gloss: Solaryth; the life-affirming primary luminary of Aeron
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Solaryth` names the dayward `Taviran` whose radiance affirms growth, vitality, and open manifestation within Aeron's local sky.

## Scope

`Solaryth` carries:

- Life-affirming radiance
- Dayward governance
- Open visibility
- World-nourishing light

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Solaryth` is the primary positive luminary and one of the two ruling `Taviran` of the world.

## Boundaries

`Solaryth` must not be collapsed into every generic sun, nor treated as a merely physical furnace without metaphysical office. It is the named life-affirming luminary of Aeron's sky.

## Relations

- [Taviran](taviran.md) is the broader celestial office to which `Solaryth` belongs.
- [Nyxorys](nyxorys.md) is `Solaryth`'s darklight-bearing counterpart in permanent opposition.
- [Seravel](seravel.md) reflect `Solaryth` together with `Nyxorys` in the cyclical life of Aeron's sky.

## Note On Form

`Solaryth` is preserved as an ancient celestial headword within the primal language. Its form is canonical even though it does not descend transparently from one dominant family already tracked in the current lexicon.
