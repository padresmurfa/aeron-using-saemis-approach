# Phaedren

- Rendered form: `Phaedren`
- Gloss: Drift Wardens; precision correctors of accumulated phase delay
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Phaedren` names the second-order harmonic intelligence that emerges to monitor and correct the cumulative phase drift introduced by inflationary scale.

## Scope

`Phaedren` carries:

- Subtle corrective influence
- Precision alignment
- Prevention of harmonic divergence

## Daralon (Inflationary Era) Use

In `Daralon` (the Inflationary Era), the `Phaedren` keep domain-separated reality from sliding from lawful delay into damaging divergence.

## Boundaries

`Phaedren` must not be mistaken for a moral censor or an autonomous arbiter of fate. It is a corrective intelligence that serves harmonic integrity.

## Relations

- [Serael](serael.md) reveals the differences the `Phaedren` must correct.
- [Phaelor](phaelor.md) governs the timing conditions under which drift accumulates.
- [Tavral](tavral.md) preserves the domain edge that the `Phaedren` keep coherent.

## Note On Form

`Phaedren` is a created-world rendering of a domain-order intelligence, not an English official title.
