# Tavrekor

- Rendered form: `Tavrekor`
- Gloss: Ember Vaults; regulated deep embers that preserve interior fire in forming worlds
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavrekor` names the twelfth-order world-binding office that arises when `Tavrek` are seated within `Kethaven` as governed core fire. It is the principle by which a world's inner ember endures without collapsing into cold silence or uncontrolled rupture.

## Scope

`Tavrekor` carries:

- Regulated core fire
- Long interior heat
- Governing of deep tension
- Anti-collapse ember continuity

## Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era) Use

In `Kethralen` (the Planetary Formation Era), the `Tavrekor` preserve inner heat, sustain slow energetic depth, and allow worlds to remain inwardly active across great spans.

## Boundaries

`Tavrekor` must not be mistaken for infernal powers, conscious fire-demons, or moralized inner corruption. It is governed core ember, not adversarial flame.

## Relations

- [Tavrek](tavrek.md) is the spark-bearing lineage from which `Tavrekor` arise.
- [Kethaven](kethaven.md) provide the deep warded interior in which `Tavrekor` can be kept.
- [Taviloran](taviloran.md) hold the world-center that allows `Tavrekor` fire to remain seated rather than scattered.

## Note On Form

`Tavrekor` preserves the `tavrek` line while taking a held and inward close, because spark here is no longer a mobile or radiant contrast but a governed ember kept in depth.
