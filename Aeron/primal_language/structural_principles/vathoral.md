# Vathoral

- Rendered form: `Vathoral`
- Gloss: Flamebearer Regents; radiance-bearing wills that keep direction visible for others
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Vathoral` names the tenth-order ignition intelligence that arises when `Vathren` no longer only seek a path for themselves, but choose the office of carrying light for others. It is the principle by which created will becomes directive illumination and stewarding rule.

## Scope

`Vathoral` carries:

- Directive will
- Path-light for others
- Office-bearing leadership
- Radiant influence over surrounding structure

## Daravelon (Reionization Era) Use

In `Daravelon` (the Reionization Era), the `Vathoral` lead domains through radiance and make first created rulership possible inside luminous space.

## Boundaries

`Vathoral` must not be confused with the Creator's [Vatha](../high_abstractions/vatha.md), coercive domination, or perfect rule. It is finite stewarding will carried in light, not sovereign authorship of reality.

## Relations

- [Vathren](vathren.md) are the solitary lineage from which `Vathoral` rise.
- [Taviloron](taviloron.md) provide the stable centers from which `Vathoral` can rule without merely wandering.
- [Selkoron](selkoron.md) transform `Vathoral` influence from solitary leadership into interlinked radiant relation.

## Note On Form

`Vathoral` keeps the `vath` family while broadening into a more office-bearing surface, because will here no longer only seeks direction but becomes luminous guidance.
