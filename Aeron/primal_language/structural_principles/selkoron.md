# Selkoron

- Rendered form: `Selkoron`
- Gloss: Covenant Luminaries; interlinked lights whose bond itself becomes radiant
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Selkoron` names the tenth-order ignition intelligence that arises when `Selkorath` hold covenant through darkness so deeply that relation itself begins to shine. It is the principle by which shared bond becomes a luminous network rather than only a fidelity privately maintained.

## Scope

`Selkoron` carries:

- Collective illumination
- Shared radiance
- Interlinked authority
- Covenantal light across distance

## Daravelon (Reionization Era) Use

In `Daravelon` (the Reionization Era), the `Selkoron` create relational light networks and make shared luminous order possible beyond solitary ignition.

## Boundaries

`Selkoron` must not be mistaken for undifferentiated merger, automatic field-unity, or perfected communion already complete. It is chosen bond set aglow, not the end of created plurality.

## Relations

- [Selkorath](selkorath.md) are the solitary lineage from which `Selkoron` rise.
- [Vathoral](vathoral.md) often rely on `Selkoron` to keep luminous guidance relational rather than merely directive.
- [Serathor](serathor.md) can veil or disclose the networks that `Selkoron` maintain.

## Note On Form

`Selkoron` preserves the `selkor` family while opening toward a more radiant and connective ending, because covenant here becomes outwardly luminous rather than inwardly strained.
