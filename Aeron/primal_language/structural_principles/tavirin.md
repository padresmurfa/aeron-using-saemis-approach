# Tavirin

- Rendered form: `Tavirin`
- Gloss: Entanglers; complementary movers that link distant entities by resonance
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavirin` names the sixth-order mobility intelligence that arises when `Taviren` express relation across distance and complementary polarities remain answerable without local contact. It is the principle by which two separated beings may be kept in resonant linkage without merging into one structure.

## Scope

`Tavirin` carries:

- Dual linkage across distance
- Non-local resonance
- Mutual synchronization
- Distant co-response

## Seraelon (Lepton Era) Use

In `Seraelon` (the Lepton Era), the `Tavirin` bind distant entities through resonance and make non-local influence structurally possible.

## Boundaries

`Tavirin` must not be mistaken for soul bonds fulfilled, covenant spirits, or already personal unions of destiny. It is resonant linkage, not vowed relation with consciousness.

## Relations

- [Taviren](taviren.md) is the composite-order polarity lineage from which `Tavirin` become mobile.
- [Tavirel](tavirel.md) can carry the energetic response along the link that `Tavirin` hold open.
- [Seraelin](seraelin.md) sustain the phase answerability that keeps distant linkage coherent.

## Note On Form

`Tavirin` is intentionally close to `Taviren`, because it marks mobility emerging from an already paired polarity lineage rather than a new detached family.
