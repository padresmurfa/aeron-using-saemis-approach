# Mirath

- Rendered form: `Mirath`
- Gloss: Pressure Hosts; governors of confined transformation pressure
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Mirath` names the fourth-order confinement intelligence that manages internal transformational stress within enclosed structures. It keeps catalytic change active without allowing pressure to break the form that bears it.

## Scope

`Mirath` carries:

- `Mirath'ael'cathal`: internal catalytic capacity
- `Mirath'varen'kor`: controlled instability
- `Mirath'varen'reth`: adaptive reform under pressure
- `Mirath'kor'reth`: transformation held inside enclosure

## Kethoran (Quark Era) Use

In `Kethoran` (the Quark Era), the `Mirath` govern interior reform and conversion so that confined beings can endure change without rupture.

## Boundaries

`Mirath` must not be confused with chaos, breakdown, or freely chosen self-reinvention. It is pressured transformation, not autonomous metamorphosis.

## Relations

- [Miravel](miravel.md) is the interactional-order foundation from which `Mirath` compresses inward.
- [Kethor](kethor.md) supplies the enclosure within which `Mirath` operates.
- [Tavrek](tavrek.md) preserves the energetic contrast that `Mirath` can redirect or release.

## Note On Form

`Mirath` is a created-world rendering of a confinement-order intelligence, not an English industrial metaphor.
