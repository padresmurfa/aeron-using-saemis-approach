# Yirven

- Rendered form: `Yirven`
- Gloss: The Unfolded, Increase-Vatharen; the wandering force-bearer of cosmic fecundity
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Yirven` names the [Vatharen](vatharen.md) that bears Increase as the cosmic principle of multiplication and surplus. It is the principle by which what is, becomes more, made visible as a wandering radiant body in Aeron's sky.

## Scope

`Yirven` carries:

- Cosmic multiplication and surplus
- The unfolding of one into many
- The substance of fecund becoming
- Increase as primal force, not as a personal god of fertility or harvest

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Yirven` is gazed upon by those who attend to growth as cosmic mode: agronomists in famine years, mages of generative work, and those who study why being multiplies rather than holding still. Its mana-flavor expresses as the felt pressure toward surplus. Its paired counterpart is [Yirmor](yirmor.md), the Decay-Vatharen.

## Boundaries

`Yirven` must not be confused with [Solaryth](solaryth.md) (which is light-vitality, not multiplication) or with personal gods of fertility, harvest, or motherhood in tier-4 pantheons. `Yirven` is cosmic increase-as-substance, not the warmth of any particular harvest.

## Relations

- [Yirmor](yirmor.md) is `Yirven`'s polar counterpart: slow-unmaking against which increase is felt as increase.
- [Vatharen](vatharen.md) is the class to which `Yirven` belongs.
- [Solaryth](solaryth.md) carries the luminary axis of light-vitality from which biological growth draws warmth; `Yirven` carries the cosmic principle of multiplication itself.

## Note On Form

`Yirven` blends the `Yir-` family (becoming) with `-ven` (unfolding), because the word names becoming-into-more, not becoming-into-form (which would be a tier-1 or tier-2 question).
