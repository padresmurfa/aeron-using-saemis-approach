# Sethen

- Rendered form: `Sethen`
- Gloss: Domain Embers; compressed remnants of standing-wave intensity with generative potential
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Sethen` names the second-order harmonic intelligence that arises as a localized remnant of `Sethan` after inflationary expansion, holding concentrated harmonic density for future formation.

## Scope

`Sethen` carries:

- Small but intense persistence
- High generative potential
- Compressed local density

## Daralon (Inflationary Era) Use

In `Daralon` (the Inflationary Era), the `Sethen` preserve concentrated potentials from which later centers of power, brightness, or sacred locality may arise.

## Boundaries

`Sethen` must not be reduced to a physical star, flame, or particle. It is a domain-order intelligence of retained potential, not yet a material object.

## Relations

- [Sethan](sethan.md) is the first-order foundation from which `Sethen` descends.
- [Daravel](daravel.md) can activate or extend the potentials `Sethen` holds.
- [Kethren](kethren.md) may later gather the local stability toward which `Sethen` points.

## Note On Form

`Sethen` is a created-world rendering of a domain-order intelligence, not an English poetic label or a literal ember.
