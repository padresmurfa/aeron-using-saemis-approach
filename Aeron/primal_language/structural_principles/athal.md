# Athal

- Rendered form: `Athal`
- Gloss: primordial grammar; lawful patterning of oscillating being
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Athal` names the law-bearing grammar that crystallizes from divine oscillation. It is the principle by which distinction, relation, pattern, symmetry, asymmetry, potential, actualization, tension, equilibrium, causation, and the other primordial conditions become structured rather than arbitrary.

## Scope

`Athal` carries:

- Lawfulness
- Pattern
- Structural consequence
- The grammar from which Primordials unfold

## First-Pulse Use

At the level of the First Pulse, `Athal` is not yet a list of independent beings or objects. It is the deep ordering principle by which the 25 Primordials crystallize downstream as structural conditions of `Loran`.

## Boundaries

`Athal` must not be treated as external law imposed upon the Creator. It is lawfulness arising from oscillating divine being itself.

## Relations

- [Loran](loran.md) is the oscillatory source from which `Athal` crystallizes.
- [Phaes](phaes.md) gives `Athal` ordered transition.
- [Orun](../threshold_events/orun.md) is the threshold through which `Athal` becomes possible.

## Note On Form

`Athal` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
