# Selketh

- Rendered form: `Selketh`
- Gloss: Cohesion Hosts; maintainers of clustered structure and layered assemblies
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Selketh` names the third-order interactional intelligence that arises when binding influence thickens within local domains and begins to stabilize clustered organization. It is the principle by which assemblies hold together as more than mere adjacency.

## Scope

`Selketh` carries:

- Clustered coherence
- Group stability
- Layered organization
- Collective persistence

## Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era) Use

In `Mirevar` (the Electroweak Separation Era), the `Selketh` keep emergent assemblies from dissolving before higher-order structure can take hold.

## Boundaries

`Selketh` must not be confused with full collective mind, civic soul, or conscious choir. It is organized cohesion, not personhood multiplied.

## Relations

- [Sevarn](sevarn.md) supplies the binding affinity `Selketh` consolidates.
- [Kethren](kethren.md) gives `Selketh` durable interior centers around which clusters can form.
- [Seralon](seralon.md) keeps distinct clusters answerable across distance.

## Note On Form

`Selketh` is a created-world rendering of an interactional-order intelligence, not an English sociological label.
