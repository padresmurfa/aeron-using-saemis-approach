# Tavrekal

- Rendered form: `Tavrekal`
- Gloss: Ash Moon; the cinder-forge moon of Aeron
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavrekal` names the ember-bearing `Seravel` of Aeron: the moon of forge heat, siege omen, and making under pressure.

## Scope

`Tavrekal` carries:

- Cinder-forge radiance
- Tempering heat under rule
- Siege omen and martial daring
- Creation sharpened by pressure

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Tavrekal` is the fireward secondary `Seravel`, most strongly associated with smiths, kilns, battle-mages, and disciplined destructive craft.

## Boundaries

`Tavrekal` must not be mistaken for infernal flame, volcanic geology alone, or a generic ash-colored moon. It is the canonical primal headword of Aeron's ember-forge moon.

## Relations

- [Seravel](seravel.md) is the broader celestial office to which `Tavrekal` belongs.
- [Tavrek](tavrek.md) gives `Tavrekal` its spark-bearing and pressure-heated tone.
- [Kethaven](kethaven.md) explains why `Tavrekal` is often read together with deep craft, furnaces, and held interior fire.

## Note On Form

`Tavrekal` preserves the `tavrek` line while widening into a more office-bearing cadence, because spark here is no longer only enclosed contrast but a recurring moon-sign of tempered making.
