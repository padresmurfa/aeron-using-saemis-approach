# Vaelvor

- Rendered form: `Vaelvor`
- Gloss: The Onward, Time-Vatharen; the wandering force-bearer of forward-flowing duration
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Vaelvor` names the [Vatharen](vatharen.md) that bears Time as forward-flow. It is the principle by which duration moves from before to after across all created things, made visible as a wandering radiant body in Aeron's sky.

## Scope

`Vaelvor` carries:

- Forward-flowing duration
- The cosmic onward of being from before to after
- Sequence and the irreversibility of moments
- Time as substance, not as a personal god of clocks

## Planetary Use

In Aeron's sky, `Vaelvor` is gazed upon by those who would understand the cosmic onward: chroniclers, calendrists, prophets reading durational cadence rather than fated outcome. Its mana-flavor expresses as the felt pressure of time-passing, distinct from any moon's monthly rhythm. Its paired counterpart is [Vaelmor](vaelmor.md), the Time-Vatharen of the held-back past.

## Boundaries

`Vaelvor` must not be confused with the cyclical phase of [Seravel](seravel.md), with the era-naming of `Dara` and its descendants, or with the personal time-gods of pantheons above. It is duration as primal force, not era as expressive capacity.

## Relations

- [Vaelmor](vaelmor.md) is `Vaelvor`'s polar counterpart: the held past against which the onward flow is read.
- [Vatharen](vatharen.md) is the class to which `Vaelvor` belongs.
- [Dara](../high_abstractions/dara.md) is the expressive capacity within the Creator's will from which forward-flow is articulated.

## Note On Form

`Vaelvor` blends the `Vael-` family (temporal motion) with `-vor` (forward-bearing), because the word names duration carried onward — not held, recorded, or returned.
