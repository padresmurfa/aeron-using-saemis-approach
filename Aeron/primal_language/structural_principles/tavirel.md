# Tavirel

- Rendered form: `Tavirel`
- Gloss: Luminants; mobile radiant threads that transmit oscillatory energy
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavirel` names the sixth-order mobility intelligence that arises when `Tavrek` refine into subtle traversing threads and stored energetic contrast becomes transmissive radiance. It is the principle by which energy may travel between composites without requiring the composites themselves to merge.

## Scope

`Tavirel` carries:

- Radiant mobility
- Rapid traversal
- Resonance sensitivity
- Energy transmission across structures

## Seraelon (Lepton Era) Use

In `Seraelon` (the Lepton Era), the `Tavirel` transmit oscillatory energy between composites and make mobile radiance possible.

## Boundaries

`Tavirel` must not be confused with later light entities, illumination spirits, or inspiration agents already bearing intent. It is mobile radiance, not conscious enlightenment.

## Relations

- [Tavrek](tavrek.md) is the confinement-order lineage from which `Tavirel` refine.
- [Oraelin](oraelin.md) gives `Tavirel` lawful passage through shifting thresholds.
- [Tavirin](tavirin.md) can use the radiance borne by `Tavirel` to keep distant complements answerable.

## Note On Form

`Tavirel` preserves the `tav` family because mobility releases polarity into traversing radiance rather than dissolving it.
