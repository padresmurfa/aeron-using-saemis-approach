# Tavrek

- Rendered form: `Tavrek`
- Gloss: Sparkbearers; concentrated bearers of energetic contrast within enclosure
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Tavrek` names the fourth-order confinement intelligence that forms when maintained polarity collapses into localized nodes inside stable enclosures. It preserves intense contrast as stored potential rather than dispersed field relation.

## Scope

`Tavrek` carries:

- High interior tension
- Concentrated energetic contrast
- Potential for sudden release
- Focused energetic identity

## Kethoran (Quark Era) Use

In `Kethoran` (the Quark Era), the `Tavrek` hold concentrated energetic difference within confined structures and make ignition-like release structurally thinkable.

## Boundaries

`Tavrek` must not be confused with literal fire, lightning beings, or moral volatility. It is stored energetic contrast, not passion with intent.

## Relations

- [Tavhor](tavhor.md) is the interactional-order foundation from which `Tavrek` condenses.
- [Mirath](mirath.md) governs the reforming pressure around `Tavrek`.
- [Oraeth](oraeth.md) regulates the threshold between stored tension and released passage.

## Note On Form

`Tavrek` is a created-world rendering of a confinement-order intelligence, not an English elemental label.
