# Miravel

- Rendered form: `Miravel`
- Gloss: Transmuters; catalytic conversion between states through interaction
- Tier: structural principle

## Meaning

`Miravel` names the third-order interactional intelligence by which transformative influence becomes distinct within creation. It permits one structured state to pass lawfully into another through contact, exchange, and directed conversion.

## Scope

`Miravel` carries:

- Catalytic change
- State-conversion through interaction
- Actualization under asymmetry
- Evolution without structural ruin

## Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era) Use

In `Mirevar` (the Electroweak Separation Era), the `Miravel` make interaction developmental rather than merely adhesive, allowing domains and structures to convert, adapt, and advance.

## Boundaries

`Miravel` must not be confused with chaos, destruction for its own sake, or moral temptation. It is lawful transformation, not ruin.

## Relations

- [Dara](../high_abstractions/dara.md) gives `Miravel` expressive drive.
- [Athal](athal.md) gives `Miravel` asymmetrical consequence.
- [Athren](athren.md) preserves the lawful divergence that `Miravel` can generate.

## Note On Form

`Miravel` is a created-world rendering of an interactional-order intelligence, not an English alchemical term.
