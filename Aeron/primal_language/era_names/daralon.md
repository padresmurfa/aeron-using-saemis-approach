# Daralon

- Rendered form: `Daralon`
- Gloss: Inflationary Era; the widening age in which domains become durable
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Daralon` names the age of rapid harmonic enlargement, when reality widens faster than full-phase coherence can be borne across the whole field and domains acquire durable locality.

## Scope

`Daralon` carries:

- Expansion of ordered reality
- Domain separation and phase lag
- The rise of localized harmonic persistence

## Canonical Status

`Daralon` is the primal-language name for the Inflationary Era.

## Relations

- [Dara](../high_abstractions/dara.md) and [Dareth](../structural_principles/dareth.md) give `Daralon` its expansive tonal family.
- [Kethren](../structural_principles/kethren.md) and the other Domain Orders arise during `Daralon`.
- [Mirevar](mirevar.md) follows once interaction itself differentiates.
