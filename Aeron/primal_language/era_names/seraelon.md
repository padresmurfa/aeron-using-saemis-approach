# Seraelon

- Rendered form: `Seraelon`
- Gloss: Lepton Era; the age of mobility, encounter, and transmissive relation
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Seraelon` names the age in which subtle mobile beings move between composites, relation can travel without binding, and encounter becomes structurally possible.

## Scope

`Seraelon` carries:

- Harmonic mobility
- Encounter without merger
- Transmissive influence
- Proto-awareness of otherness

## Canonical Status

`Seraelon` is the primal-language name for the Lepton Era.

- [Tavirel](../structural_principles/tavirel.md), [Oraelin](../structural_principles/oraelin.md), and [Seraelin](../structural_principles/seraelin.md) express its radiant passage, threshold mobility, and phase-carrying relation.

- `Seraelon` remains downstream of [Selkoran](selkoran.md) and upstream of later conscious ages still uncataloged.
