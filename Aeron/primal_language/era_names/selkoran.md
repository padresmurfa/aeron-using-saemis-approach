# Selkoran

- Rendered form: `Selkoran`
- Gloss: Hadron Era; the age of composite binding and nested interiority
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Selkoran` names the age in which confined interiors bind into higher-order unities and composite identity becomes structurally real.

## Scope

`Selkoran` carries:

- Composite binding
- Nested interiority
- Coordinated sub-identities
- Proto-personhood without self-ownership

## Canonical Status

`Selkoran` is the primal-language name for the Hadron Era.

## Relations

- [Selkor](../structural_principles/selkor.md) gives `Selkoran` its tonal family of interior collectivity.
- [Selkoren](../structural_principles/selkoren.md) and [Athoren](../structural_principles/athoren.md) express its composite coherence and proto-self structure.
- [Ketharen](../structural_principles/ketharen.md), [Phaemoren](../structural_principles/phaemoren.md), [Oraethen](../structural_principles/oraethen.md), and [Taviren](../structural_principles/taviren.md) deepen its stable plurality.
- `Selkoran` remains downstream of [Kethoran](kethoran.md) and upstream of later self-aware ages still uncataloged.
