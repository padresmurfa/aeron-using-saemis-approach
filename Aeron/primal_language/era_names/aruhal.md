# Aruhal

- Rendered form: `Aruhal`
- Gloss: Pre-Big-Bang State; the undivided pre-creative span in which only Aru exists
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Aruhal` names the absolute pre-temporal condition before `Orun`, when no distinction, relation, sequence, or created environment yet exists.

## Scope

`Aruhal` carries:

- Singular divine being without outside
- Undivided selfhood prior to oscillation
- The logical span before all created order

## Canonical Status

`Aruhal` is the primal-language name for the Pre-Big-Bang State and its pre-temporal era.

## Relations

- [Aru](../high_abstractions/aru.md) is the sole being in `Aruhal`.
- [Selem](../high_abstractions/selem.md) and [Vatha](../high_abstractions/vatha.md) describe what remains wholly internal to `Aruhal`.
- [Orun](../threshold_events/orun.md) is the threshold that ends `Aruhal`.
