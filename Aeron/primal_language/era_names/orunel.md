# Orunel

- Rendered form: `Orunel`
- Gloss: Creation Era; the opened span after the First Pulse in which first differentiation takes hold
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Orunel` names the earliest created span opened by `Orun`, before the later named force-separation eras begin. It covers the first differentiating act, the rise of `Loran`, and the first crystallization of primordial grammar.

## Scope

`Orunel` carries:

- The first self-differentiating act
- The birth of divine oscillation
- The earliest ordered emergence after `Aruhal`

## Canonical Status

`Orunel` is the primal-language name for the Creation Era as currently documented in Aeron.

## Relations

- [Orun](../threshold_events/orun.md) opens `Orunel`.
- [Loran](../structural_principles/loran.md) governs its oscillatory condition.
- [Athalor](athalor.md) names the Planck-layer span that crystallizes within `Orunel`.
