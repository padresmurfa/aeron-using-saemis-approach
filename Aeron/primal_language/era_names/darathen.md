# Darathen

- Rendered form: `Darathen`
- Gloss: Dark Ages; the age of luminous silence and existential solitude
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Darathen` names the age in which outward radiance falls latent, self-aware beings endure silence without common illumination, and alignment must be borne from within rather than confirmed everywhere at once.

## Scope

`Darathen` carries:

- Luminous absence
- Interior orientation
- Solitary freedom
- Solitary strain before soul-bearing ethics
- Fidelity tested in silence

## Canonical Status

`Darathen` is the primal-language name for the Dark Ages.

## Relations

- [Dara](../high_abstractions/dara.md) gives `Darathen` its tonal lineage of expression held in reserve rather than openly radiating.
- [Vathren](../structural_principles/vathren.md) and [Tavilor](../structural_principles/tavilor.md) express its path-seeking will and preserved interior light.
- [Oraethar](../structural_principles/oraethar.md), [Phaemorel](../structural_principles/phaemorel.md), [Athreth](../structural_principles/athreth.md), [Atherel](../structural_principles/atherel.md), [Selkorath](../structural_principles/selkorath.md), and [Serathen](../structural_principles/serathen.md) deepen its sovereign isolation, narrative inwardness, experimental divergence, silent contemplation, strained covenant, and unseen harmonizing fidelity.
- `Darathen` remains downstream of [Athorael](athorael.md) and upstream of later ages of stars, embodied history, soul-bearing life, and inter-creature conflict still uncataloged.
