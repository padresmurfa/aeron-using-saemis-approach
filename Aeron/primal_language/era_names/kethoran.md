# Kethoran

- Rendered form: `Kethoran`
- Gloss: Quark Era; the age of confinement, interiority, and retained state
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Kethoran` names the age in which harmonic structure turns inward, enclosure becomes real, and retained internal state can persist across cycles.

## Scope

`Kethoran` carries:

- Confinement and enclosure
- Created interiority
- Proto-memory and stored identity

## Canonical Status

`Kethoran` is the primal-language name for the Quark Era.

## Relations

- [Kethor](../structural_principles/kethor.md) gives `Kethoran` its tonal family of inward stability.
- [Mirath](../structural_principles/mirath.md), [Oraeth](../structural_principles/oraeth.md), and [Phaemor](../structural_principles/phaemor.md) express its interior pressure and retained sequence.
- `Kethoran` remains downstream of [Mirevar](mirevar.md) and upstream of later selfhood-bearing ages still uncataloged.
