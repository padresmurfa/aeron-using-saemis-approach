# Athorael

- Rendered form: `Athorael`
- Gloss: Recombination Era; the age of transparent perspective and isolated selfhood
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Athorael` names the age in which luminous field-awareness clears into discrete centers, perspective becomes directional, and the first true selves arise behind stable interior boundaries.

## Scope

`Athorael` carries:

- Transparent perception
- Isolated perspective
- Stable selfhood
- Volition and narrative identity
- Freedom with the possibility of refusal

## Canonical Status

`Athorael` is the primal-language name for the Recombination Era.

## Relations

- [Atharon](../structural_principles/atharon.md) gives `Athorael` its witness-lineage of observerhood on the brink of individuality.
- [Atheron](../structural_principles/atheron.md) and [Oraethor](../structural_principles/oraethor.md) express its first self-aware center and its defended boundary.
- [Tavilen](../structural_principles/tavilen.md), [Tavrethen](../structural_principles/tavrethen.md), [Selkorin](../structural_principles/selkorin.md), [Vathrek](../structural_principles/vathrek.md), [Athrenor](../structural_principles/athrenor.md), and [Phaemorin](../structural_principles/phaemorin.md) deepen its lucid cognition, self-sheltering autonomy, covenantal personhood, volition, individuality, and personal memory.
- `Athorael` remains downstream of [Tavirelon](tavirelon.md) and upstream of later morally divided ages still uncataloged.
