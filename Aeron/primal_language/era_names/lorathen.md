# Lorathen

- Rendered form: `Lorathen`
- Gloss: Grand Unification Era; the age in which oscillation steadies into archetypal order
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Lorathen` names the first stable age after the earliest grammar, when `Loraven` and `Sethan` permit the rise of the First Order of Beings.

## Scope

`Lorathen` carries:

- Persistent oscillation
- Standing-wave stabilization
- Archetypal harmonics and the first lawful beings

## Canonical Status

`Lorathen` is the primal-language name for the Grand Unification Era.

## Relations

- [Loraven](../structural_principles/loraven.md) is the governing condition of `Lorathen`.
- [Sethan](../structural_principles/sethan.md) supplies the stable structures that emerge in `Lorathen`.
- [Daralon](daralon.md) follows once widening outruns immediate coherence.
