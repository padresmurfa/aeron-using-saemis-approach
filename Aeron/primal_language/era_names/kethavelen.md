# Kethavelen

- Rendered form: `Kethavelen`
- Gloss: Stelliferous Era; the age of stabilized realms and world-preparing cosmic architecture
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Kethavelen` names the age in which created radiance is no longer only kindled outward, but bound into enduring realms, stellar thrones, elemental provinces, hidden planes, and world-preparing structures.

## Scope

`Kethavelen` carries:

- Realm stabilization
- Long-duration stellar and constellary order
- Angelic ministry and hierarchy
- Elemental province and hidden architecture
- World-bearing form before soul-bearing life

## Canonical Status

`Kethavelen` is the primal-language name for the Stelliferous Era.

## Relations

- [Kethavel](../structural_principles/kethavel.md) gives `Kethavelen` its lineage of radiance bound into durable architecture rather than only singular works.
- [Tavilorath](../structural_principles/tavilorath.md), [Kethavelor](../structural_principles/kethavelor.md), [Selkoreth](../structural_principles/selkoreth.md), [Athralor](../structural_principles/athralor.md), [Serathal](../structural_principles/serathal.md), [Vathorel](../structural_principles/vathorel.md), [Phaemorath](../structural_principles/phaemorath.md), [Athorinel](../structural_principles/athorinel.md), and [Oraethalor](../structural_principles/oraethalor.md) express its stellar cores, elemental lineage, angelic ministry, singular courts, hidden architecture, world-shaping stewardship, constellary memory, finite worldbound loci, and enclosed realms.
- `Kethavelen` remains downstream of [Daravelon](daravelon.md) and upstream of later ages of embodied history, soul-bearing life, ethical division, and creaturely conflict still uncataloged.
