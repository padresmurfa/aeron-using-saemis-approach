# Athralen

- Rendered form: `Athralen`
- Gloss: Continental Differentiation Era; the age of articulated land and terrain identity
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Athralen` names the age in which bound worlds cease to be smooth cradles and gain differentiated surfaces: plates, continents, mountain chains, rifts, basins, and geological regions capable of enduring identity.

## Scope

`Athralen` carries:

- Articulated crust and plate motion
- Continental uplift and separation
- Terrain identity and regional distinction
- Geological memory in surface form
- Stable land before life

## Canonical Status

`Athralen` is the primal-language name for the Continental Differentiation Era.

## Relations

- [Athrenor](../structural_principles/athrenor.md) and [Kethralen](kethralen.md) together give `Athralen` its tonal lineage of differentiated identity grounded into enduring place.
- [Kethralor](../structural_principles/kethralor.md), [Mirathor](../structural_principles/mirathor.md), [Athrenel](../structural_principles/athrenel.md), [Miravelen](../structural_principles/miravelen.md), [Phaemoralen](../structural_principles/phaemoralen.md), [Tavreken](../structural_principles/tavreken.md), [Tavraleth](../structural_principles/tavraleth.md), [Selkorel](../structural_principles/selkorel.md), and [Seraelen](../structural_principles/seraelen.md) express its plate authority, uplift, fracture, basin continuity, stone memory, volcanic renewal, shielding, soil preparation, and horizon-scale climate rhythm.
- `Athralen` remains downstream of [Kethralen](kethralen.md) and upstream of later ages of life, creaturehood, culture, rivers, forests, and soul-bearing history still uncataloged.
