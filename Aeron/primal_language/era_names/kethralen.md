# Kethralen

- Rendered form: `Kethralen`
- Gloss: Planetary Formation Era; the age of bound worlds and stable place
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Kethralen` names the age in which realm-scale radiance is drawn inward and bound into local worlds, layered environments, recurring cycles, and enduring places.

## Scope

`Kethralen` carries:

- Bound world formation
- Local gravity-like cohesion
- Layered interiors, skies, and seas
- Cyclical recurrence on world scale
- Stable place before life

## Canonical Status

`Kethralen` is the primal-language name for the Planetary Formation Era.

- [Kethren](../structural_principles/kethren.md) and [Tavral](../structural_principles/tavral.md) together give `Kethralen` its tonal lineage of held center and guarded place, fitting an era in which realms settle into bounded worlds.
- [Taviloran](../structural_principles/taviloran.md), [Kethaven](../structural_principles/kethaven.md), [Oraethalen](../structural_principles/oraethalen.md), [Miravelor](../structural_principles/miravelor.md), [Seraelor](../structural_principles/seraelor.md), [Vathalen](../structural_principles/vathalen.md), [Tavrekor](../structural_principles/tavrekor.md), and [Tavralen](../structural_principles/tavralen.md) express its gravitic centrality, mantle depth, sky-bearing envelope, oceanic continuity, axial recurrence, cradle-balance, interior ember, and shielding ring.
- `Kethralen` remains downstream of [Kethavelen](kethavelen.md) and upstream of later ages of life, souls, civilization, moral history, and creaturely conflict still uncataloged.
