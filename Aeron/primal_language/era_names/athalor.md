# Athalor

- Rendered form: `Athalor`
- Gloss: Planck Layer; the primordial grammar span in which the Primordials crystallize
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Athalor` names the layer of earliest created grammar in which the Primordials emerge as structural conditions of oscillating being.

## Scope

`Athalor` carries:

- Primordial unfolding
- The first lawful crystallization of created grammar
- The Planck-layer foundations of later structure

## Canonical Status

`Athalor` is the primal-language name for the Planck Layer / Primordial Unfolding span within `Orunel`.

## Relations

- [Athal](../structural_principles/athal.md) is the grammar that defines `Athalor`.
- [Orunel](orunel.md) is the broader era within which `Athalor` appears.
- [Lorathen](lorathen.md) follows once oscillation steadies into archetypal order.
