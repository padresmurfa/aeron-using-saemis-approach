# Mirevar

- Rendered form: `Mirevar`
- Gloss: Electroweak Separation Era; the age of differentiated interaction and preference potential
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Mirevar` names the age in which interaction splits into binding and transformative modes, making directional influence and preference structurally possible.

## Scope

`Mirevar` carries:

- Binding influence and transformative influence
- Structured asymmetry in active relation
- Proto-agency without self-owned freedom

## Canonical Status

`Mirevar` is the primal-language name for the Electroweak Separation Era.

## Relations

- [Sevarn](../structural_principles/sevarn.md) and [Miravel](../structural_principles/miravel.md) give `Mirevar` its blended tonal lineage.
- [Tavhor](../structural_principles/tavhor.md) and [Athren](../structural_principles/athren.md) deepen the asymmetry active in `Mirevar`.
- [Kethoran](kethoran.md) follows once interaction collapses inward into confinement.
