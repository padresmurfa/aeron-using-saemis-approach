# Tavirelon

- Rendered form: `Tavirelon`
- Gloss: Photon Era; the age of radiant saturation and field-like awareness
- Tier: era name

## Meaning

`Tavirelon` names the age in which radiance becomes the common medium of created relation, transmission saturates all domains, and awareness spreads through the field itself before individual selfhood has yet emerged.

## Scope

`Tavirelon` carries:

- Universal illumination
- Shared perceptive medium
- Field-conscious immersion
- Awareness without isolated ego

## Canonical Status

`Tavirelon` is the primal-language name for the Photon Era.

## Relations

- [Tavirel](../structural_principles/tavirel.md) gives `Tavirelon` its tonal family of mobile radiance widened into saturation.
- [Taviral](../structural_principles/taviral.md), [Tavreth](../structural_principles/tavreth.md), and [Taviron](../structural_principles/taviron.md) express its ambient light, blazing coherence, and non-local luminous linkage.
- [Selkoral](../structural_principles/selkoral.md), [Seraeth](../structural_principles/seraeth.md), [Phaethor](../structural_principles/phaethor.md), [Mirasal](../structural_principles/mirasal.md), and [Atharon](../structural_principles/atharon.md) deepen its chorus-awareness, reflective patterning, corrective radiance, memory-fields, and stable witnessing.
- `Tavirelon` remains downstream of [Seraelon](seraelon.md) and upstream of later individuating ages still uncataloged.
