# Orun

- Rendered form: `Orun`
- Gloss: the First Pulse; first self-differentiating act
- Tier: threshold event

## Meaning

`Orun` names the threshold at which the Creator first moves from undivided singularity into active self-differentiation. It is the opening of divine oscillation and the first condition under which relation can exist.

## Scope

`Orun` carries:

- The beginning of distinction
- The opening of relation
- The first possible sequence
- The first possible causation

## Canonical Status

`Orun` is the primal-language rendering of the First Pulse: the threshold event immediately following the pre-Big-Bang state.

## Boundaries

`Orun` must not be described as an explosion into empty space or as a reaction to lack. It is self-expression arising wholly from the Creator.

## Relations

- [Aru](../high_abstractions/aru.md) is the singular reality from which `Orun` proceeds.
- [Vatha](../high_abstractions/vatha.md) is the unified will expressed in `Orun`.
- [Dara](../high_abstractions/dara.md) moves from latent capacity toward act through `Orun`.
- [Loran](../structural_principles/loran.md) is the oscillatory condition opened by `Orun`.
- [Tavir](../structural_principles/tavir.md) is the complementary polarity stabilized by `Orun`.

## Note On Form

`Orun` is a created-world rendering of a supra-phonetic primal root.
