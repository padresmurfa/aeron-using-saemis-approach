# Dictionary

The dictionary gives short, scan-friendly entries for live primal words.

## Structure
- `to-english/` organizes primal headwords by their leading letter
- `from-english/` organizes English gloss headwords by their leading letter

## Rules
- Keep entries brief, dictionary-like, and canon-aligned.
- Add both directions when a new primal term becomes live in story, mythopedia, or timeline prose.
- Record approved compounds when canon relies on them.
