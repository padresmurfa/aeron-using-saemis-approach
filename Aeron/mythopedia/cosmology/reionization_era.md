# Daravelon (Reionization Era)

## Definition

`Daravelon` is the stage of early creation that follows [Darathen (Dark Ages)](dark_ages.md), analogically corresponding to roughly `100-200 million` years after the Big Bang in physical cosmology. During this era, the first stable created lights ignite. Self-aware beings no longer only preserve illumination within themselves, but begin to radiate outward across vast darkened spans.

## Ontological Shift

Before this era:

- Selfhood exists.
- Isolation exists.
- Volition exists.
- Inwardly preserved light exists.
- No created being yet sustains large-scale outward radiance.

During `Daravelon`:

- Certain beings ignite into stable luminous centers.
- Light is generated outwardly from created interiority.
- Radiance begins to travel across long dark spans again.
- Illumination becomes an active office rather than only a preserved condition.

## What Ignition Introduces

Ignition introduces:

- Sustained interior-to-exterior radiance
- Creative output
- Influence across vast distances
- Stewardship through illumination
- Patterning of domains by created light

This is the first era in which created beings become luminous shapers rather than only participants or solitary keepers.

## The Tenth Order Of Beings

The Tenth Order are Ignition Orders. They arise when solitary lineages and older hearth-structures pass from preserved inward light into sustained outward radiance.

They are the first created luminous offices capable of illuminating regions beyond themselves, stabilizing new centers, and shaping large-scale conditions inside creation. They possess self-awareness, volition, memory, interior light, and external creative influence.

Soul-bearing ethical categories remain far downstream. Yet created stewardship has now become architecturally potent.

### I. Taviloron (Starforged)

`Taviloron` arise when `Tavilor` (Keepers of the Inner Flame) cease only to preserve light inwardly and become stable outward-radiant centers, often awakening the old hearth-capacity latent in `Ketharen` (Heartforged). They generate the first durable luminous cores in creation.

Future Evolution:
Foundations of stellar deities, world-light beings, and cosmic hearth-centers.

### II. Vathoral (Flamebearer Regents)

`Vathoral` arise when `Vathren` (Wayseekers) no longer only seek a course, but willingly carry illumination for others. They lead domains through radiance and become the first created archetype of rulership within luminous space.

### III. Selkoron (Covenant Luminaries)

`Selkoron` arise when `Selkorath` (Covenant Strainers) hold relation through darkness so fully that bond itself begins to shine. They create relational light networks and make interlinked radiant authority possible without dissolving persons into sameness.

Future Evolution:
Precursors to archangelic choirs and great covenantal intelligences.

### IV. Athralon (Divergent Suns)

`Athralon` arise when `Athreth` (Divergent Flames) sustain outward radiance according to self-chosen proportion. They make singular and asymmetric brilliance possible at large scale, so whole regions may now bear the mark of one strong created variance.

### V. Kethavel (Forgers of Form)

`Kethavel` arise when the hearth-logic of `Ketharen` and the directed force of `Vathrek` (Sparked Intenders) are taken up into sustained creative shaping. They condense radiance into lasting form and make great works possible inside creation.

Future Evolution:
Precursors to elemental lords, cosmic architects, and world-shaping makers.

### VI. Atheror (Witness Sovereigns)

`Atheror` arise when `Atherel` (Silent Observers) ignite through contemplative clarity and witness itself becomes a steady source of illumination. They clarify truth without coercion and hold authority by making what is real more plainly seen.

Future Evolution:
Foundations of wise higher beings and luminous contemplative authorities.

### VII. Phaemoral (Memory Stars)

`Phaemoral` arise when `Phaemorel` (Rememberers) burn with remembered continuity and narrative itself becomes a radiant anchor. They stabilize identity across epochs and transmit myth-bearing memory beyond the limits of one solitary life.

Future Evolution:
Precursors to mythic ancestors and civilizational memory-centers.

### VIII. Serathor (Veiled Radiants)

`Serathor` arise when `Serathen` (Hidden Singers) begin to reveal harmony by measured disclosure instead of laboring wholly unseen. They regulate access to luminous knowledge and keep deeper order from being poured out without proportion.

Future Evolution:
Precursors to hidden deities, mystery keepers, and guarded revelatory orders.

### IX. Oraethal (Enclosed Radiants)

`Oraethal` arise when `Oraethar` (Veilbound Sovereigns) ignite under hard boundary rule and sustain radiance without wide reciprocity. They become the first structures in creation capable of large-scale self-enclosed luminous order, in which light is real but not freely shared.

## Ketharel (Dimensional Accretion Rule)

All prior beings gain ignition aspects. Composite structures can now radiate, mobility can become illumination, memory can become myth-bearing continuity, and will can become stewardship.

Creation begins to cast durable lights across itself.

## Cosmological Significance

`Daravelon` introduces active creative radiance, luminous stewardship, domain-shaping power, and long-range influence carried by created centers. The universe no longer merely contains selves and silence; it now contains makers of visible order.

## Canon Boundaries

- Soul-bearing ethical categories still lie far downstream.
- No being can stand outside `Aru` or `Loran`; both remain the fabric of reality itself.
- Created stewardship in this era must not be confused with [Vatha](../../primal_language/high_abstractions/vatha.md), the Creator's sovereign unified will.
- `Athralon` and `Oraethal` mark creative asymmetry and enclosed radiance inside creation, not later adversarial powers, completed ethical dualism, or secession from reality.
- `Taviloron`, `Vathoral`, and `Selkoron` are created luminous offices, not a return to the undivided field-awareness of [Tavirelon](photon_era.md).
- Stars and large-scale created lights belong to this era, but worlds, embodied life, souls, and ethical dualism remain downstream.
