# Divine Oscillation

## Definition

Divine oscillation is the dynamic alternation that begins with the First Pulse. It is the first internal relational structure within singular divine being and the root condition for all later cosmic structure.

## Core Character

Divine oscillation is:

- Singular, not dualistic
- Harmonic, not antagonistic
- Rhythmic, not chaotic
- Structuring, not merely expressive

Aru does not cease to be one. Rather, one being (now Loran) alternates between complementary states.

## Harmonic Poles

The alternating poles of divine oscillation may be described metaphorically as:

- Expansion and containment
- Emission and reception
- Projection and return
- Crest and trough

These are descriptive metaphors for created understanding. They are not literal mechanical phases and they do not introduce moral opposition.

## Phase, Sequence, And Time

Oscillation introduces phase. Phase introduces ordered transition. Ordered transition creates the first seed of sequence. Sequence becomes the condition by which time can later emerge.

Time is therefore downstream of oscillation, not prior to it.

## Resonance, Harmony, And Distortion

Resonance becomes possible only when oscillation exists. Harmony is alignment between phases of oscillation. Disharmony is phase distortion or misalignment.

At the moment of the First Pulse, divine oscillation is perfect and stable. Distortion is meaningful as a category at this stage, but not yet actualized as moral evil.

## Canon Boundaries

- Divine oscillation must not be described as internal civil war or divided consciousness.
- Divine oscillation must not be reduced to physical vibration in pre-material space.
- Harmony and disharmony at this stage are structural, not moral.
