# Loran (Divine Oscillation)

## Definition

`Loran` is the dynamic alternation that begins with [Orun (The First Pulse)](../events/first_pulse.md). It is the first internal relational structure within singular divine being and the root condition for all later cosmic structure.

## Core Character

`Loran` is:

- Singular, not dualistic
- Harmonic, not antagonistic
- Rhythmic, not chaotic
- Structuring, not merely expressive

Aru does not cease to be one. Rather, one being now alternates between complementary states without becoming divided.

## Harmonic Poles

The alternating poles of `Loran` may be described metaphorically as:

- Expansion and containment
- Emission and reception
- Projection and return
- Crest and trough

These are descriptive metaphors for created understanding. They are not literal mechanical phases and they do not introduce moral opposition.

## Phase, Sequence, And Time

Oscillation introduces `Phaes` (phase). Phase introduces ordered transition. Ordered transition creates the first seed of sequence. Sequence becomes the condition by which time can later emerge.

Time is therefore downstream of `Loran`, not prior to it.

## Resonance, Harmony, And Distortion

Resonance becomes possible only when `Loran` exists. `Seren` (harmony) is alignment between phases of oscillation. Disharmony is phase distortion or misalignment.

At the moment of the First Pulse, `Loran` is perfect and stable. Distortion is meaningful as a category at this stage, but not yet actualized as moral evil.

## Canon Boundaries

- `Loran` must not be described as internal civil war or divided consciousness.
- `Loran` must not be reduced to physical vibration in pre-material space.
- Harmony and disharmony at this stage are structural, not moral.
