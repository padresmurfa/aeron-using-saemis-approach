# Daralon (Inflationary Era)

## Definition

`Daralon` is the stage of early creation that follows [Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)](grand_unification_era.md), analogically corresponding to roughly `10^-36` to `10^-32` seconds in physical cosmology. During this era, harmonic reality expands exponentially. Scale increases faster than full-phase coherence can propagate across the whole field, producing domain separation, phase lag, local coherence, and the Second Order of Beings.

## Structural Character

`Daralon` introduces:

- Exponential harmonic expansion
- Domain separation
- Phase lag across widening scale
- Local coherence within domains
- Proto-individuation without moral selfhood

Reality remains one in origin and law, but it is no longer equally immediate everywhere in the same way.

## Canon Boundaries

- Domain separation must not be described as fracture from `Loran` or secession from the Creator.
- Localized expression must not be mistaken for sovereign will.
- Misalignment in this era is structural rather than moral.
- `Daralon` remains innocent even while the cosmos grows more complex.
