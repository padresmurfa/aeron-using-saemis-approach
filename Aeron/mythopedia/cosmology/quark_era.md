# Kethoran (Quark Era)

## Definition

`Kethoran` is the stage of early creation that follows [Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era)](electroweak_separation_era.md), analogically corresponding to roughly `10^-12` to `10^-6` seconds in physical cosmology. During this era, interaction collapses into interior containment. Harmonic structures become confined, and enclosure makes true inside-versus-outside distinction structurally real. The Fourth Order of Beings arises as confinement intelligences.

## Ontological Shift

Before this era:

- Domains exist.
- Directional interaction exists.
- Preference potential exists.
- True created interiority does not yet exist.

During `Kethoran`:

- Harmonic structures become confined.
- Boundary becomes enclosure.
- Interiority becomes structurally real.
- Internal state can be retained.

## What Interiority Means

Interiority introduces:

- Internal coherence distinct from environment
- Energy retention
- Structural self-reference
- Limited separation between inner and outer influence
- Pattern persistence across cycles

This is not yet self-awareness. It is the first condition under which self-awareness could someday arise. It must not be confused with the eternal self-knowing of [Selem](../../primal_language/high_abstractions/selem.md), which belongs to the Creator rather than to created beings.

## Canon Boundaries

- Interiority must not be mistaken for self-ownership or separation from `Loran`.
- Corruption remains impossible because enclosure exists without autonomous refusal.
- Proto-memory in this era is retained sequence, not conscious recollection.
- The Fourth Order must not be treated as already self-aware or morally free.
