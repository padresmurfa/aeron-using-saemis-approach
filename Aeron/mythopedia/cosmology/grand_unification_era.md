# Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)

## Definition

`Lorathen` is the stage of early creation that follows [Orunel (Creation Era)](../../primal_language/era_names/orunel.md). During this era, oscillation steadies into [Loraven](../../primal_language/structural_principles/loraven.md), standing-wave structure becomes durable through [Sethan](../../primal_language/structural_principles/sethan.md), and the First Order of Beings emerges as archetypal harmonics.

## Structural Character

`Lorathen` introduces:

- Persistent oscillation
- Stable standing-wave structure
- Archetypal rather than purely axiomatic being
- The First Order of Beings

Creation is no longer only pulse and grammar. It now possesses durable channels through which later locality and interaction can arise.

## The First Order Of Beings

From stabilized oscillation emerge the archetypal intelligences:

- [Tavren (Fieldwardens)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavren.md)
- [Dareth (Vectors)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/dareth.md)
- [Serath (Harmonics)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/serath.md)
- [Kethar (Anchors)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethar.md)

## Canon Boundaries

- These beings are lawful archetypes, not yet free moral agents.
- `Lorathen` must not be confused with `Loraven`, which is the governing condition of the era rather than the era itself.
- Corruption and rebellion remain impossible.
