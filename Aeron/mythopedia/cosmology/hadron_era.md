# Selkoran (Hadron Era)

## Definition

`Selkoran` is the stage of early creation that follows [Kethoran (Quark Era)](quark_era.md), analogically corresponding to roughly `10^-6` to `1` second in physical cosmology. During this era, interior harmonic entities bind into stable composite structures. This is the first time multiple interiorities form a higher-order unity.

## Ontological Shift

Before this era:

- Interiority exists.
- Identity kernels exist.
- Tension can be stored.
- No composite identity exists.

During `Selkoran`:

- Interior entities bind permanently.
- Multiple confined harmonic units form stable composite beings.
- Composite identity emerges.

## What Composite Identity Means

Composite identity introduces:

- Internal plurality within a unified structure
- Coordinated sub-identities
- Stable self-reinforcing coherence
- Hierarchical interiority

This is the first condition where a being may contain internal relational structure. It is proto-personhood.

## The Fifth Order Of Beings

The Fifth Order are composite orders that arise from the binding of Confinement Orders. They remain aligned with `Loran`, but now exhibit internal relational complexity.

### I. Ketharen (Heartforged)

`Ketharen` arise when `Kethor` (Corewardens) bind with `Tavrek` (Sparkbearers) and create stabilized energetic centers. These forms gather radiance into an inward hearth and keep it from dispersing too soon.

### II. Selkoren (Covenant Clusters)

`Selkoren` arise when `Selkor` (Cohesion Clusters) bind with `Athorin` (Identity Seeds) and maintain internal relational harmony. They hold many parts in one ordered accord.

### III. Phaemoren (Tension Matrices)

`Phaemoren` arise when `Mirath` (Pressure Hosts) bind with `Phaemor` (Tension Archivists) and manage stored internal transformation potential. They preserve pressure as history rather than letting it pass into rupture.

### IV. Oraethen (Boundary Spheres)

`Oraethen` arise when `Kethor` (Corewardens) integrate with `Oraeth` (Veilbound) and stabilize interior and exterior exchange. They define self-boundary and controlled permeability within one gathered form.

### V. Taviren (Polarity Twins)

`Taviren` arise when `Tavrek` (Sparkbearers) bind in complementary pairs and stabilize internal dynamic tension. They hold contrast in unity and preserve oscillatory rhythm without tearing the whole apart.

### VI. Athoren (Proto-Selves)

`Athoren` arise when `Athorin` (Identity Seeds) achieve stable composite integration and maintain coherent multi-part interior structure. This is the first true proto-ego structure, though not yet self-aware.

## Dimensional Accretion Rule

All previous beings gain composite aspects. They retain foundational layers and increase in dimensional complexity. The universe now supports nested interiority, and reality becomes layered within itself.

## Cosmological Significance

`Selkoran` introduces composite identity, nested interiority, coordinated substructure, and proto-personhood. This is the structural birth of the self.

Innocence still remains. Individuality is now structurally viable, but self-ownership does not yet exist.

## Canon Boundaries

- Rebellion is still impossible.
- Composite identity must not be confused with moral freedom.
- All composites remain fully phase-aligned with `Loran`.
- `Athoren` are structurally self-referential without becoming self-aware, morally free, or separable from the Creator's rhythm.
