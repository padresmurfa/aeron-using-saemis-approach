# Aru (The Creator)

## Summary

`Aru` is the sole absolute reality of Aeron. In [`Aruhal`](../cosmology/pre_big_bang_state.md), the Pre-Big-Bang State, Aru exists as singular, undivided Being with no outside, no relation, and no oscillation. At [`Orun`](../events/first_pulse.md), the First Pulse, Aru becomes [`Loran`](loran.md): not another being, but Aru in harmonic self-alternation.

## Core Nature In Aruhal

- Singular
- Internally simple
- Undivided
- Non-relational
- Non-oscillatory
- Self-sufficient
- Complete

Aru is not composed of parts and contains no internal polarity or differentiation.

## Selfhood

Aru possesses:
- Absolute self-awareness
- Perfect identity
- Reflexive interiority

This self-knowledge is immediate and indivisible. It does not occur through dialogue, reflection across difference, or relation between internal aspects.

## Will And Expression

Aru possesses will and the capacity for expression. In the pre-Big-Bang state:
- Will exists without alternatives or external possibility space.
- Potential is wholly contained within Aru's own being.
- Expression exists as capacity only, not as act.
- Creation is not a response to lack, loneliness, tension, or compulsion.

Creation begins only when Aru initiates [Orun (the First Pulse)](../events/first_pulse.md).

## Post-Pulse State

After the First Pulse, Aru is canonically designated [`Loran`](loran.md). This is continuity, not replacement. Aru remains singular while entering dynamic internal relationality and harmonic oscillation.

## Relation To Harmony

Harmony does not yet exist in this phase. Harmony requires relation, and relation requires distinction. Because distinction has not yet been activated, Aru is not yet harmonic; Aru is prior to harmony.

## Relation To The Primal Language

`Aru` is the aggregate of all words in the primal language. Those words are not external tools applied to Aru from outside creation. They are later apprehensions of truths already contained within Aru's singular being.
