# Loran (formerly C)

## Summary

`Loran` is the canonical designation for Aru after the First Pulse. Loran is not a second god, a split fragment, or a derivative being. Loran is the same singular Aru now existing as a dynamically oscillating, internally relational, harmonic being.

Primary references:
- [Aru](creator.md)
- [The First Pulse](../events/first_pulse.md)

- [The Striking of the First Pulse](../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_striking_of_the_first_pulse.md)

## Identity


Loran is:
- Singular
- Oscillatory
- Harmonic
- Self-relational
- Dynamically complete

There are not two gods, two beings, or two wills in Loran. There is one divine identity in dynamic alternation between complementary states.

## Harmonic Polarity


The alternating states within Loran are:

- Co-essential
- Interdependent
- Non-antagonistic
- Necessary to one another

These poles are not moral opposites. Neither is superior, evil, incomplete, or disposable.

## What Becomes Possible In C


Because alternation now exists within singular divine being:
- Love becomes possible as return within relation
- Expression becomes possible as emission and reception
- Creation becomes possible as structured emergence
- Resonance, phase, and harmony become meaningful

These possibilities arise within Loran before any created cosmos yet exists.

## What Still Does Not Exist

At the stage of Loran immediately following the First Pulse, the following still do not yet exist:

- Space
- Matter
- Physical energy
- Light
- Darkness
- Angels
- Elementals
- Souls
- Moral good and evil

Only oscillating Divine Being (Loran) exists.

## Canon Boundaries

- Loran must not be described as a different being from Aru.
- Loran must not be described as divided into parts.
- Loran must not be moralized into a battle of opposites.
