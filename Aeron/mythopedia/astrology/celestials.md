# Celestials (Vatharen)

The eight `Vatharen` are the **planet-like celestial bodies** of Aeron's sky: wandering radiant bodies that visibly bear a primal force between [Seravel (moons)](moons_of_aeron.md) and the personal gods of the galaxies above. They occupy **tier 3** of the [mythic hierarchy](../cosmology/mythic_hierarchy.md): primal forces between moons and gods.

## Tier 3 Doctrine

`Vatharen` are direct Creator-creations. Each `Vatharen` bears one of the Creator's primal force-modes — distilled, visible, gazable. Each carries a unique mana-flavor by which mortals can recognize its character without needing a personal name beyond the primal headword.

The doctrine of tier 3 is that **the `Vatharen` are forces, not personalities**. They can be gazed upon and resonated with through the gaze-resolution mechanic. Mortal prayer to a `Vatharen` is a real act of attention, and the connection is bidirectional in proportion to mana, attunement, and time spent. **But a `Vatharen` does not reply with personal will.** It is closer to natural law than to deity. What returns is the felt presence of the force — clearer and stronger as resonance deepens — not the spoken answer of a god.

This is the canonical seam between tier 3 and tier 4. A god in a galaxy answers with intent. A `Vatharen` answers with substance.

## The Eight Vatharen

The `Vatharen` are organized in **four polar pairs**. Each pair shares a primal-language root and is split by opposed endings, so the polarity reads off the name. The pairs do not antagonize one another; their opposition is co-essential, after the manner of [Aru](../entities/aru.md)/[Loran](../entities/loran.md), [Solaryth](../../primal_language/structural_principles/solaryth.md)/[Nyxorys](../../primal_language/structural_principles/nyxorys.md), and [Serenel](../../primal_language/structural_principles/serenel.md)/[Seramor](../../primal_language/structural_principles/seramor.md).

### Pair 1 — Time and Memory

- **[Vaelvor](../../primal_language/structural_principles/vaelvor.md)**, the Onward — Time-Vatharen. **Mana-flavor:** the felt pressure of forward-flow; duration carried from before to after across all created things. **Gazed by:** chroniclers, calendrists, prophets reading durational cadence. Gaze-resolution: full at peak attunement; the viewer perceives `Vaelvor` as a slow-turning radiance whose movement reveals the cosmic onward.
- **[Vaelmor](../../primal_language/structural_principles/vaelmor.md)**, the Held — Memory-Vatharen. **Mana-flavor:** the held-down weight of accumulated deed; preservation of what has happened. **Gazed by:** archivists, mnemonic mages, those who seek the weight of the past. Gaze-resolution: full at peak attunement; the viewer perceives `Vaelmor` as deep-set radiance, the substance of the held past made visible.

The Vael-pair is read together when a question of *cause and consequence across time* is asked of the sky.

### Pair 2 — Order and Strife

- **[Taelden](../../primal_language/structural_principles/taelden.md)**, the Bound — Order-Vatharen. **Mana-flavor:** the felt weight of rule that does not need a ruler; cosmic coherence as substance. **Gazed by:** lawgivers, mages of binding, philosophers of cosmic structure. Gaze-resolution: full at peak attunement; the viewer perceives `Taelden` as a still-bright body whose presence makes adjacent law more legible.
- **[Taelmoth](../../primal_language/structural_principles/taelmoth.md)**, the Friction — Strife-Vatharen. **Mana-flavor:** cosmic agitation; the felt necessity of stress within pattern. **Gazed by:** reformers, insurrectionists, mages of disruption, those who study why coherent things break. Gaze-resolution: full at peak attunement; the viewer perceives `Taelmoth` as a body whose light flickers without ever extinguishing — friction made visible without rupture.

The Tael-pair is read together when a question of *whether to hold or to break* is asked of the sky. Neither is good; neither is evil. Both are necessary.

### Pair 3 — Increase and Decay

- **[Yirven](../../primal_language/structural_principles/yirven.md)**, the Unfolded — Increase-Vatharen. **Mana-flavor:** the felt pressure toward surplus; multiplication as cosmic mode. **Gazed by:** agronomists, mages of generative work, those who study why being multiplies rather than holding still. Gaze-resolution: full at peak attunement; the viewer perceives `Yirven` as a body that seems quietly to bloom outward without growing in apparent size.
- **[Yirmor](../../primal_language/structural_principles/yirmor.md)**, the Slow-Unmade — Decay-Vatharen. **Mana-flavor:** the felt pull toward depth and exhaustion; the patience of dissolution. **Gazed by:** physicians of long illness, ruin-readers, mages of decomposition. Gaze-resolution: full at peak attunement; the viewer perceives `Yirmor` as a body whose radiance softens at its edges, never extinguishing but always seeming on the verge of doing so.

The Yir-pair is read together when a question of *whether being is gathering or dispersing* is asked of the sky.

### Pair 4 — Binding and Severance

- **[Aenel](../../primal_language/structural_principles/aenel.md)**, the Knotted — Binding-Vatharen. **Mana-flavor:** the felt presence of unseen connection; relation across distance as cosmic substance. **Gazed by:** scribes of lineage, mages of sympathetic linkage, diplomats and matchmakers reading cosmic threads of affinity. Gaze-resolution: full at peak attunement; the viewer perceives `Aenel` as a body trailing faint, near-imperceptible filaments toward and through every neighboring sky-body.
- **[Aenor](../../primal_language/structural_principles/aenor.md)**, the Cut — Severance-Vatharen. **Mana-flavor:** the felt edge along which one thing ends and another begins. **Gazed by:** classifiers, edge-mages, surgeons of the cosmic kind, those who hold that being requires unjoining as much as joining. Gaze-resolution: full at peak attunement; the viewer perceives `Aenor` as a body whose outline is preternaturally sharp — the cleanest edge in the entire sky.

The Aen-pair is read together when a question of *what is to be joined and what is to be parted* is asked of the sky.

## Orbital Behavior and the Above-Physics Rule

`Vatharen` mostly follow fixed paths around Aeron, lawful and predictable enough for astrologers to chart. They are above physics per the rule canonized in the [mythic hierarchy](../cosmology/mythic_hierarchy.md): they may deviate, with rarity scaling steeply with their sky-observed apparent size and consequence scaling with rarity.

- A small `Vatharen` deviation (slight off-path drift over months) is uncommon but readable, and is interpreted by astrologers as the force itself bearing down differently for a season.
- A large or sudden `Vatharen` deviation is once-in-millennia and is a tier-defining mythic event. The deviation of a paired `Vatharen` while its counterpart holds true is among the gravest omens recorded.
- The `Vatharen` are not physically tied to one another; their pairings are co-essential but not orbitally locked. Conjunctions, oppositions, and longer alignments occur and are read as substantive astrological events.

## Universal-vs-Positional

`Vatharen` are **universal as entities** — there is one `Vaelvor`, one `Vaelmor`, one of each — and **positional as observed phenomena**, visible only when above the local horizon, like all sky bodies in Aeron's geocentric round geometry. Their visibility is **not** faith-modulated (that rule applies to galaxies/pantheons at tier 4, not to `Vatharen`). A `Vatharen`'s observed brightness is governed by ordinary celestial geometry plus its own above-physics deviation behavior.

## Astrological Significance

- The eight `Vatharen` together form an unspoken question to the sky: *what kind of thing is happening?* Time-versus-Memory speaks to the temporal frame of an event; Order-versus-Strife speaks to its lawful character; Increase-versus-Decay speaks to its directional gradient; Binding-versus-Severance speaks to its relational character.
- A skilled astrologer reads the four pairs together to characterize a moment — the qualitative answer to "what cosmic forces are pressing on now?" — distinct from what specific gods (tier 4) or beasts (tier 5) are favored.
- `Vatharen` readings sit beneath pantheon astrology and above moon astrology in the layered interpretive stack.

## Canon Boundaries

- The `Vatharen` are forces, not personalities. Pages and stories that treat a `Vatharen` as a god who answers with personal speech contradict the tier-3 doctrine.
- The eight `Vatharen` named here are the canonical full set. There are not nine; there are not seven. Future canon may name minor adjacent celestial bodies, but those will not be `Vatharen`.
- Numeric orbital periods, apparent-size rankings, mana-flavor cross-effects under conjunction, and the catalog of every astrological reading combination are not yet canonized. These are open elaborations for downstream specialist work.
- Gaze-resolution full-attunement scenes for each `Vatharen` (the experience of "seeing" the body in detail) are sketched here in one line each but are open for narrative deepening in saga prose.
- Per-`Vatharen` derived classes (mortal lineages, monsters, magic systems descended from one specific `Vatharen`) are not canonized here. The `Vatharen` are direct Creator-creations and most of their effects on Aeron flow through resonance and gaze, not through descended children.
- The `Vatharen` do not replace any tier 2, tier 4, or tier 5 entity. Where a domain might seem to overlap (e.g. `Vaelmor` memory vs `Phaemorath` memory constellations), the boundaries section in each headword file states the distinction explicitly.
