# Stars

Stars in Aeron are [Taviloren (stars)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviloren.md): created luminous centers, not accidental scatter or decorative backdrop.

## Canonical Sequence

- In [Daravelon (Reionization Era)](../cosmology/reionization_era.md), `Taviloron` (Starforged) ignite the first stable created lights and `Phaemoral` (Memory Stars) make radiant continuity myth-bearing.
- In [Kethavelen (Stelliferous Era)](../cosmology/stelliferous_era.md), `Tavilorath` (Stellar Thrones) anchor enduring star-systems and `Phaemorath` (Memory Constellations) stabilize remembered heavens at realm scale.
- In [Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era)](../cosmology/planetary_formation_era.md), worlds arise within systems already ordered by these earlier luminous architectures.

## Astrological Significance

- Every primal-language word has a corresponding `Taviloren` bearing the same name, so the sky is also a visible lexicon of creation.
- Studying a `Taviloren` grants deeper insight into the meaning, tendencies, and hidden relations of its corresponding word.
- Fixed stars are long-duration structures used for continuity, orientation, and deep-cycle reading rather than sudden omen bursts.
- They provide the durable background against which moons, luminaries, constellations, and shooting stars are interpreted.

## Canon Boundaries

- Do not treat the Stelliferous Era as the first appearance of all stars; it is the era of enduring stellar and constellary stabilization after Reionization ignition.
- A star does not replace the word it bears; it reveals and witnesses that word in the heavens.
- Fixed stars are distinct from [shooting stars](shooting_stars.md), which are transient law-bearing events.
