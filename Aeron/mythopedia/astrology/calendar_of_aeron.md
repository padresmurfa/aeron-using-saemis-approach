# Calendar of Aeron

## Foundational Constant

- Aeron's solar year is exactly 364 days.
- The year consists of 13 months of 28 days each.
- There are no leap days, intercalary insertions, or compensatory corrections.

## Monthly Rhythm

- Each month is divided into two fortnights.
- A fortnight corresponds to the waxing dominance of one primary moon while the other wanes.
- One fortnight favors sunlight dominance and the other darklight dominance.

## Astral Synchronization

- Civil timekeeping is synchronized directly to the `Serenel`-`Seramor` Water axis rather than detached from lunar rhythm.
- Secondary moons create layered long cycles but do not alter the 13-by-28 structure.

## Canon Boundaries

- Elemental tides modify conditions within time, not the fixed structure of timekeeping.
- The calendar is exact and non-drifting inside Aeron's cosmic order.
