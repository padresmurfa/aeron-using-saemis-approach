# Constellations

Constellations in Aeron are [Tavirath (constellations)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavirath.md): lawful patterns of relation and memory, not arbitrary star pictures imposed on a neutral sky.

## Canonical Forms

- In [Tavirelon (Photon Era)](../cosmology/photon_era.md), `Taviron` (Entangled Constellations) make constellation-like relation possible before later fixed star-heavens fully mature.
- In [Kethavelen (Stelliferous Era)](../cosmology/stelliferous_era.md), `Phaemorath` (Memory Constellations) preserve myth-bearing continuity across arranged heavens.

## Astrological Significance

- `Tavirath` can be read as relations among star-words, including affinities, compounds, and long-range family resemblance inside the primal language.
- Constellations carry pattern, relation, and memory; they are suited to long-cycle interpretation rather than short-term disruption.
- They matter most when read together with fixed stars, moon harmonics, and rare shooting-star epochs.

## Canon Boundaries

- Named planet-era constellations, zodiacal houses, and their divisions are not yet canonized.
- This folder records constellary doctrine, not a completed sky-map catalog.
