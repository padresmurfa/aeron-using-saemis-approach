# Moons of Aeron

Aeron has seven [Seravel (moons)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/seravel.md): [Serenel](../../primal_language/structural_principles/serenel.md) (Silver Moon), [Seramor](../../primal_language/structural_principles/seramor.md) (Blood Moon), [Tavrekal](../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavrekal.md) (Ash Moon), [Taviril](../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviril.md) (Blue Moon), [Kethorel](../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethorel.md) (Iron Moon), [Oraemor](../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraemor.md) (Pale Moon), and [Serathil](../../primal_language/structural_principles/serathil.md) (Veiled Moon).

These `Seravel` are read in relation to [Aeron (The World)](../cosmology/aeron.md), the stable terrestrial reference frame of current celestial canon.

## Tier 2 Status

The seven `Seravel` occupy **tier 2** of Aeron's [mythic hierarchy](../cosmology/mythic_hierarchy.md): the raw powers most directly derived from the Creator, second only to the Creator itself. Each `Seravel` is a direct Creator-creation. Each carries a distinct **mana-flavor** — its own way of expressing the Creator's lifeblood — that colors its tidal, magical, ritual, and cultural pressure on Aeron. The mana-flavor is the moon's own signature, not a separate spirit, deity, or aspect-being.

## Canonical Architecture

- `Serenel` and `Seramor` remain the primary Water axis and the civilizational backbone of tides, calendar structure, and ordinary coastal life.
- `Solaryth` and `Nyxorys` remain [Taviran (luminaries)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviran.md), not moons.
- The five secondary `Seravel` are no longer read as a balanced elemental matrix. They are raw Creator-derived powers, each carrying a distinct mana-flavor (see "Mana-Flavors" below) and a corresponding visual, magical, and cultural identity.
- `Serenel` and `Seramor` remain in permanent diametric opposition on a shared primary plane and complete a 28-day cycle.
- The primary `Seravel` reflect both sunlight and darklight.
- Orbital planes remain intentionally non-identical, preserving varied eclipse likelihoods and visible sky texture.

## Primary Water Axis

- `Serenel` (Silver Moon): the open and visibly governing half of the Water axis; bright, broadly trusted, and central to harbor, ritual, and civic time.
- `Seramor` (Blood Moon): the red and depth-bearing counterpart; tide-memory, wound-memory, and the harsher half of the same lawful water cycle.

## Mana-Flavors

Each `Seravel` carries a distinct mana-flavor: a recognizable signature in how its Creator-derived power expresses itself, and a recognizable register in how mortals interact with it. The flavor distills the moon's saga rather than replacing it; saga prose remains the primary evocation.

- **`Serenel` (Silver Moon) — lucid composure.** The moon's mana arrives as visible law and steadying rhythm; harbors, magistracies, calendars, and pilgrimages settle around it. Mortals interact with `Serenel` through honesty, public ritual, and an order that does not need concealment to endure.
- **`Seramor` (Blood Moon) — grave depth.** Power expresses as undertow, wound-memory, and the quiet weight of prices already paid. Mortals interact with `Seramor` through reckoning, mourning, surgical work, and oaths that cannot be undone by speech alone.
- **`Tavrekal` (Ash Moon) — tempering pressure.** A forge-edged mana that draws making and unmaking onto the same anvil. Mortals interact with `Tavrekal` through endurance under heat — smithing, kiln-work, siegecraft, battle-magic — where discipline divides masterpiece from frenzy.
- **`Taviril` (Blue Moon) — distance-made-intimate.** Power expresses as far-sight, courier swiftness, and the road that calls. Mortals interact with `Taviril` through travel, message, prophecy at range, and the ache of journeys that change the traveler.
- **`Kethorel` (Iron Moon) — oath-bound endurance.** A mana of binding, sealing, and weight carried in silence. Mortals interact with `Kethorel` through covenant, fortress, judgment, vault, and the long-drawn cost of relics and chains kept honestly.
- **`Oraemor` (Pale Moon) — liminal invitation.** Power expresses through threshold light: ember after flame, ruin after triumph, dream-edge between waking and remembrance. Mortals interact with `Oraemor` through shrine crossings, burial rite, and the slow drift in which roads begin to know more than maps.
- **`Serathil` (Veiled Moon) — measured concealment.** A mana of half-seen passage; never wholly given, never wholly withheld. Mortals interact with `Serathil` through masked court, hidden school, dream-crossing, and disclosure delivered in fragments precise enough to save and incomplete enough to ruin.

The flavors are not interchangeable adjectives. A `Tavrekal` rite cannot be substituted by a `Kethorel` rite, even where both speak of metal; the two flavors approach the same matter from different sides of the Creator's lifeblood.

## Accepted Revision Of Oraemor

- `Oraemor` (Pale Moon) is no longer classified as a shared Fire/Earth moon.
- `Oraemor` is now the Threshold/Dream moon: the moon of embers not fully cold, ruins not fully silent, and roads between waking, memory, and the hidden.
- This preserves some old ash-and-earth imagery at the level of symbolism while moving `Oraemor`'s canonical function into liminality rather than a blunt elemental overlap.

## Mana-Flavor Pressure And Affinities

The "pressure" of a `Seravel` on Aeron is the way its mana-flavor (above) makes itself felt across magic, rite, beast, institution, atmosphere, and tide. Pressure is mana, not a parallel subsystem.

- Gravitational tidal force follows the rule `moon mass / distance^3`.
- Apparent size is not a valid proxy for mana-flavor force.
- Water remains the ordinary physical-tide backbone because of the `Serenel`-`Seramor` axis.
- `Tavrekal` and `Kethorel` mana-flavors often resonate in forge, siege, and oath-metal patterns.
- `Taviril` and `Serathil` mana-flavors often resonate in skyward, hidden, and far-seeing patterns.
- `Oraemor` stands apart as a liminal intensifier rather than a routine reinforcement partner; its flavor does not blend cleanly with the others.
- The secondary `Seravel` exert their strongest pressure on magic, rites, beasts, institutions, and atmosphere rather than on apocalyptic ocean upheaval.

## Canon Boundaries

- Exact numeric orbital distances, masses, eccentricities, and densities are not yet canon-locked in this repo.
- A full named taxonomy of special nights and major alignment-event history remains pending.
- Baseline lunar harmonics remain non-apocalyptic; catastrophe requires uncommon convergence beyond ordinary peaks.
- These revisions enrich the setting identity of the secondary `Seravel` without changing the `Serenel`-`Seramor` backbone of Aeron's calendar and world rhythm.
- Mana-flavors are descriptions of how each moon's Creator-derived power expresses itself. They are not separate sub-entities, sub-spirits, or aspect-deities. Each `Seravel` is one being with one flavor.
- Numeric mana-magnitudes, mana-flavor cross-effects under conjunction, and the catalog of how flavors compound or interfere with one another are not canonized here.
