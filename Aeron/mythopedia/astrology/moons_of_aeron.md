# Moons of Aeron

Aeron has seven [Seravel (moons)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/seravel.md): [Serenel](../../primal_language/structural_principles/serenel.md) (Silver Moon), [Seramor](../../primal_language/structural_principles/seramor.md) (Blood Moon), [Tavrekal](../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavrekal.md) (Ash Moon), [Taviril](../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviril.md) (Blue Moon), [Kethorel](../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethorel.md) (Iron Moon), [Oraemor](../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraemor.md) (Pale Moon), and [Serathil](../../primal_language/structural_principles/serathil.md) (Veiled Moon).

These `Seravel` are read in relation to [Aeron (The World)](../cosmology/aeron.md), the stable terrestrial reference frame of current celestial canon.

## Canonical Architecture

- `Serenel` and `Seramor` remain the primary Water axis and the civilizational backbone of tides, calendar structure, and ordinary coastal life.
- `Solaryth` and `Nyxorys` remain [Taviran (luminaries)](../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviran.md), not moons.
- The five secondary `Seravel` are no longer treated only as a balanced elemental matrix. They are dramatic moons with stronger visual, magical, and cultural identities.
- `Serenel` and `Seramor` remain in permanent diametric opposition on a shared primary plane and complete a 28-day cycle.
- The primary `Seravel` reflect both sunlight and darklight.
- Orbital planes remain intentionally non-identical, preserving varied eclipse likelihoods and visible sky texture.

## Primary Water Axis

- `Serenel` (Silver Moon): the open and visibly governing half of the Water axis; bright, broadly trusted, and central to harbor, ritual, and civic time.
- `Seramor` (Blood Moon): the red and depth-bearing counterpart; tide-memory, wound-memory, and the harsher half of the same lawful water cycle.

## Secondary Dramatic Moons

| Moon | Cycle | Domain | Visual signature | Story pressure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Tavrekal` (Ash Moon) | 3 months | Fire and forge | ember-shedding, smoky halo | smith cults, siege omens, reckless courage, battle-mages |
| `Taviril` (Blue Moon) | 8 months | Air, distance, and far sight | stormglass clarity, unnaturally crisp light | navigators, prophecy, aerial orders, long-distance longing |
| `Kethorel` (Iron Moon) | 15 months | Earth, oath, and war-metal | scarred face, heavy shadow, ring-like gloom | fortress culture, judges, miners, relic activation, endurance |
| `Oraemor` (Pale Moon) | 5 months | Threshold, dream, and ruin-memory | bone-white, lantern-soft, never wholly warm | shrine roads, undertakers, prophecy, burial heat, things not fully dead or cooled |
| `Serathil` (Veiled Moon) | 9 months | Secrets, masked passage, and spirit crossing | shrouded edge-glow, half-seen face | spies, seers, hidden schools, dream crossings, forbidden revelation |

## Accepted Revision Of Oraemor

- `Oraemor` (Pale Moon) is no longer classified as a shared Fire/Earth moon.
- `Oraemor` is now the Threshold/Dream moon: the moon of embers not fully cold, ruins not fully silent, and roads between waking, memory, and the hidden.
- This preserves some old ash-and-earth imagery at the level of symbolism while moving `Oraemor`'s canonical function into liminality rather than a blunt elemental overlap.

## Pressure Fields And Affinities

- Gravitational tidal force follows the rule `moon mass / distance^3`.
- Apparent size is not a valid proxy for magical or symbolic force.
- Water remains the ordinary physical-tide backbone because of the `Serenel`-`Seramor` axis.
- `Tavrekal` and `Kethorel` often resonate in forge, siege, and oath-metal patterns.
- `Taviril` and `Serathil` often resonate in skyward, hidden, and far-seeing patterns.
- `Oraemor` stands apart as a liminal intensifier rather than a routine elemental reinforcement partner.
- The secondary `Seravel` exert their strongest pressure on magic, rites, beasts, institutions, and atmosphere rather than on apocalyptic ocean upheaval.

## Canon Boundaries

- Exact numeric orbital distances, masses, eccentricities, and densities are not yet canon-locked in this repo.
- A full named taxonomy of special nights and major alignment-event history remains pending.
- Baseline lunar harmonics remain non-apocalyptic; catastrophe requires uncommon convergence beyond ordinary peaks.
- These revisions enrich the setting identity of the secondary `Seravel` without changing the `Serenel`-`Seramor` backbone of Aeron's calendar and world rhythm.
