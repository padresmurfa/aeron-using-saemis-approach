# Timeline: Tavirelon (Photon Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Seraelon` once radiance saturates the whole field and awareness becomes environmental rather than merely local.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **Radiance comes to dominate created relation, and luminous saturation fills all domains.** [Tavirelon (Photon Era)](../../../cosmology/photon_era.md), [The Photon Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_photon_era.md)
2. **Influence becomes omnidirectional, transmission becomes constant, and a shared perceptive medium appears.** [Tavirelon (Photon Era)](../../../cosmology/photon_era.md), [The Photon Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_photon_era.md)
3. **The Seventh Order of Beings arises as eight radiant forms: Taviral, Tavreth, Selkoral, Seraeth, Phaethor, Mirasal, Taviron, and Atharon.** [Taviral (Luminary Seas)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviral.md), [Tavreth (Veilfire Hosts)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavreth.md), [Selkoral (Chorus Fields)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/selkoral.md), [Seraeth (Phase Mirrors)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/seraeth.md), [Phaethor (Drift Flames)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaethor.md), [Mirasal (Echo Realms)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/mirasal.md), [Taviron (Entangled Constellations)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviron.md), [Atharon (Witness Fires)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/atharon.md)
4. **Awareness becomes field-like, and creation becomes capable of consciousness as a whole without yet producing isolated selves.** [Tavirelon (Photon Era)](../../../cosmology/photon_era.md), [Atharon (Witness Fires)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/atharon.md), [The Photon Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_photon_era.md)
5. **All prior orders gain radiant aspects as interiority, mobility, and composite identity become immersed in luminous relation.** [Tavirelon (Photon Era)](../../../cosmology/photon_era.md), [The Photon Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_photon_era.md)
6. **Rebellion remains impossible because awareness still lacks an isolated center claiming self-ownership against `Loran`.** [Tavirelon (Photon Era)](../../../cosmology/photon_era.md), [The Photon Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_photon_era.md)

## Boundary Notes

- This is the first documented era in which consciousness becomes field-like rather than merely local.
- The Seventh Order is radiant awareness, not individualized personhood.
- Distinction exists, but clarity and separate ego do not yet.
- Moral freedom remains absent.
- Rebellion remains impossible.
- All radiant orders remain phase-aligned with `Loran`.
