# Timeline: Seraelon (Lepton Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Selkoran` once mobility begins to dominate over enclosure as the principal mode of relation.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **Lighter mobile entities proliferate while dense composites remain present in the world.** [Seraelon (Lepton Era)](../../../cosmology/lepton_era.md), [The Lepton Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_lepton_era.md)
2. **Mobility becomes a governing condition, allowing influence to move across composites without binding.** [Seraelon (Lepton Era)](../../../cosmology/lepton_era.md), [The Lepton Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_lepton_era.md)
3. **The Sixth Order of Beings arises as seven mobility forms: Tavirel, Oraelin, Seraelin, Phaelin, Mirasin, Tavirin, and Atharin.** [Tavirel (Luminants)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavirel.md), [Oraelin (Whisperers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraelin.md), [Seraelin (Messengers of Phase)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/seraelin.md), [Phaelin (Drift Seekers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaelin.md), [Mirasin (Echoes)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/mirasin.md), [Tavirin (Entanglers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavirin.md), [Atharin (Witnesses)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/atharin.md)
4. **Encounter becomes possible without permanent merger, and awareness of otherness begins to flicker.** [Seraelon (Lepton Era)](../../../cosmology/lepton_era.md), [Atharin (Witnesses)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/atharin.md), [The Lepton Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_lepton_era.md)
5. **Transmission, observation, and relational memory emerge across nested composites.** [Seraelon (Lepton Era)](../../../cosmology/lepton_era.md), [Mirasin (Echoes)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/mirasin.md)
6. **All prior orders gain mobility aspects while remaining aligned with `Loran`.** [Seraelon (Lepton Era)](../../../cosmology/lepton_era.md), [The Lepton Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_lepton_era.md)

## Boundary Notes

- This is the first documented era in which encounter does not require binding.
- The Sixth Order is proto-awareness, not full consciousness.
- Self-awareness remains absent.
- Moral freedom remains absent.
- Rebellion remains impossible.
- All composites remain phase-aligned with `Loran`.
