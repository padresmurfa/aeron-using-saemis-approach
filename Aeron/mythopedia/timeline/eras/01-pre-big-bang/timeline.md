This era is pre-temporal. The ordering below is logical rather than clock-time.

## Boundary Notes

- No distinction, relation, sequence, time, space, matter, or moral polarity exists in `Aruhal`.
- `Orun` is the boundary out of pre-big-bang stillness, not an event within it.
