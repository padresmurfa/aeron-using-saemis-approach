# Timeline: Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Kethavelen` once realm-scale radiance is drawn inward and bound into stable worlds, layered interiors, atmospheres, seas, cycles, and guarded local environments.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **The first enduring worlds begin to condense from realm-prepared systems, and creation gains local places rather than only stars, provinces, and hidden planes.** [Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era)](../../../cosmology/planetary_formation_era.md), [The Planetary Formation Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_planetary_formation_era.md)
2. **The Twelfth Order of Beings arises as eight world-binding forms: Taviloran, Kethaven, Oraethalen, Miravelor, Seraelor, Vathalen, Tavrekor, and Tavralen.** [Taviloran (Gravethrones)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviloran.md), [Kethaven (Mantle Wardens)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethaven.md), [Oraethalen (Skybearers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraethalen.md), [Miravelor (Oceanic Foundations)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/miravelor.md), [Seraelor (Axis Keepers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/seraelor.md), [Vathalen (Cradle Architects)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/vathalen.md), [Tavrekor (Ember Vaults)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavrekor.md), [Tavralen (Boundary Rings)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavralen.md)
3. **Local gravity-like centers, deep interiors, skies, fluid basins, and protective rings become stable enough for worlds to keep continuity across ages.** [Taviloran (Gravethrones)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviloran.md), [Kethaven (Mantle Wardens)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethaven.md), [Oraethalen (Skybearers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraethalen.md), [Miravelor (Oceanic Foundations)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/miravelor.md), [Tavralen (Boundary Rings)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavralen.md)
4. **Axis, tilt, and recurring cycle are embedded into worlds so that day, season, and long recurrence become local rather than merely cosmic.** [Seraelor (Axis Keepers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/seraelor.md), [Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era)](../../../cosmology/planetary_formation_era.md)
5. **Cradle-like environmental balance becomes possible, though life itself has not yet begun and no world has become a soul-bearing or awakened planetary person.** [Vathalen (Cradle Architects)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/vathalen.md), [Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era)](../../../cosmology/planetary_formation_era.md)
6. **Creation enters its first age of stable local places, while organisms, souls, culture, moral history, and later inter-creature conflict remain downstream.** [Kethralen (Planetary Formation Era)](../../../cosmology/planetary_formation_era.md), [The Planetary Formation Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_planetary_formation_era.md)

## Boundary Notes

- This is the first documented era of enduring world-scale place rather than only stellar and realm-scale architecture.
- The Twelfth Order marks structural world-binding offices inside creation, not awakened planets or self-originating world-minds.
- Life, souls, culture, and moral history are still absent here.
- Continental articulation, mountain-building, soils, and terrain identity remain downstream in `Athralen`.
- No being can stand outside `Aru` or `Loran`; even the most stable world remains created participation in first principles.
- Planetary interiors, atmospheres, oceans, cycles, and shielding belong to this era, but biospheres, creaturely persons, and soul-bearing ethics remain downstream.
