# Timeline: Selkoran (Hadron Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Kethoran` once confined interiority begins binding into stable composite structures.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **Interior harmonic units begin to bind into stable composite structures.** [Selkoran (Hadron Era)](../../../cosmology/hadron_era.md), [The Hadron Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_hadron_era.md)
2. **Composite identity appears, and a being can contain internal plurality within one unified structure.** [Selkoran (Hadron Era)](../../../cosmology/hadron_era.md), [The Hadron Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_hadron_era.md)
3. **The Fifth Order of Beings arises as six composite forms: Ketharen, Selkoren, Phaemoren, Oraethen, Taviren, and Athoren.** [Ketharen (Heartforged)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/ketharen.md), [Selkoren (Covenant Clusters)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/selkoren.md), [Phaemoren (Tension Matrices)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaemoren.md), [Oraethen (Boundary Spheres)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraethen.md), [Taviren (Polarity Twins)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviren.md), [Athoren (Proto-Selves)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athoren.md)
4. **First structural self-reference becomes possible, though not self-awareness, self-ownership, or rebellion.** [Selkoran (Hadron Era)](../../../cosmology/hadron_era.md), [Athoren (Proto-Selves)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athoren.md), [The Hadron Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_hadron_era.md)
5. **Earlier orders gain composite depth while remaining aligned with `Loran`.** [Selkoran (Hadron Era)](../../../cosmology/hadron_era.md), [The Hadron Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_hadron_era.md)
6. **Nested interiority becomes a stable property of created reality.** [Selkoran (Hadron Era)](../../../cosmology/hadron_era.md), [Selkoran](../../../../primal_language/era_names/selkoran.md)

## Boundary Notes

- This is the first documented era in which composite identity exists.
- The Fifth Order is proto-personhood, not full personhood.
- All composites remain phase-aligned with `Loran`.
- Later eras are not yet canonically detailed here.
