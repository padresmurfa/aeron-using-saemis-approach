# Timeline: Kethoran (Quark Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Mirevar` once interaction collapses into interior containment and enclosed harmonic structure gains a true inside.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **Interaction collapses inward and confinement becomes a governing condition of structure.** [Kethoran (Quark Era)](../../../cosmology/quark_era.md), [The Quark Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_quark_era.md)
2. **Boundary thickens into enclosure, making inside-versus-outside distinction structurally real.** [Kethoran (Quark Era)](../../../cosmology/quark_era.md), [Kethren](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethren.md), [Orael](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/orael.md)
3. **The Fourth Order of Beings arises as seven Confinement Orders: Kethor, Mirath, Tavrek, Oraeth, Athorin, Selkor, and Phaemor.** [Kethor (Corewardens)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethor.md), [Mirath (Pressure Hosts)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/mirath.md), [Tavrek (Sparkbearers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavrek.md), [Oraeth (Veilbound)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraeth.md), [Athorin (Identity Seeds)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athorin.md), [Selkor (Cohesion Clusters)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/selkor.md), [Phaemor (Tension Archivists)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaemor.md)
4. **Retained internal state, stored pressure, and proto-memory become possible within enclosed beings.** [Kethoran (Quark Era)](../../../cosmology/quark_era.md), [Phaemor (Tension Archivists)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaemor.md), [The Quark Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_quark_era.md)
5. **The first true proto-self becomes structurally possible while ego, self-ownership, and soul-bearing ethics remain absent.** [Kethoran (Quark Era)](../../../cosmology/quark_era.md), [Athorin (Identity Seeds)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athorin.md), [The Quark Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_quark_era.md)
6. **All prior orders gain interior density without losing their earlier nature or alignment.** [Kethoran (Quark Era)](../../../cosmology/quark_era.md), [The Quark Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_quark_era.md)

## Boundary Notes

- This is the first documented era in which true created interiority exists.
- Interiority appears here without self-ownership, conscious ego, or metaphysical separation from `Loran`.
