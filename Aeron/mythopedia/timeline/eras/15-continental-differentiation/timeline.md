# Timeline: Athralen (Continental Differentiation Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Kethralen` once stable worlds begin to articulate their surfaces into plates, continents, mountains, rifts, basins, soils, and land-scale climatic regions.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **Bound worlds cease to be largely smooth shells and begin differentiating into enduring terrain-bearing surfaces.** [Athralen (Continental Differentiation Era)](../../../cosmology/continental_differentiation_era.md), [The Continental Differentiation Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_continental_differentiation_era.md)
2. **The Thirteenth Order of Beings arises as nine land-shaping forms: Kethralor, Mirathor, Athrenel, Miravelen, Phaemoralen, Tavreken, Tavraleth, Selkorel, and Seraelen.** [Kethralor (Plate Sovereigns)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethralor.md), [Mirathor (Mountain Fathers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/mirathor.md), [Athrenel (Rift Mothers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athrenel.md), [Miravelen (Basin Keepers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/miravelen.md), [Phaemoralen (Stone Archivists)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaemoralen.md), [Tavreken (Fire Veins)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavreken.md), [Tavraleth (Shield Wardens)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavraleth.md), [Selkorel (Soil Weavers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/selkorel.md), [Seraelen (Horizon Keepers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/seraelen.md)
3. **Plate motion, subduction, uplift, and rifting create enduring continents, mountain systems, and separating ocean margins.** [Kethralor (Plate Sovereigns)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethralor.md), [Mirathor (Mountain Fathers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/mirathor.md), [Athrenel (Rift Mothers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athrenel.md)
4. **Basins, strata, and volcanic corridors accumulate deep-time memory so that terrain begins to preserve geological history in visible form.** [Miravelen (Basin Keepers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/miravelen.md), [Phaemoralen (Stone Archivists)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaemoralen.md), [Tavreken (Fire Veins)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavreken.md)
5. **Surface shielding, soils, and land-scale climate rhythm make continents durable and future-life-ready without yet producing life.** [Tavraleth (Shield Wardens)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavraleth.md), [Selkorel (Soil Weavers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/selkorel.md), [Seraelen (Horizon Keepers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/seraelen.md)
6. **Creation enters its first age of terrain identity: regions become distinct, but organisms, cultures, rivers of history, and soul-bearing creaturehood remain downstream.** [Athralen (Continental Differentiation Era)](../../../cosmology/continental_differentiation_era.md), [The Continental Differentiation Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_continental_differentiation_era.md)

## Boundary Notes

- This is the first documented era of articulated land and regional terrain identity rather than only stable world-scale place.
- The Thirteenth Order marks structural land-shaping offices inside creation, not awakened continents, mountain-gods, or finished geographies of culture.
- Life, roots, rivers, forests, creaturely societies, and soul-bearing history are still absent here.
- Multi-moon tidal loading may modulate tectonic release timing, but plate-driven causality remains primary.
- No being can stand outside `Aru` or `Loran`; even the oldest continent remains created participation in first principles.
