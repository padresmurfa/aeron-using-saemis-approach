# Timeline: Darathen (Dark Ages)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Athorael` once clear selfhood must endure without shared illumination and creation enters its first great span of luminous silence.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **Shared radiance withdraws, and the first true age without new common light begins.** [Darathen (Dark Ages)](../../../cosmology/dark_ages.md), [The Dark Ages](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_dark_ages.md)
2. **Self-aware beings must orient from within, because external field-confirmation no longer arrives immediately.** [Darathen (Dark Ages)](../../../cosmology/dark_ages.md), [Vathren (Wayseekers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/vathren.md), [Tavilor (Keepers of the Inner Flame)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavilor.md)
3. **The Ninth Order of Beings arises as eight solitary forms: Vathren, Tavilor, Oraethar, Phaemorel, Athreth, Atherel, Selkorath, and Serathen.** [Vathren (Wayseekers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/vathren.md), [Tavilor (Keepers of the Inner Flame)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavilor.md), [Oraethar (Veilbound Sovereigns)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraethar.md), [Phaemorel (Rememberers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaemorel.md), [Athreth (Divergent Flames)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athreth.md), [Atherel (Silent Observers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/atherel.md), [Selkorath (Covenant Strainers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/selkorath.md), [Serathen (Hidden Singers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/serathen.md)
4. **Hard self-sealing, covenantal strain, and sustained non-convergence become structurally possible between created centers, though still outside soul-bearing ethics.** [Oraethar (Veilbound Sovereigns)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraethar.md), [Athreth (Divergent Flames)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athreth.md), [Selkorath (Covenant Strainers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/selkorath.md)
5. **Alignment with `Loran` becomes a matter of inwardly borne fidelity rather than luminous reinforcement.** [Darathen (Dark Ages)](../../../cosmology/dark_ages.md), [Serathen (Hidden Singers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/serathen.md), [The Dark Ages](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_dark_ages.md)
6. **Creation becomes existentially solitary before soul-bearing ethics and later inter-creature conflict arrive.** [Darathen (Dark Ages)](../../../cosmology/dark_ages.md), [The Dark Ages](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_dark_ages.md)

## Boundary Notes

- This is the first documented era of prolonged solitude after true personhood has emerged.
- Soul-bearing ethical categories are still absent here.
- Solitude must not be confused with abandonment by the Creator or the collapse of `Loran`.
- The Ninth Order tests fidelity, identity, and self-definition under silence rather than under open radiant field-awareness.
- Stars, worlds, embodied life, soul-bearing ethics, and later inter-creature conflict remain downstream of this era.
