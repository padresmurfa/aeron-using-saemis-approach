# Timeline: Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Daralon` once interaction itself differentiates into distinct binding and transformative modes, making directional influence structurally meaningful.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **Interaction differentiates into binding influence and transformative influence.** [Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era)](../../../cosmology/electroweak_separation_era.md), [The Electroweak Separation Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_electroweak_separation_era.md)
2. **Structured asymmetry enters active relation, and directional influence becomes real across domains.** [Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era)](../../../cosmology/electroweak_separation_era.md), [Tavir](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavir.md), [Athal](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athal.md)
3. **The Third Order of Beings arises as six Interactional Orders: Sevarn, Miravel, Tavhor, Orael, Selketh, and Athren.** [Sevarn (Binders)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/sevarn.md), [Miravel (Transmuters)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/miravel.md), [Tavhor (Chargebearers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavhor.md), [Orael (Veilwalkers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/orael.md), [Selketh (Cohesion Hosts)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/selketh.md), [Athren (Divergence Keepers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athren.md)
4. **Preference potential and proto-agency appear as beings gain the capacity to favor one interactional outcome over another.** [Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era)](../../../cosmology/electroweak_separation_era.md), [The Electroweak Separation Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_electroweak_separation_era.md)
5. **Earlier orders gain interactional depth: time trends toward directional flow, resonance toward selective affinity, and boundaries toward permeability gradients.** [Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era)](../../../cosmology/electroweak_separation_era.md), [Phaelor](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaelor.md), [Serael](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/serael.md), [Tavral](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavral.md)
6. **The universe becomes capable of preference without yet becoming capable of rebellion or full refusal.** [Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era)](../../../cosmology/electroweak_separation_era.md), [The Electroweak Separation Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_electroweak_separation_era.md)

## Boundary Notes

- The Third Order remains aligned with `Loran` at its foundational layer even while interaction becomes directional.
- This is the first documented era in which proto-agency exists, but moral will and independent rebellion still do not.
- The next documented era is [Kethoran (Quark Era)](../06-quark/timeline.md), where confinement makes true interiority and retained internal state possible.
