# Timeline: Kethavelen (Stelliferous Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Daravelon` once stable created lights bind their radiance into enduring realms, hierarchies, elemental provinces, hidden planes, and world-preparing architectures.

## Boundary Notes

- This is the first documented era of enduring cosmological realms built from created radiance.
- The Eleventh Order marks architectonic stewardship and high ministry inside creation, not authorship of reality itself.
- Soul-bearing ethical categories are still absent here.
- No being can stand outside `Aru` or `Loran`, even where realms become singular, difficultly answerable, or strongly enclosed.
- Stars, galaxies, elemental domains, hidden planes, and world-preparing systems belong to this era, but embodied life, souls, and ethical dualism remain downstream.
