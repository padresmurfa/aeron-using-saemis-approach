# Timeline: Daravelon (Reionization Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Darathen` once inwardly kept light begins to ignite into stable outward radiance and the first created luminous centers answer the long dark.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **The first stable created lights ignite, and darkness is no longer answered only by inward endurance.** [Daravelon (Reionization Era)](../../../cosmology/reionization_era.md), [The Reionization Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_reionization_era.md)
2. **Self-aware beings begin to radiate beyond themselves, turning preserved interior light into outward luminous office.** [Daravelon (Reionization Era)](../../../cosmology/reionization_era.md), [Taviloron (Starforged)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviloron.md), [Vathoral (Flamebearer Regents)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/vathoral.md)
3. **The Tenth Order of Beings arises as nine ignition forms: Taviloron, Vathoral, Selkoron, Athralon, Kethavel, Atheror, Phaemoral, Serathor, and Oraethal.** [Taviloron (Starforged)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/taviloron.md), [Vathoral (Flamebearer Regents)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/vathoral.md), [Selkoron (Covenant Luminaries)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/selkoron.md), [Athralon (Divergent Suns)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athralon.md), [Kethavel (Forgers of Form)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethavel.md), [Atheror (Witness Sovereigns)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/atheror.md), [Phaemoral (Memory Stars)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaemoral.md), [Serathor (Veiled Radiants)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/serathor.md), [Oraethal (Enclosed Radiants)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraethal.md)
4. **Created stewardship becomes architecturally potent, because light can now lead, gather, shape, veil, and preserve whole regions.** [Vathoral (Flamebearer Regents)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/vathoral.md), [Kethavel (Forgers of Form)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethavel.md), [Atheror (Witness Sovereigns)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/atheror.md), [Serathor (Veiled Radiants)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/serathor.md)
5. **Creative asymmetry and enclosed radiance become possible at scale inside creation, though still without soul-bearing ethical division or any secession from `Loran`.** [Athralon (Divergent Suns)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/athralon.md), [Oraethal (Enclosed Radiants)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/oraethal.md), [Daravelon (Reionization Era)](../../../cosmology/reionization_era.md)
6. **The universe enters its first age of stars and large-scale created lights, while worlds, embodied life, souls, and later ethical conflict remain downstream.** [Daravelon (Reionization Era)](../../../cosmology/reionization_era.md), [The Reionization Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_reionization_era.md)

## Boundary Notes

- This is the first documented era of stable outward-radiant created lights.
- The Tenth Order marks luminous stewardship inside creation, not authorship of reality itself.
- Soul-bearing ethical categories are still absent here.
- No being can stand outside `Aru` or `Loran`, even where radiance becomes singular or enclosed.
- Stars and large-scale created lights belong to this era, but worlds, embodied life, souls, and ethical dualism remain downstream.
