# Timeline: Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)

## Era Scope

This era begins once post-pulse oscillation steadies into `Loraven` and yields the first stable archetypal beings.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **Divine oscillation steadies into Loraven (Persistent Oscillation).** [Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)](../../../cosmology/grand_unification_era.md), [Loraven (Persistent Oscillation)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/loraven.md)
2. **Sethan (Standing Wave) forms stable harmonic structures within the oscillatory field.** [Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)](../../../cosmology/grand_unification_era.md), [Sethan (Standing Wave)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/sethan.md)
3. **The Tavren (Fieldwardens) emerge as the archetype of containment polarity.** [Tavren (Fieldwardens)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavren.md), [Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)](../../../cosmology/grand_unification_era.md)
4. **The Dareth (Vectors) emerge as the archetype of expansion polarity.** [Dareth (Vectors)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/dareth.md), [Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)](../../../cosmology/grand_unification_era.md)
5. **The Serath (Harmonics) emerge as the archetype of resonant interaction.** [Serath (Harmonics)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/serath.md), [Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)](../../../cosmology/grand_unification_era.md)
6. **The Kethar (Anchors) emerge as the archetype of persistence and localized stability.** [Kethar (Anchors)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethar.md), [Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)](../../../cosmology/grand_unification_era.md)
7. **The First Order of Beings is established, and oscillation now flows through structured channels and domains.** [Lorathen (Grand Unification Era)](../../../cosmology/grand_unification_era.md)

## Boundary Notes

- These beings are lawful archetypes, not yet free moral agents.
- The next documented era is [Daralon (Inflationary Era)](../04-inflationary/timeline.md), where rapid expansion produces domain separation and the Second Order of Beings.
