# Timeline: Daralon (Inflationary Era)

## Era Scope

This era follows `Lorathen` once rapid expansion outruns full-phase coherence across the whole harmonic field and creation becomes durably domain-shaped.

## Ordered Milestones

1. **Harmonic reality expands exponentially across newly stabilized structure.** [Daralon (Inflationary Era)](../../../cosmology/inflationary_era.md), [The Inflationary Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_inflationary_era.md)
2. **Scale outpaces immediate phase propagation, producing domain separation and phase lag.** [Daralon (Inflationary Era)](../../../cosmology/inflationary_era.md), [Phaes](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaes.md), [The Inflationary Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_inflationary_era.md)
3. **Domain-level identity, local coherence, and proto-individuation emerge as durable conditions within Sethan-born structure.** [Daralon (Inflationary Era)](../../../cosmology/inflationary_era.md), [Sethan (Standing Wave)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/sethan.md)
4. **The Second Order of Beings arises as eight Domain Orders: Kethren, Tavral, Serael, Phaedren, Daravel, Seralon, Sethen, and Phaelor.** [Kethren (Domain Thrones)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/kethren.md), [Tavral (Boundary Lords)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/tavral.md), [Serael (Phase Weavers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/serael.md), [Phaedren (Drift Wardens)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaedren.md), [Daravel (Impulse Bearers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/daravel.md), [Seralon (Harmonic Bridges)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/seralon.md), [Sethen (Domain Embers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/sethen.md), [Phaelor (Latency Keepers)](../../../../primal_language/structural_principles/phaelor.md)
5. **Misalignment and drift become possible without rebellion, corruption, or moral evil.** [Daralon (Inflationary Era)](../../../cosmology/inflationary_era.md), [The Inflationary Era](../../../../sagas/tales_of_creation/the_inflationary_era.md)

## Boundary Notes

- The Domain Orders remain aligned with `Loran` at their origin layer even while expression becomes localized.
- This is the first documented era in which local structure is durable enough to support future narrative beings.
- The next documented era is [Mirevar (Electroweak Separation Era)](../05-electroweak-separation/timeline.md), where interaction differentiates and preference first appears within created orders.
